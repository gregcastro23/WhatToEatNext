## Overview
We have reached the milestone of **150 fully upgraded recipes** (~50% of the total catalog). This massive expansion involved three major movements across American, French, Chinese, Greek, Indian, Italian, and Japanese cuisines.

## Key Advancements
1.  **Metabolic Granularity:** All recipes now include `sodium_mg`, `sugar_g`, and specific arrays for `vitamins` and `minerals`, enabling the MMR algorithm to match based on physiological needs.
