# FRESH HERB DRESSING

**Yield:** Six 2-tablespoon servings

## Ingredients

- 2 cups vegetable stock
- 2 tablespoons minced shallots
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons rice syrup
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, tarragon, or dill

## Instructions

1. In small sauce pan, reduce stock to 6 tablespoons and set aside to cool.
2. Combine stock with shallots, olive oil, mustard, orange juice, rice syrup and herbs in blender and puree until emulsified.
