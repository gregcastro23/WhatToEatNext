# CUCUMBER AGUA FRESCA

**Yield:** 7 cups

## Ingredients

- 6 English cucumbers with skin (12 ounces each), seeded and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 cup lime juice (approximately 6 limes)
- 2 cups mint leaves (approximately 1/2 ounce)
- 3 cups water
- 1/4 cup plus one tablespoon agave
- 2 limes, sliced for garnish

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumbers, lime juice, mint, water, and agave in blender and puree until smooth.
2. Strain puree through sieve and serve in glasses with slice of lime.
