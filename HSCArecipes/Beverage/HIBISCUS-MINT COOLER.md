# HIBISCUS-MINT COOLER

**Yield:** 16 8-ounce servings

## Ingredients

- 1 gallon water
- 3/4 cup (1 1/2 ounces) hibiscus tea leaves
- 1/2 cup agave
- 1 ounce of mint

## Instructions

1. Combine water and hibiscus tea in stockpot. Bring to boil, turn off tea, and steep for 20 minutes. Chill tea in ice bath.
2. Combine agave and mint leaves in suribachi with 2 cups of cooled tea. Muddle (crush) mint leaves with agave and tea using surikogi (pestle) until well combined.
3. Add mint mixture to remaining tea and strain.
4. Serve over ice and garnish with mint sprig.
