# POMEGRANATE, BLUEBERRY, AND GINGER ELIXIR

**Yield:** Approximately eight 1-cup servings

## Ingredients

- 4 cups pomegranate juice
- 1 pint blueberries, washed and stemmed
- 1/4 cup ginger juice (approximately 3-inch piece)
- 1 cup filtered water
- 2 tablespoons agave

## Instructions

1. Combine pomegranate juice, blueberries, ginger juice, and agave in VitaMix and puree. Do not strain. Serve over ice.
