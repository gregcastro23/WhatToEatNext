# Orange-Miso Barbecue Sauce

**Yield:** approximately 1/2 cup

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons rice syrup
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon red miso
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- Zest and juice of 1 orange
- 1 tablespoon kuzu
- 1/3 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. In small pot combine rice syrup, tomato paste, mustard, miso, vinegar, zest and juice. Cook for approximately 5 minutes.
2. In small bowl, dissolve kuzu in 2 tablespoons of water; then add enough water to make 1 cup.
3. Pour kuzu mixture into saucepan and heat slowly, stirring with whisk to prevent lumping. Add salt.
4. Simmer 5 minutes and adjust seasonings.
