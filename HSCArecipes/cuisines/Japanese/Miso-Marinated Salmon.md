# Miso-Marinated Salmon

**Yield:** 6 ounce servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds salmon filet, skinned and cut into 6 portions
- 2 cups Miso Marinade (recipe below)
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F degrees.
2. Combine salmon filets in large bowl with miso marinade. Marinate salmon for 30-40 minutes.
3. Remove filets from marinade and transfer to parchment-lined baking sheet.
4. Bake salmon for 8-10 minutes or until flesh is springy to touch.
5. Alternatively, heat large sauté pan and add sesame oil. Sear top (presentation side) of salmon filets until caramelized, flip filets, then place sauté pan in oven. Cook 8-10 minutes or until flesh is springy to touch.
