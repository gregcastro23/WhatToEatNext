# PAN-FRIED TEMPEH

**Yield:** 16 (1 ounce) portions

## Ingredients

- 1 pound tempeh, cut into 16 (1-ounce) slices
- 1/2 teaspoon ground fennel
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 cup apple juice
- 3 tablespoons tamari
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 recipe Chermoula Sauce (recipe following)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350° F.
2. In a small baking vessel, combine tempeh with fennel, cumin, apple juice, and tamari.
3. Place in oven and bake 15 – 20 minutes.
4. Remove tempeh from oven and cool to room temperature.
5. Prepare a half sheet pan with paper towels and set aside.
6. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil. Pan-fry tempeh slices until golden on each side. Transfer to paper-lined half sheet pan to drain.
7. Serve with Chermoula Sauce.
