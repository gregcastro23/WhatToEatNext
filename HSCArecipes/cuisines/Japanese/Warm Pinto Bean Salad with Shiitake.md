# Warm Pinto Bean Salad with Shiitake

**Yield:** 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups pinto beans, soaked overnight
- 2-inch piece rinsed kombu
- 15 fresh shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced thinly
- 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 small red onion, minced
- 1 bunch radishes, small dice
- 1 bunch chives, sliced thinly
- 1 bunch watercress
- 2 1/2 tablespoons shoyu
- 2 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons rice syrup
- dash apple cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoon brown rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Bring beans, kombu, and water to cover beans (plus 3 inches above) to full pressure in pressure cooker. Lower flame, cook 15 minutes.
2. In 10-inch sauté pan, lightly sauté fresh shiitake mushrooms in oil. Set aside.
3. Combine shiitakes, onion and radishes in medium mixing bowl.
4. In small bowl, combine shoyu, mustard, rice syrup, and vinegars. Slowly whisk in olive oil until emulsified. Pour dressing over vegetables.
5. Drain beans, add to vegetables, and toss until well mixed.
6. Gently mix in chives and watercress right before serving.
