# TERIYAKI SAUCE

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons tamari
- 1 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup rice vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- 1/2 cup coconut sugar
- 1/4 cup rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon fresh ground ginger juice
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic (1 large clove)

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, simmer all ingredients until desired consistency (nappé) is achieved.
