# SPINACH-SHIITAKE SALAD

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup + 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 1/4 pounds shiitake mushrooms, sliced thinly (6 cups packed)
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 pound stemmed spinach
- 6 tablespoons toasted pine nuts
- 6 tablespoon finely minced red onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons Sherry Vinaigrette (recipe below)
- 1 recipe 'Cheese Medallions' (recipe follows)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil over medium flame in 12-inch sauté pan. Add mushrooms and salt, and sauté until mushrooms are crispy and golden brown, stirring often to prevent sticking. There should be approximately 1 1/2 cups of cooked mushrooms.
2. Wash and dry spinach thoroughly. Transfer to large bowl and toss with mushrooms, pine nuts and onion.
3. Mix salad with dressing and top with 'Cheese Medallions.' Serve immediately.
