# "CHEESE" MEDALLIONS (FOR SPINACH-SHIITAKE SALAD)

**Yield:** 16 one-ounce medallions

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cashews, soaked overnight and drained
- 2 lemons, juiced
- 2 tablespoons white miso
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- 1 cup boiling water
- 4 1/2 teaspoons pectin powder
- 1/4 cup calcium water
- 2 cups crushed pistachios

## Instructions

1. Combine cashews, lemon juice, miso, salt, coconut oil, and boiling water in food processor. Process for 1 minute, or until smooth, scraping down sides occasionally. Add pectin powder and pulse until combined. Stream in calcium water as engine is running.
2. Transfer mixture to bowl and refrigerate for 10 to 20 minutes, or until slightly firm. Scoop into medallions using retractable ice cream scoop. Roll sides in crushed pistachios.
