# TOFU TERIYAKI

**Yield:** Four 3-ounce servings

## Ingredients

- 1 package firm tofu (about 14 ounces)
- 1/2 cup coconut oil
- Teriyaki Sauce (see recipe below)

## Instructions

1. Slice tofu in half horizontally. Place tofu on plate between paper towels and weigh down for 20-30 minutes to extract water. Slice pressed tofu into 1/2 inch slabs.
2. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil over medium to medium-high heat and sear tofu slices until golden on both sides.
3. Drain any excess oil. Add teriyaki sauce to tofu and reduce over high heat until sauce is syrupy (about 15 minutes).
