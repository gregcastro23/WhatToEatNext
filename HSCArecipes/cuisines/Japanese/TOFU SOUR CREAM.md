# TOFU SOUR CREAM

**Yield:** 2 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 14 ounces (1 block) soft tofu, blanched for 1-2 minutes
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1/8 cup water
- 1/3 bunch chives or dill

## Instructions

1. Puree tofu, lemon juice, salt, olive oil, and water in food processor until smooth and creamy.
2. Add dill or chives and process for 30 seconds more. Chill for 20 minutes.

*Cuisine: Japanese | Categories: Condiment, Vegan, Dairy-Free*
