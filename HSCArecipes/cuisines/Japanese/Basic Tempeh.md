# Basic Tempeh

**Yield:** 8 ounces

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons shoyu
- 4 slices of ginger
- 1 strip kombu, about 1 inch
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 8 ounce package tempeh, cut in two equal halves
- water

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine shoyu, ginger, kombu, sea salt, and tempeh. Pour in enough water to cover tempeh (about 3 cups). Cover pan and bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer about 20-25 minutes.
2. Drain tempeh and set aside. Reserve cooking liquid for use in Tempeh Scaloppini.
