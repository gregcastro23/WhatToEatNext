# PESTO (CILANTRO-PUMPKIN SEED)

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 5 ounces cilantro, stemmed
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
- 1 clove garlic
- 2 teaspoons umeboshi paste
- 2 teaspoons chickpea miso
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Prepare an ice bath. In 2 1/2 quart pot, bring 2 cups of water to boil. Add cilantro and pinch of salt. Blanch cilantro 2-3 seconds. Shock cilantro in ice bath, drain, and squeeze out excess moisture. Set aside.
2. Combine and puree pumpkin seeds, garlic, cilantro, umeboshi paste, and miso in food processor.
3. Through feed tube, add oil slowly and puree until well combined (20-30 seconds).
