# Chocolate Pudding

**Yield:** 6 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 quart Rich Almond Milk (recipe preceding)
- 5 tablespoons agar flakes
- 9 tablespoons cocoa powder
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 1/4 cups maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 1/4 cup bittersweet chocolate chips
- 3 tablespoons kuzu, dissolved in 1/2 cup water

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, combine almond milk and agar flakes. Let mixture sit 10 minutes. Bring mixture to boil, lower heat, and simmer uncovered or partially covered until agar is fully dissolved, about 10 minutes. Stir often, scraping sides and bottom of pot with spoon to dissolve agar.
2. Whisk in cocoa powder, salt, maple syrup, vanilla, and chocolate chips. Bring mixture to boil.
3. Whisk kuzu slurry into saucepan with cocoa mixture, stirring constantly until liquid returns to boil. Continue to cook 1 minute longer until mixture clears (loses chalky appearance) and thickens. Remove from heat.
4. Pour cocoa mixture into hotel pan and cool in freezer or refrigerator until firm.
5. Transfer cocoa mixture to food processor and puree until very smooth and creamy.
