# BASIL-WALNUT PESTO

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup walnuts for pesto plus 1/2 cup for garnish
- 2 cups basil leaves, firmly packed
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon mellow barley miso
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Toast walnuts on sheet tray for 10 minutes. Remove from oven and transfer to strainer. Rub walnuts against strainer over bowl to loosen some of skins. Whatever readily comes off is enough.
2. Transfer walnuts from strainer and add 3/4 cup to food processor. Add remaining ingredients.
3. Process everything until smooth. Chop by hand remaining 1/2 cup of walnuts into pieces. Reserve for garnish.
