# Mushroom Consommé

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 ounces dried shiitake mushrooms
- 1 ounce dried porcini mushrooms
- 2 pieces kombu
- 5 quarts water
- 1/2 pound onion (1/2 large), peeled and chopped
- 1/2 pound carrot (1 large), peeled and chopped
- 1 pound fennel bulb, chopped
- 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 1/2 pounds tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- 8 cloves garlic, sliced
- 2 pounds button mushrooms, sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/2 ounce fresh parsley stems
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 full sprigs thyme
- 5 black peppercorns
- 2 sprigs parsley, finely chopped
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 ounces onion, finely chopped (approximately 1/4 cup)
- 1 ounce carrot, finely chopped (approximately 2 tablespoons)
- 1 ounce celery, finely chopped (approximately 2 tablespoons)
- 1 plum tomato, seeded and chopped
- 1/4 cup madeira wine
- 4 egg whites
- 1 ounce chives, finely sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine shiitakes, porcinis, and kombu with water in 2-gallon pot. Simmer stock 20 minutes, strain and reserve stock, discarding mushrooms and kombu.
2. Preheat oven to 350°F. Toss onion, carrot and fennel with oil. Roast vegetables until well caramelized, approximately 20 to 30 minutes. Add tomatoes and garlic for last 10 minutes.
3. In 12-inch sauté pan, sauté button mushrooms in oil until well caramelized, stirring often. Deglaze pan over high heat with some reserved stock.
4. Transfer sautéed mushrooms, their deglazing liquid, and roasted vegetables to 1-gallon pot. Add bouquet garni and remaining stock. Simmer 45 minutes, skimming surface. Strain and reserve broth, discarding bouquet garni and vegetables.
5. In 1-gallon pot, reduce broth to about 8 cups. Chill broth in ice bath to room temperature.
6. Add clarification to pot, mixing well. Heat broth to simmer, stirring occasionally. When simmer is reached, cease stirring. Clarification will rise to top, forming “raft.” Simmer uncovered another 20 minutes. Strain resulting “consommé” (clarified broth) through chinois lined with many layers of cheesecloth.
7. Degrease surface of consommé with paper towels, if necessary. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle soup in bowls and garnish with chives.
