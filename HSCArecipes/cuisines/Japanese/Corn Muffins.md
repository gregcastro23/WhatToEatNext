# Corn Muffins

**Yield:** about 38 mini muffins

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 cup chopped onion, 1/4-inch dice
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1/2 cup chopped red pepper, 1/4-inch dice
- 1 tablespoon + 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped jalapeno pepper, minced
- 1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 1/2 cups yellow cornmeal
- 1 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 3/4 cup oat milk
- 1/3 cup kuzu
- 1 1/4 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F. Line mini muffin pans with muffin papers and set aside.
2. In 8-inch sauté pan, heat oil. Add onions and garlic. Cook, on medium-low heat, to caramelize (about 10-15 minutes). Remove from heat and stir in red pepper, jalapeno pepper, and cilantro. Set aside.
3. In medium bowl, sift cornmeal, flour, maple crystals, baking powder, and baking soda. Whisk to combine.
4. In separate medium bowl, whisk together oat milk, kuzu, salt, vinegar, and olive oil.
5. Pour wet mixture into flour mixture and gently stir with spatula just to combine then add onion/pepper mixture. Mix just enough to incorporate ingredients.
6. With 1-ounce scoop, pour batter into liner-filled muffin tins to almost the top (about 3/4 full). Transfer muffin tins to half sheet pans. Bake 5-6 minutes, rotate pans, and continue to bake (about 6-8 minutes more) until top of muffin bounces back when touched and toothpick comes out clean.
7. Remove muffins from pans immediately and cool completely before serving.
