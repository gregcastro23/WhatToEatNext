# Blueberry Muffins

**Yield:** 32 mini muffins

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 3/4 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg
- 3/4 cup oat milk
- 2 tablespoons kuzu
- 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1 1/2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 cup (heaping) fresh blueberries

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F. Line muffin pans with paper liners and set aside.
2. In medium bowl, sift flours, baking powder, baking soda, and spices together and whisk to combine.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together oat milk, kuzu, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, vinegar, and salt. Pour wet mixture into dry ingredients bowl. Whisk to combine. Gently stir in blueberries.
4. With 1-ounce scoop, pour batter into muffin papers to fill almost to top (about 3/4 full). Transfer muffin tins to half sheet pans. Bake 5-6 minutes, rotate pans, and continue to bake (about 6-8 minutes more) until top of muffins bounces back when touched and toothpick comes out clean.
5. Remove muffins from pans immediately and cool completely before serving.
