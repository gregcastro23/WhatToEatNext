# Fried Shrimp with Wasabi Garnish

*(Year-Round)*

**Yield:** 24 bite-size serving

## Ingredients

- 24 medium shrimp, peeled, de-veined
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic (1 large clove)
- 1 tablespoon chickpea OR mellow miso
- 2 tablespoons tamari
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons minced chives (¼ ounce)
- 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
- 2 lemons, cut into 24 wedges for garnish
- 1 quart coconut or canola oil for frying
- 2 tablespoons wasabi powder
- 1-2 tablespoons water
- ½ teaspoon lemon juice (½ lemon)

## Instructions

1. In food processor, combine shrimp, garlic, miso, tamari, curry powder, and salt. Process to smooth paste. Add 1 tablespoon of chives and pulse once or twice until incorporated into mixture. Transfer mixture from processor to small bowl.
2. Using 1-ounce scoop, portion shrimp balls and roll in breadcrumbs.
3. Refrigerate breaded shrimp for 20-30 minutes.
4. Heat oil in wok to 350°F. Deep fry shrimp until golden. Drain shrimp on paper towels.
5. In small bowl, mix wasabi powder with water and juice. Add enough liquid to achieve smooth, pipe-able consistency. Transfer mixture to small pastry bag with small round tip.
6. Sprinkle shrimp with salt and a squeeze of lemon juice. Skewer shrimp with toothpick and garnish with small dab of wasabi paste and remaining 1 tablespoon of chives. Garnish tray with lemon wedges.
