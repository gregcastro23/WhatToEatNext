# Indian-Style Seitan Kebabs with Mango Coulis

**Yield:** 20-26 skewer kebabs

## Ingredients

- 1 pound seitan, 1-inch wide slabs
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/3 cup dried mango, soaked in 1 cup boiling water until soft (drain and reserve soaking liquid)
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 2 tablespoons agave syrup
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1/3 teaspoon vegan Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, toasted, ground
- 1 teaspoon fennel seeds, toasted, ground
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 2/3 cup apple juice
- Grilled seitan cubes (from step 1)
- 6 ounces purple pearl onions, trimmed (50 pieces)
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 large mango, firm but ripe
- 1 oz cilantro
- 1 1/2 cup chopped fresh mango (excess from mango pit and unused mango from skewers)
- Pinch sea salt
- 1/3-1/2 cups water
- Splash hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F. Heat griddle grill. Toss seitan with oil in small bowl. Grill seitan on all sides and set aside. When cool, cut seitan into 1/2-inch pieces (approximately 50). Set aside.
2. In food processor, puree mango, tomato paste, agave, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, cumin, fennel, turmeric, and salt, adding apple juice a bit at a time. Mixture should have thick, sauce-like consistency. If more liquid is needed, use reserved mango soaking liquid.
3. Transfer sauce from food processor to half-hotel pan. Add seitan and roast in oven approximately 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until much of sauce is absorbed.
4. In 2 1/2 quart pot, bring 1 quart water to boil with 1/2 teaspoon sea salt. Prepare an ice bath. Blanch pearl onions for one minute, shock in ice water, and drain. Remove onion skins and toss onions in small bowl with vinegar. Grill pearl onions until slightly charred. Set aside.
5. Dice mango into 25-30 half-inch pieces. Reserve mango scraped from pit and other unused cubes for mango coulis.
6. Place seitan on skewer followed by red onion and mango. Repeat sequence, finishing with one more piece of seitan, mango, and pearl onion.
7. Transfer leftover mango meat to blender. Add pinch of sea salt and splash of hot sauce. Blend mango mixture with leftover dried mango soaking liquid until smooth consistency is achieved.
8. Reheat skewers on sheet tray in oven and garnish with mango coulis and cilantro.
