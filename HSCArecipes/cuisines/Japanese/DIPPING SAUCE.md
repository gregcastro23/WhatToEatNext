# DIPPING SAUCE

**Yield:** approximately 1/2 cup

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons tamari
- 3 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- 2 teaspoons ginger juice

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in small bowl.
