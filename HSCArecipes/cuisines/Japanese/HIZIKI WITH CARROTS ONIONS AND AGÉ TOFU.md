# HIZIKI WITH CARROTS, ONIONS, AND AGÉ TOFU

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 cups canola or coconut oil for frying
- 7 ounces (1/2 block) firm tofu, pressed, cut into 1/2 inch slabs, then 1/2 inch strips
- 1/3 cup loosely packed hiziki (.05 ounces), soaked 30 minutes in water to cover, drained, set aside
- 1 cup apple juice
- 3/4 cup water
- 6 ounces carrot (1 medium), matchsticks
- 10 ounces onion (1 medium), sauté slices
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
- Shoyu to taste
- 1 tablespoon white sesame seeds, lightly toasted

## Instructions

1. Heat oil to 375°F in 2 1/2 quart pot. Line plate with paper towel. Fry tofu in batches until golden. Drain on paper towel.
2. Combine hiziki in separate 2 1/2 quart pot with apple juice and water. Bring to boil. Add carrots, onion, fried tofu, toasted sesame oil, and shoyu. Simmer 30 minutes or until all liquid is absorbed.
3. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.
