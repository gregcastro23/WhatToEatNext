# WAKAME CUCUMBER SALAD WITH ORANGE

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup wakame (loosely packed), soaked 10 minutes in water to cover and drained
- 2 cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 juice oranges, supremed (plus juice extracted from squeezing membrane after supreme)
- 1/4 bunch cilantro, minced
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons mirin
- 1 tablespoon shoyu
- 1 1/2 tablespoons maple syrup

## Instructions

1. In strainer set over bowl, mix cucumber with sea salt. Press with weighted plate until liquid is expressed.
2. Chop wakame coarsely and combine with cucumbers, oranges, and cilantro in medium bowl.
3. In separate small bowl, combine dressing: rice vinegar, mirin, shoyu, and maple syrup.
4. Toss salad with dressing. Chill 20 minutes.
