# FRESH BERRY TART WITH NUT CRUST

**Yield:** One 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 cup pecans, roasted and cooled
- 3/4 cup Gluten-Free Flour Blend
- 2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- pinch sea salt
- 1 tablespoon melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons agar flakes
- 1 3/4 cups apple juice
- 2 tablespoons kuzu dissolved in 1/4 cup juice
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- sea salt
- 2 cups blueberries, washed and drained
- 2 cups raspberries, rinsed and drained

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Oil 9-inch tart pan or use 5-6 four-inch, non-stick paper tart pans.
2. In food processor, grind pecans to fine meal with flour, maple crystals, baking powder, and salt.
3. In separate small bowl, whisk together oil and maple syrup.
4. Add oil and syrup to dry ingredients in processor. Pulse lightly until dough comes together.
5. Press crust mixture into tart pan(s). Refrigerate 15-20 minutes, then bake 20-25 minutes. Let cool completely.
6. In small pot, simmer agar flakes in apple juice until agar completely dissolves. Add kuzu/juice slurry. Whisk until mixture thickens. Add maple syrup, pinch of salt, and berries.
7. Cook 6-8 minutes. Remove from heat and let mixture cool partially. Pour filling into cooled crust.
8. Let set completely. Garnish with mint before serving. There may be filling left over. Puree in food processor to make pudding.
