# TEMPEH SAUSAGE

**Yield:** Twelve 1-ounce patties

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces tempeh
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons tamari
- 1/4 teaspoon sage
- 1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
- 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/4 teaspoon paprika
- pinch cayenne
- 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds, lightly toasted
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons oat flour
- cornmeal or flour for dredging
- 3-4 tablespoons oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Cut tempeh into 2-inch pieces and steam for 20 minutes in steamer basket over simmering water. Cool to room temperature.
2. Grate tempeh on large holes on box grater. Combine grated tempeh with garlic, water, olive oil, tamari, sage, marjoram, thyme, paprika, cayenne, fennel seeds, pepper, and oat flour. Mix well.
3. Form mixture into walnut-sized balls using 1-ounce ice cream scoop and press into patties. Dredge in cornmeal or flour.
4. Prepare plate with paper towel for draining. In sauté pan, heat oil and fry patties until well browned on both sides. Drain tempeh.
