# Almond-Fruit Tart with Almond Crust

**Yield:** 6 four-inch tarts

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup blanched almonds
- 1/3 cup rolled oats
- 3/4 cup oat flour
- 1/4 cup + 1 Tbsp maple syrup
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 Tbsp melted coconut oil
- 6 Tbsp agar flakes, ground
- 1 1/2 cups water
- 3/4 cup maple syrup
- 2 cups Rich Almond Milk (see recipe)
- Large pinch sea salt
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp almond extract
- 2 Tbsp kuzu dissolved in 2 Tbsp water
- 2 oranges, supreme
- 1 pint raspberries
- 6 strawberries
- 2 kiwi, sliced
- 1 package freeze-dried raspberries
- 1 container edible flowers
- 3/4 cup pistachios, toasted and chopped
- Raspberry Sauce (see recipe)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection). Gather 6 four-inch, non-stick paper tart pans.
2. In food processor, grind almonds, oats, and oat flour together until pulverized to fine meal.
3. In small bowl, combine maple syrup, salt, and oil. Whisk until emulsified.
4. Pour maple-oil mixture into food processor, pulsing to form cohesive dough.
5. Using fingers, press dough evenly into miniature tart pans. Bake 15-20 minutes or until edges of crust are browned. Set aside to cool.
6. Combine agar, water, and maple syrup in 2 1/2 quart saucepan. Soak agar 10 minutes.
7. Bring agar mixture to boil, lower heat and simmer until agar is dissolved, about 15 minutes. Whisk or stir often, scraping agar from sides and bottom of saucepan.
8. Stir almond milk, salt, and vanilla and almond extracts into agar mixture. Bring mixture to simmer, whisking often.
9. Add kuzu slurry, whisking vigorously, until mixture comes to boil. Cook few minutes longer.
10. Pour mixture into shallow pan and chill in freezer or refrigerator until set.
11. In food processor, whip chilled mixture until very smooth and creamy. Halfway through processing, stop machine and scrape sides of work bowl with spatula.
12. Fill tart crusts with Almond Pastry Cream. Arrange fruit on top of cream. Serve with pistachios, flowers, freeze-dried raspberries and raspberry sauce (recipe follows).
