# PINE NUT RICOTTA ON TREVISO LEAVES

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup pine nuts, soaked overnight in 2 cups water, and drained
- 1 head garlic, roasted, cooled, and paste extracted
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon chickpea OR mellow miso
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons umeboshi paste
- Pinch of sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives (1/2 ounce)
- 2 tablespoons red pepper, brunoise
- 2 tablespoons yellow pepper, brunoise
- 3 tablespoons toasted pine nuts
- 2 heads Treviso (red endive)

## Instructions

1. In food processor, puree pine nuts, garlic paste, oil, miso, lemon juice, and umeboshi paste. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer mixture to medium bowl or pastry bag with round tip.
2. Dollop or pipe 1 heaping tablespoon of nut filling in each Treviso leaf and garnish with chives, yellow and red peppers, and pine nuts.
