# Grilled Seitan

**Yield:** 6 (2 ounce) servings

## Ingredients

- 12-ounce package seitan, cut into six 2-ounce slabs (reserve liquid from package)
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 recipe Chimichurri Sauce (recipe following)

## Instructions

1. Combine seitan in medium bowl with liquid from package, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, olive oil, and salt. Marinate for 30 minutes.
2. Heat cast iron griddle. Grill seitan on each side until grill marks form. Return seitan to bowl with marinade until ready to serve. Serve with Chimichurri Sauce.
