# Seitan Kebabs with Yellow Rice

**Yield:** 20-25 Kebabs

## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh seitan, cut in 2-inch chunks
- 6 ounces red onion, large dice
- 1 pound crimini mushrooms, cleaned, stemmed, and halved
- 2 pints cherry tomatoes, rinsed
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons minced garlic (3 large cloves)
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (2 ounces)
- 1/2 cup brown rice syrup
- 1/4 cup shoyu
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 2 tablespoons kuzu dissolved in 2 tablespoons water for thickening marinade
- 2 cups long grain brown rice, rinsed and drained
- 3 1/2 cups boiling water
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (2 ounces)

## Instructions

1. Soak 25 skewers in cold water for 30 minutes.
2. In large bowl, combine seitan, onions, mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes with marinade ingredients for 30 minutes. Strain seitan and vegetables from marinade, but reserve marinade to make sauce (step 5).
3. Heat griddle grill. Make skewers with 2 pieces seitan, 2 pieces mushrooms, 2 pieces tomato, and red onion, alternating seitan and vegetables.
4. Grill kebabs 3-5 minutes on each side or until grill marks appear. Set aside.
5. Bring marinade to simmer. Whisk in kuzu slurry, stirring constantly, until marinade thickens to sauce consistency (approximately 5 minutes).
6. Serve atop or alongside Yellow Rice. Pour marinade sauce over kebabs or serve alongside.
7. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine rice, water, salt, and turmeric. Bring rice to boil, cover pot, reduce heat, and simmer for approximately 40-50 minutes or until all water is absorbed.
8. Remove rice from heat, and set rice aside covered to steam 10 minutes more.
9. Fluff rice lightly with fork. Add parsley and mix to combine. Serve alongside or underneath Seitan Kebabs.
