# CARAMEL

**Yield:** 3/4 cup

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup brown rice syrup
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in sauté pan. Bring sauce to simmer and cook uncovered for 10-12 minutes until sauce is reduced and thick enough to coat back of spoon. Remove from heat and let cool completely.
