# Barbecue Tofu

**Yield:** 8-10 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds firm tofu
- 3-4 tablespoons canola or coconut oil (for grilling)
- Marinade: 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons Tabasco sauce
- 1 tablespoon barley malt syrup
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon stone-ground mustard
- 2 cups pureed tomatoes
- 1/3 cup apple juice
- 1 tablespoon umeboshi vinegar
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 chipotle chile

## Instructions

1. Cut tofu in half horizontally (about 3/4” thick). Press fifteen minutes between paper towels.
2. Slice tofu into finger-sized pieces (about 1 inch wide).
3. Heat grill, brush with canola or coconut oil. Place tofu on griddle. Grill on each side about 2-3 minutes or until grill marks appear.
4. In large sauté pan, combine all marinade ingredients. Transfer tofu to marinade and simmer gently 25-30 minutes or place sauté pan in 350° oven for 20 minutes.
5. Remove tofu from pan. Reduce sauce to desired consistency.
