# POACHED PEAR TART WITH CORNMEAL CRUST

**Yield:** 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 prepared Cornmeal Crust (recipe below)
- 1 quart white grape juice
- pinch sea salt
- 4 pears, peeled, halved, and cored
- 1 tablespoon agar flakes
- 1 tablespoon kuzu dissolved in 1/2 cup water
- 3/4 cup seedless raspberry jam
- 1 pint fresh raspberries
- 1/4 cup maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Poach pears in grape juice until fork tender. Transfer pears to plate and chill; reserve poaching liquid.
2. In 2 1/2 quart pot, simmer 1 1/2 cups poaching liquid with agar flakes until agar is dissolved. Stir in kuzu slurry and cook, stirring gently, until mixture turns clear. Remove from heat, cover with lid, and set aside.
3. Spread layer of raspberry jam in bottom of prepared crust. Arrange sliced or fanned pears on top of jam. Top with glaze. Chill until set (about 20 minutes).
4. In blender, puree raspberries and maple syrup. Strain out seeds and pour into squeeze bottles. Serve with tart.
