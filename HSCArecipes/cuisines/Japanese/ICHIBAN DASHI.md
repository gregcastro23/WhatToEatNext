# ICHIBAN DASHI

*Traditional Japanese Broth*

**Yield:** approximately 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 5-inch piece kombu
- 4 cups water
- 1/3 cup Bonito flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. Wipe kombu with towel, if it has salty residue. Place it in pot and cover with cold water. Let stand 30 minutes.
2. Bring to boil, add bonito flakes and remove from heat.
3. Wait till bonito flakes sink to bottom of pot and strain through cheesecloth.
