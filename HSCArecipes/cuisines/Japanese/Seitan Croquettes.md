# Seitan Croquettes

**Yield:** 16 croquettes (1/4 cup-sized patties)

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 6 ounces onion (1 medium), fine dice
- 2 pounds seitan
- 2 tablespoons flax, ground, and mixed with 3 tablespoons water
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 teaspoon fresh sage (1/4 ounce)
- 2 teaspoons fennel seeds, ground
- 1/2 cup toasted and ground sunflower seeds
- 1/2 cup whole wheat flour (for dredging)
- 4 ounces red onions, salad slice
- 2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon agave syrup
- Pinch sea salt
- 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives (1/2 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium-high flame. Sauté onion until caramelized. Transfer to medium bowl and set aside.
2. Squeeze all excess liquid from seitan. In food processor, pulse seitan to texture of ground beef. Transfer seitan to bowl with onions. Mix in flax mixture, salt, sage, fennel seeds, and sunflower seeds to combine.
3. Form seitan mixture into small patties using 1/4 cup measure. Dredge in flour. Heat remaining 3 tablespoons oil in same 10-inch sauté pan used to caramelize onions. Pan-sear croquettes for several minutes on both sides.
4. Combine red onion in small bowl with vinegar, agave, and salt. Mix to combine.
5. Garnish croquettes with mustard, red onion and chives.
