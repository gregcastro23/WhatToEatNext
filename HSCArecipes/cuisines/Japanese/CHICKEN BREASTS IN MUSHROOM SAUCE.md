# CHICKEN BREASTS IN MUSHROOM SAUCE

**Yield:** 4 Servings

## Ingredients

- 4 boneless, skinless, chicken breast halves, pounded to 1/2 inch thickness
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 8 ounces portobello mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup minced shallots
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1/2 cup white wine
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 3 tablespoons cold, sweet butter, cut into small pieces
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. In shallow bowl, dredge chicken in flour. Shake off excess flour.
2. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat 3 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat.
3. Cook chicken in oil until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
4. Remove chicken from pan, season immediately with salt and pepper, cover to keep warm, and let rest for 10 minutes.
5. Add remaining oil to pan and heat over medium-high flame. Add mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes. Add shallots and garlic and continue cooking until mushrooms turn golden brown (2 to 3 minutes), stirring often to prevent garlic and shallots from burning.
6. Pour wine into pan and cook over high heat until almost evaporated, scraping browned bits from bottom of pan. Add stock and cook until reduced by half.
7. Remove pan from heat and whisk in butter. Season to taste with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
8. Slice chicken thinly on bias and arrange by fanning slices.
