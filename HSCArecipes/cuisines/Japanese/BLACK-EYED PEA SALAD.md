# BLACK-EYED PEA SALAD

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup black-eyed peas, soaked overnight, drained and rinsed
- 3 cups water
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 piece kombu
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
- 1/3 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 ribs celery, small dice
- 2 plum tomatoes, concassé
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, chopped
- 2 tablespoons thinly sliced scallions (1/3 bunch)
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley (1/3 ounce)
- Sea salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine beans, water, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and kombu in 1-gallon pot and bring to boil over high heat.
2. Lower heat and simmer uncovered for 15 minutes or until beans are tender. Remove kombu and drain beans in colander.
3. Whisk together remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt, pepper, vinegar, red pepper flakes, and olive oil. Pour over warm beans.
4. When beans have cooled, toss together with celery, tomatoes, olives, scallions, and parsley.
5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
