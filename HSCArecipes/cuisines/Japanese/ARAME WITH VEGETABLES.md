# ARAME WITH VEGETABLES

**Yield:** 12 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 10 ounces onion (1 large), sauté slice
- 6 ounces carrot (1 medium), small dice
- 1 1/2 cups arame, loosely packed (1.5 ounces), soaked in water to cover for 10 minutes, drained
- 2 tablespoons shoyu
- 2 tablespoons brown rice syrup
- 2 tablespoons mirin
- 8 ounces bok choy stems and leaves, chiffonade

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil over medium flame. Sweat onion for 5 minutes or until translucent. Add carrot and sweat another 5 minutes.
2. Add arame to onions and carrots. Stir well. Sweat for another minute until arame is heated through.
3. Add water to cover arame halfway. Bring water to boil. Add shoyu, brown rice syrup, and mirin. Simmer for 20-30 minutes or until all liquid has evaporated.
4. Add bok choy stems and sweat until stems just become tender. Add leaves and sweat until leaves wilt.
