# SEA PALM, WAKAME AND DULSE SEAWEED SALAD WITH CREAMY CARROT DRESSING

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds carrots (2 medium), 1-inch roll cut
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 5 cloves garlic, smashed, peeled
- 3 sprigs rosemary (1 ounce)
- 1 cup loosely packed sea palm (1 ounce), soaked in cold water for 10 minutes, drained, chopped, set aside
- 1/4 cup shoyu
- 1/4 cup loosely packed wakame (.03 ounces), soaked in cold water to cover for 10 minutes, drained, chopped, set aside
- 1/2 cup loosely packed dulse (.04 ounces), soaked in cold water to cover for 1 minute, drained, chopped, set aside
- 1 pound cauliflower (1/2 head), florets, blanched, shocked
- 1 head romaine lettuce, washed, spun, chopped
- 2 tablespoons black sesame seeds, toasted
- 1/4 pound roasted carrots, reserved from salad
- 1/4 cup brown rice vinegar
- 3 tablespoons ginger juice (3 ounce piece of ginger)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/3 cup + 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 1/2-2 cups water

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 400°F. Toss carrots with olive oil, garlic, and rosemary. Transfer carrots to parchment-lined half sheet tray. Roast carrots until tender (about 20-30 minutes). Set aside to cool, reserving 1/4 pound carrots for dressing.
2. In 2 1/2 quart pot, bring sea palm, with enough water to cover, and shoyu to boil. Lower flame and simmer 20 minutes uncovered. Drain sea palm and set aside.
3. Transfer reserved 1/4 pound roasted carrots to Vitamix. Add rice vinegar, ginger juice, salt, olive oil, and water. Blend until very smooth, adding more water as necessary.
4. Arrange lettuce on plates. Arrange cauliflower, remaining roasted carrots, sea palm, wakame, and dulse on top of lettuce. Drizzle carrot dressing over salad. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds.
