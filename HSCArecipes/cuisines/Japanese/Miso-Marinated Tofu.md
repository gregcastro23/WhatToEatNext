# Miso-Marinated Tofu

**Yield:** Six 2-ounce portions

## Ingredients

- 14 ounces tofu (1 block), sliced into 6 slices (lengthwise)
- 2 cups Miso Marinade (see recipe above)

## Instructions

1. Turn salamander on high heat.
2. Pour marinade in 12-inch sauté pan. Place tofu slices in pan.
3. Bring marinade to boil then reduce to low simmer.
4. Simmer tofu for 15 minutes on one side, then gently flip tofu and simmer for another 15 minutes on other side. As tofu simmers, marinade should reduce considerably and become thick.
5. Remove tofu from marinade. Spread any remaining marinade on top of tofu.
6. Broil tofu under salamander for approximately 5-7 minutes or until it begins to caramelize.
