# Roasted Red Pepper Coulis

**Yield:** 1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 pound red peppers (3 large)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 1 ounce onion, minced
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic (2 large cloves)
- 2-3 tablespoons vegetable stock, or more as needed
- 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- Dash hot sauce, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375°F.
2. Arrange peppers on half sheet pan, and roast in oven until blistered, about 20-30 minutes. Transfer peppers to covered, small bowl to steam for 10 minutes. When peppers are cool, remove skins and seeds.
3. In 8-inch sauté pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over a medium flame and sweat onions with garlic until onions are translucent.
4. Combine peppers with onion-garlic mixture in VitaMix and blend until creamy. Add remaining 2 tablespoons oil through feed tube, slowly while machine is running.
5. Add warm vegetable stock as needed for desired consistency. Add lemon juice to taste. Adjust seasonings with salt, pepper, and hot sauce.
