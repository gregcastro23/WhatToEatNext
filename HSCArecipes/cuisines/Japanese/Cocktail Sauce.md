# Cocktail Sauce

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup organic ketchup
- 1/3 cup chili sauce
- 2 tablespoons freshly grated horseradish
- Dash hot sauce
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 4 dashes Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, and serve with poached shrimp.
