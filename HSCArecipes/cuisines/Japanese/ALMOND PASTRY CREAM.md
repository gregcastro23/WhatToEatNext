# ALMOND PASTRY CREAM

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons agar flakes
- 1 1/2 cups water
- 3/4 cup maple syrup
- 2 cups Rich Almond Milk (recipe preceding)
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon kuzu, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, combine agar, water, and maple syrup. Let mixture sit for 10 minutes.
2. Bring agar mixture to boil. Lower heat and simmer uncovered or with pan lid slightly ajar until agar is dissolved, about 15 minutes. Stir often, scraping agar from sides and bottom of saucepan with spoon. Whisk mixture as necessary to break up bits of un-dissolved agar.
3. Stir almond milk, salt, and vanilla extract into agar mixture. Bring to boil, whisking often.
4. Lower heat and add kuzu slurry, whisking vigorously until mixture comes back to simmer. Cook a few minutes longer.
5. Pour mixture into hotel pan and chill in freezer or refrigerator until set.
6. In food processor, puree chilled mixture until very smooth and creamy. Halfway through processing, stop machine and scrape sides of work bowl with rubber spatula. If sweeter cream is desired, add additional maple syrup to taste.
7. Chill almond cream thoroughly, if thicker texture is desired.
