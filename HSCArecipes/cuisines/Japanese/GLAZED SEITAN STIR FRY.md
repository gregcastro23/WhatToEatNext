# GLAZED SEITAN STIR FRY

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- ¼ cup canola oil
- 12 ounces seitan, cut into ½-inch wide strips and patted dry
- 4 ounces onion, sauté slice
- 8 ounces carrots, matchstick
- 3 large cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon minced ginger (1 ounce)
- 4 ounces snow peas, cut in half on bias
- Pinch crushed red pepper flakes
- 6 tablespoons maple syrup
- ¼ cup orange juice (2 oranges)
- 2 tablespoons shoyu
- 1 tablespoon canola oil
- 1 tablespoon arrowroot dissolved in 2 tablespoons cold water
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- ½ cup cashews, toasted, coarsely chopped
- 2 scallions, trimmed, thin diagonal slice

## Instructions

1. In wok, heat oil over medium-high flame. Add seitan and stir-fry until caramelized.
2. Add onions and stir-fry until lightly caramelized. Add carrots and continue stir-frying until carrots are just tender. Add garlic, ginger, snow peas, and red pepper flakes. Continue to stir-fry until all vegetables are tender but crisp.
3. In small bowl, combine all glaze ingredients and whisk to combine. Pour glaze over vegetables and seitan. Mix to combine, cooking until mixture thickens and all ingredients are evenly coated.
4. Drizzle with toasted sesame oil and garnish with cashews and scallions.
