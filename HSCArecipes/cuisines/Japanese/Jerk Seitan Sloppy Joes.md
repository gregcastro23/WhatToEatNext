# Jerk Seitan Sloppy Joe's

**Yield:** 12 (1/4 cup servings)

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon olive oil, divided
- 6 ounces onion (1 medium), medium dice
- 1 pound seitan, pulsed in food processor to texture of ground beef
- 1/4 cup minced garlic (4 large cloves)
- 2 tablespoons chopped thyme (1/3 ounce)
- 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/3 teaspoon ground allspice
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/4 teaspoon ground Mexican cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 14-ounce can crushed tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon lime juice (1 lime)
- 1 ounce ginger, juiced (1 tablespoon)
- 12 corn tortillas
- 1 avocado, sliced into 12 wedges, tossed with 1 tablespoon lime juice (1 lime)
- 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (1/2 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In 12-inch sauté pan, heat oil on medium-high flame. Add onions and sauté until slightly caramelized.
2. Add seitan and sauté until caramelized, lower heat, and add garlic, thyme, paprika, salt, allspice, red pepper flakes, cinnamon, and black pepper. Sweat until onions are tender.
3. Add tomatoes, maple syrup, and mustard. Simmer 5-10 minutes or until desired 'stewy' consistency.
4. Season to taste with lime and ginger juices.
5. Heat cast iron pan or griddle on medium-low flame. Place tortillas on pan or griddle and heat until softened and flexible.
6. Dollop 1/4 cup of seitan mixture into each tortilla. Garnish with 1 slice of avocado and cilantro.
