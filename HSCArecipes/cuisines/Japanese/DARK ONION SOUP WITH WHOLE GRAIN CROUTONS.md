# DARK ONION SOUP WITH WHOLE GRAIN CROUTONS

**Yield:** 8 cups

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 3 pounds onions (3-4 large), sauté slice
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch black pepper
- 3 tablespoons sherry
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 cups Brown Stock (saved from Stock Practicum)
- 1 tablespoon tamari or to taste
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or to taste
- Whole Grain Croutons (recipe below)

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil over medium flame and sweat onions with salt until translucent. Lower heat, and continue to sweat until onions begin to soften and color, stirring often to prevent burning. Continue until onions are caramelized (brown and soft), about 30-40 minutes. Add black pepper, sherry, and garlic and continue cooking until sherry reduces and garlic softens.
2. Add thyme sprigs, bay leaf, and brown stock. Simmer 20-25 minutes.
3. Season to taste with tamari and vinegar.
