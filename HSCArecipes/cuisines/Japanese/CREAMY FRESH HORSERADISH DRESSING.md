# CREAMY FRESH HORSERADISH DRESSING

**Yield:** approximately 1 3/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons peeled, freshly grated horseradish (3/4 ounces)
- 1/2 pound silken tofu (1/2 block), blanched for 2-3 minutes (wrap in cheesecloth first)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup chickpea miso
- 1/3 cup water
- 1 garlic clove (sliced)
- 1 lemon, juiced (3 tablespoons)
- 1 tablespoon brown rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon umeboshi paste
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- Pepper to taste
- Tabasco OR hot sauce to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine horseradish, tofu, oil, miso, water, garlic, lemon, vinegar, umeboshi paste, and mustard in blender. Process until creamy and smooth.
2. Add pepper and Tabasco to taste, blending until incorporated.
