# SAUTÉED MUSHROOMS WITH HERBS

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 shallot, sliced
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 pound Shiitake mushrooms
- 1/2 pound Cremini mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups white wine
- 1/4 cup oat flour
- 2 cups vegetable stock
- Additional salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 pound Reggiano parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

## Instructions

1. In large pan, heat oil over medium flame. Add shallots and garlic, lower heat, and sauté for 1-2 minutes. Add mushrooms with salt to pan and sauté until well caramelized, stirring only occasionally, 10-15 minutes.
2. Add wine to pan, turn up heat, and simmer until wine has evaporated.
3. All flour to pan. Stir until incorporated. Add stock and simmer until sauce is thickened, about 10 minutes.
4. Season with additional salt and pepper, if needed.
5. Add cheese to taste, if using.
