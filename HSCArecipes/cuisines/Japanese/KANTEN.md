# KANTEN

**Yield:** 8-10 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons agar flakes
- 2 cups apple juice
- 1 pint strawberry (sliced thin)

## Instructions

1. Soak agar flakes in juice for 10 minutes in 2 1/2 quart saucepan.
2. Bring mixture to boil. Watch carefully to prevent liquid from boiling over. Reduce heat to simmer and cook until agar is completely dissolved, whisking frequently.
3. Place sliced strawberries in an 8-inch square baking dish.
4. Slowly pour agar and juice mixture over berries. Set aside until no steam is rising from mixture.
5. Place in refrigerator until firmed up, about 45 minutes. Can either be served cut into cubes or blended in food processor for 'pudding.'
