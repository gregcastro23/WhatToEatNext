# Seitan Bordelaise

**Yield:** 2-3 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound seitan, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- 1/4 cup whole wheat flour (for dredging)
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 1/4 cups vegetable stock
- 1/3 cup red wine
- 3 tablespoons minced shallots (1 ounce)
- 2 ounces (12) shiitake mushrooms, stemmed, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh thyme (1/4 ounce)
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Dredge seitan in flour and shake off any excess.
2. In 10-inch sauté pan over medium-high flame, heat oil and sear seitan until lightly caramelized on both sides. Remove seitan from pan and set aside. Add shallots to pan and sauté until caramelized.
3. Add seitan back to pan with stock, red wine, shiitake mushrooms, and thyme. Simmer until liquid is reduced to nappé consistency.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
