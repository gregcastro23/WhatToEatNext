# COCONUT-LIME FLAN

**Yield:** 6 half-cup servings

## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 cups coconut milk
- 2 tablespoon agar flakes
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon kuzu
- 3 tablespoons lime juice (3 limes)
- 1/2 cup water
- Canola oil for ramekins
- 1/2 cup maple crystals
- 1/2 cup toasted dried coconut

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk and agar. Soak agar for 5 minutes.
2. Bring mixture to boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for about 5 minutes or more, until agar is completely dissolved.
3. Add maple syrup and stir until combined.
4. Dissolve kuzu in lime juice and water until there are no lumps. Add to milk mixture and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
5. Lightly oil ramekins and sprinkle 2 teaspoons maple crystals in bottom of each ramekin.
6. Pour custard into ramekins. Refrigerate to set.
7. When custard is set, run paring knife around the side of each ramekin to separate custard. Invert custard onto plate. Garnish with coconut.
