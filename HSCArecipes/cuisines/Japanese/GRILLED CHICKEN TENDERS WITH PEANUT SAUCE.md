# GRILLED CHICKEN TENDERS WITH PEANUT SAUCE

**Yield:** 4 portions (8 individual skewers)

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons unrefined sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon tamari
- 1 1/2 teaspoons mirin
- Pinch red pepper flakes
- 1 pound chicken tenders
- Peanut Sauce (recipe below)

## Instructions

1. Soak 8 wooden skewers in bowl of water to prevent burning on grill.
2. In medium-size bowl, whisk together sesame oil, tamari, mirin, and red pepper flakes. Add tenders to mixture and marinate for 45 minutes.
3. Heat cast iron griddle grill over medium heat.
4. Thread tenders on skewers. Depending on size, use one or two tenders per skewer. Discard marinade.
5. Grill tenders each side about 4 minutes or until chicken has grill marks and is opaque in appearance. Serve with peanut sauce.
