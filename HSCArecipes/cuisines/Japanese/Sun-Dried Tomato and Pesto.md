# Sun-Dried Tomato and Pesto

## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch fresh basil, washed and stems removed
- 1 1/2 cups walnuts, toasted
- 10 sun-dried tomatoes, reconstituted
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon white miso
- sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in food processor until puréed. Taste and adjust seasoning.
