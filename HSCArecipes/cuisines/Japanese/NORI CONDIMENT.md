# NORI CONDIMENT

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 7 sheets Toasted Nori, torn into tiny pieces
- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons shoyu
- 2 tablespoons brown rice syrup

## Instructions

1. In 2-quart saucepan, add nori, water, shoyu, and rice syrup.
2. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, and stir often. Let simmer until all the liquid is absorbed.
3. Serve as condiment on grains or beans.

*Cuisine: Japanese | Categories: Condiment, Vegetarian*
