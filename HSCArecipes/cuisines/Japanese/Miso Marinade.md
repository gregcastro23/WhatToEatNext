# Miso Marinade

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup white miso
- 3 cloves garlic, sliced
- 3 tablespoons ginger juice (1-inch knob)
- 2 tablespoons brown rice vinegar
- Juice of 1 lemon (2 tablespoons)
- 1/4 cup rice syrup
- 1 cup water (omit if marinating fish)

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in blender until smooth and well mixed.
