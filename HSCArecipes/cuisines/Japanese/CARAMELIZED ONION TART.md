# CARAMELIZED ONION TART

**Yield:** 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 unbaked 9-inch pie dough
- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 3-4 tablespoons unsalted butter OR olive oil
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup cream
- 2 large eggs
- pinch salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter or oil 9-inch tart pan with removable bottom.
2. Roll crust between parchment; chill for 15 minutes. Transfer crust to prepared pan and rest in fridge again for 30 minutes more.
3. Heat butter or oil in 12-inch sauté pan over medium/low heat. Add onions and cook over (stirring occasionally) until caramelized, about 20 minutes.
4. While onions cook, half blind bake tart crust (with pie weights) for approximately 10-12 minutes (or until lightly golden).
5. Remove crust from oven; turn temperature down to 335°F.
6. Spread caramelized onions into tart shell (do not pat down).
7. Whisk together custard ingredients and pour over onions.
8. Bake until custard is set and crust is golden brown, approximately 12-15 minutes.
