# Scallion-Ginger Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1 scallion, thinly sliced on bias
- 1/4 ounce fresh ginger, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons tamari
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 teaspoon maple crystals

## Instructions

1. In bowl mix all ingredients together. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1/2 hour.
