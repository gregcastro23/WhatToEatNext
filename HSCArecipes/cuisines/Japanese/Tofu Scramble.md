# Tofu Scramble

**Yield:** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
- 1 clove of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons pine nuts
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 pound firm tofu, pressed and then mashed
- 1 large ripe tomato (peeled, seeded and chopped)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt or to taste
- 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice

## Instructions

1. In 12-inch sauté pan over medium heat, warm oil. Add onion and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in garlic, pine nuts, cumin, red pepper flakes and turmeric; sauté 2 minutes more.
2. Add mashed tofu, tomato, salt and lime juice to pan. Raise heat and simmer, stirring, for 5 minutes to heat through.
