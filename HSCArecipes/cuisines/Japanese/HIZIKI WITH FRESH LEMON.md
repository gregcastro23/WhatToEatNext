# HIZIKI WITH FRESH LEMON

**Yield:** 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried hiziki (rinsed and soaked 15-20 minutes)
- 1 small to medium onion, small dice
- 2 teaspoons sesame oil
- water
- 2 to 3 tablespoons shoyu
- 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
- 6 scallions, finely sliced

## Instructions

1. Drain off any remaining soaking water from hiziki and discard.
2. In 10-inch pan, sauté onion in oil for 3 to 5 minutes. Add hiziki, mixing well.
3. Add enough water to just barely cover hiziki. Add shoyu. Bring to boil. Cover, lower flame, and cook until most of liquid has evaporated – about 30 minutes.
4. Gently stir in scallions and lemon juice until well mixed. Serve.
