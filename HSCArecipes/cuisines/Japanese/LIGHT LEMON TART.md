# LIGHT LEMON TART

**Yield:** one 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup agar flakes
- 2 cups apple juice or white grape juice
- 1 cup rice syrup
- 1/2 cup Grade A maple syrup
- 1/8 teaspoon turmeric
- pinch sea salt
- 1/4 cup kuzu mixed with 1/2 cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3/4 cup lemon juice
- 1 1/2 tablespoons lemon zest

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, simmer agar in apple juice until agar is completely dissolved.
2. Add rice syrup, maple syrup, turmeric and pinch of salt.
3. Combine kuzu slurry with vanilla; add to agar-juice mixture. Cook until kuzu begins to thicken and goes from cloudy to clear.
4. Add lemon juice and zest.
5. Pour mixture into shallow bowl or pan and cool completely. Whisk frequently so that it does not 'set up.'
6. When mixture is cooled, pour into pre-baked and cooled tart crust.
7. Refrigerate tart to set filling.
