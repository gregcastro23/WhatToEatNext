# Ketchup (for Tempeh Reuben Sandwich)

**Yield:** approximately 1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup golden raisins (soaked in 1/4 cup water, 10-15 minutes)
- 1 1/2 cups tomato puree
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled & coarsely chopped
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne powder
- pinch cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine raisins (with soaking liquid), tomato puree, garlic, water, paprika, cayenne, cinnamon, and salt. Blend until all ingredients are combined.
2. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, on high heat, heat oil. Slowly add tomato mixture. Lower heat and simmer 40 minutes. Add Worcestershire sauce and lemon juice. Simmer 5 minutes more. Adjust seasonings.
