# Tapenade

*(Fall/Winter)*

**Yield:** approximately 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rinsed and pitted Niçoise OR Kalamata olives
- 2 tablespoons capers, rinsed
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 cup pine nuts, toasted
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh basil
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 cups stemmed parsley, loosely packed
- 1 sheet nori, toasted and crumbled

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in food processor. Pulse to spreadable paste-like consistency.
