# TOFU BURGERS

**Yield:** 8-10 burgers

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
- 1/4 cup shoyu
- 1 cup cooked millet OR rice OR bread crumbs
- 1 pound firm tofu
- 2 tablespoons arame
- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil OR sesame oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup onion, minced
- 1 teaspoon curry powder OR other spice blend
- 1/4 cup carrot, finely minced
- 1 stalk celery, finely minced
- 1 1/2 tablespoon umeboshi paste
- 2 tablespoons tahini
- Salt to taste
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper or more to taste
- Cornmeal for dredging

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix seeds and shoyu together. Roast on parchment paper until dried and toasted.
2. Prepare millet (2 cups water to 1 cup millet for 30 minutes) or brown rice (2 1/2 cups water to 1 cup rice for 55 minutes).
3. Press tofu to remove excess water by placing tofu between paper towels and putting weighted plate on top. Let sit at least 5-10 minutes.
4. Soak arame in water 10-15 minutes. Drain and chop finely.
5. Heat oil, sauté garlic, onion, spices, carrot and celery for 2-3 minutes.
6. Grind sunflower seeds halfway in food processor. In a separate bowl, mash tofu, add sautéed vegetables, 1 cup cooked grain, ground sunflower seeds, arame, ume, tahini, and salt and pepper to taste. Adjust salt if needed. Mix well.
7. Form 1/2" thick burgers with about 1/2 cup mixture.
8. Bake burgers at 350°F until set (about 15-20 minutes) or pan fry (dredge in corn meal first) on each side, only turning once until golden (drain on paper towels).
9. Serve on whole wheat burger bun with lettuce, red onion slice, and mustard.
