# ZUCCHINI PEANUT NOODLE BOWL W/ SRIRACHA STRING BEANS & LEMONGRASS TOFU

**Yield:** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 4 large zucchinis
- 1/3 cup cilantro leaves, sliced
- 2 cups peanuts, roasted
- 1/3 cup water
- 4 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1/3 cup toasted sesame oil
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 2 limes, juiced
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 1 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, or to taste
- 1/2 pound green beans, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon sriracha
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 3 tablespoons sesame seeds
- 2 blocks firm tofu, cut into small even cubes
- 3 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 lemongrass stalk, bruised and cut
- 2 tablespoons brown rice vinegar
- 2/3 cup apple juice
- 3 tablespoons tamari
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2-inch ginger, peeled and minced
- 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
- 1 cup sesame oil
- 1/2 cup green microgreens
- 1/2 small red cabbage, shaved thinly

## Instructions

1. Cut zucchinis in half crosswise, and position on spiralizer. Turn to create zucchini noodles, trimming them with scissors if they are too long. Toss with cilantro before serving.
2. Add peanuts, water, garlic, sesame oil, maple syrup, lime juice, cayenne, coconut milk and salt to Vitamix, or high-speed blender, and puree until smooth.
3. Bring medium pot with steamer basket, filled with 2 inches of water to boil. Steam string beans 3 minutes, or until bright green and slightly tender. Cut in half on diagonal.
4. Heat coconut oil over medium heat in large sauté pan. Cook beans with salt for 2 minutes, stirring often. Add sriracha, syrup, and sesame seeds and cook for additional 30 seconds.
5. Preheat oven to 375°F. Arrange tofu in single layer in baking dish.
6. In medium bowl whisk together sesame oil, vinegar, apple juice, tamari, garlic and ginger. Pour over tofu, add lemongrass, and marinate for 15 to 30 minutes. Bake uncovered for 30 to 40 minutes, or until tofu is golden and has absorbed most of liquid. Discard lemongrass.
7. Combine turmeric and sesame in small saucepan. Warm for 30 seconds, remove from heat, strain out turmeric before using.
8. Arrange noodles in bowl. Top with string beans, tofu, turmeric oil, microgreens, cabbage, and peanut sauce.
