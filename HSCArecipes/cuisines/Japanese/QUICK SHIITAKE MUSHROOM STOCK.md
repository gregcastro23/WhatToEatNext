# QUICK SHIITAKE MUSHROOM STOCK

**Yield:** 2 quarts

## Ingredients

- 2 ounces dried shiitake mushrooms
- One 8-inch piece kombu

## Instructions

1. In small stockpot, soak mushrooms in 2 quarts water for 30 minutes.
2. Add kombu and bring to boil. Lower flame and simmer 5 minutes.
3. Strain stock through fine chinois, pressing firmly to extract as much liquid as possible.

*Cuisine: Japanese | Categories: Stock, Vegetarian*
