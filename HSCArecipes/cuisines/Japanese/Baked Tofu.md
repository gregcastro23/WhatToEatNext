# Baked Tofu

**Yield:** 16 (3/4-ounce) portions

## Ingredients

- 14 ounces firm tofu (1 block), cut into 4 slabs, pressed for 30 minutes
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/3 cup apple juice
- 2 tablespoons tamari
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 recipe Romesco Sauce (recipe following)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F.
2. Slice each slab of tofu into 16 cubes.
3. In a small baking vessel, combine tofu with cumin, paprika, garlic powder, apple juice, tamari, and oil.
4. Transfer to oven and bake 15 - 20 minutes. Remove from oven and serve with Romesco Sauce.
