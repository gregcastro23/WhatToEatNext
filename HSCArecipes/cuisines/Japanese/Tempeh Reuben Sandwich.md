# Tempeh Reuben Sandwich

**Yield:** 6-8 sandwiches

## Ingredients

- 1 recipe Fried Tempeh (recipe follows)
- 1 recipe Spelt Bread (recipe follows)
- Sliced tomato
- Avocado spread or Nayonaise
- Mustard
- Ketchup (recipe below)
- Sauerkraut
- Pickles

## Instructions

1. Assemble the sandwich by spreading avocado spread or Nayonaise on sliced Spelt Bread.
2. Layer with Fried Tempeh, sliced tomato, mustard, ketchup, and sauerkraut.
3. Garnish with pickles and serve immediately.
