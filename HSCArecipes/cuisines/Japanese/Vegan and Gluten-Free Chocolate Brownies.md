# Vegan and Gluten-Free Chocolate Brownies

**Yield:** 36 brownies

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups almond flour
- 1/2 cup sorghum flour
- 1/2 cup tapioca flour
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/4 cups chopped chocolate, divided
- 1/4 cup solidified coconut oil
- 1 1/2 cups coconut sugar
- 1/4 cup coconut milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 tablespoons kuzu + 3 tablespoons water
- 1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (convection oven to 300°F). Oil and flour 9-inch square pan and line with parchment.
2. In bowl, whisk together almond flour, sorghum flour, tapioca flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Process to combine. Transfer dry ingredients to bowl.
3. In separate bowl set over simmering water, melt 1 cup of chocolate and coconut oil. Remove from heat and whisk chocolate and coconut oil together. Stir in kuzu-water mixture, coconut sugar, coconut milk, and vanilla. Whisk wet ingredients to emulsify.
4. Pour wet mixture into dry and stir well. The batter should be stiff. Fold in walnuts and remaining 1/4 cup chocolate.
5. Transfer batter to pan, place parchment atop brownie. Smooth surface with hands until even.
6. Bake 30 minutes. Cool in pan for about 1 hour (otherwise brownie will crack when cut). Cut edges of brownie away from sides of pan before removing from pan.
