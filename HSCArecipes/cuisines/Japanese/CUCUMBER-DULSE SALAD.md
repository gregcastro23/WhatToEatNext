# CUCUMBER-DULSE SALAD

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 pound Kirby cucumbers
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 cup dulse, loosely packed (.04 ounces)
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic (1 large clove)
- 1 teaspoon stone-ground mustard
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Score skin of cucumbers along length with peeler or zester. Slice thinly into rounds and sprinkle with salt. Set aside for 30 minutes. Rinse, if too salty.
2. Quickly rinse dulse in bowl of cold water, until softened. Squeeze excess liquid out. Finely chop dulse.
3. Combine dulse and cucumbers in medium bowl. Mix well.
4. In small bowl, whisk, rice vinegar, garlic, and mustard. Slowly drizzle in olive oil and whisk to combine. Pour dressing over vegetables. Let marinate 15-30 minutes.
