# Morning Glory Muffins

*(Vegan)*

**Yield:** 16 muffins, 1/4 batter cup per muffin

## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 3/4 cups coconut sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 7 ounces carrot, grated
- 1/2 cup raisins, soaked for 5 minutes in hot water and drained
- 1/2 cup pecans, finely chopped
- 2/3 cup shredded coconut
- 1 Granny Smith apple, peeled and grated
- 1 tablespoon flax seed, ground, mixed with 3 tablespoons water
- 2 tablespoons ground kuzu, mixed with 3 tablespoons water
- 3/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350° F (325° F in convection). Prepare muffin tins with paper liners.
2. Sift flour, sugar, baking soda, and cinnamon into bowl. Whisk ingredients together and set aside.
3. Combine carrot, raisins, pecans, coconut, and apple in bowl. Mix into the dry ingredients.
4. Whisk together the flax mixture, kuzu mixture, oil, and vanilla.
5. Add the liquid ingredients to batter. Stir to combine. Let batter sit for 2-3 minutes to allow moisture from the fruit to loosen batter, stir again.
6. Line muffin tin with cupcake liners. Portion batter with 1/4 cup ice cream scoop (yellow handle).
7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until skewer or toothpick inserted in center of muffin comes out clean. Cool muffins completely on cooling rack before serving.
