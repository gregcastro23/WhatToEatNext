# Vegan Caesar Salad

*Year-Round*

**Yield:** approximately 1 cup dressing

## Ingredients

- 2-3 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 tablespoon white miso
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon + 1 1/2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon umeboshi paste
- 1 teaspoon shoyu
- 1 ounce soft tofu, blanched and drained
- 1/3 sheet nori, toasted
- 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 head romaine lettuce, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
- 2 cups Croutons (recipe below)
- Freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine garlic, miso, lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, umeboshi paste, shoyu, tofu, nori, and oil. Blend until mixture is smooth and creamy.
2. Toss salad with croutons and dressing; serve immediately.
