# VEGETABLE AND TEMPEH WRAPS WITH AVOCADO-CILANTRO CREAM

**Yield:** Eight to ten 1-cup (3 ounce) wraps/1 cup avocado cream

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
- 8-ounce package tempeh, grated to texture of ground beef
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 red onion (6 ounces), 1/8-inch salad slice, soaked in water for 10 minutes, drained
- 1 red pepper (8 ounces), seeded, pith removed, and cut into 1/8-inch strips
- 1 zucchini (7 ounces), seeded, cut into 3-inch julienne
- 1 English cucumber (12 ounces), peel, seed, cut into 3-inch julienne
- 1 carrot cut (6-8 ounces), cut into 3-inch julienne, blanched for 1 1/2 minutes and shocked
- 8-10 sheets rice paper
- 2 ripe avocados
- 1 ounce cilantro leaves, picked, washed, and spun dry
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon brown rice vinegar
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In non-stick pan, heat oil and lightly brown tempeh for 4-5 minutes. Add cumin and coriander, cooking 3 minutes more. Set mixture aside in bowl to cool.
2. Drain onion and combine with red pepper, zucchini, cucumber, carrot, and tempeh mixture. Mix well.
3. Rehydrate each rice paper wrap in bowl of warm water until soft. Transfer wrap to clean dry towel.
4. Spoon 1 cup of vegetable-tempeh filling on lower part of wrap in sausage shape. Fold bottom of wrap over mixture, then left and right sides to meet in middle. Finish rolling wrap forward to form shape of spring roll. Repeat with remaining rolls.
5. In bowl of food processor puree avocado, cilantro, olive oil, brown rice vinegar, pepper, and salt until smooth.
6. Slice each wrap in half on diagonal and top with dollop of avocado cream.
