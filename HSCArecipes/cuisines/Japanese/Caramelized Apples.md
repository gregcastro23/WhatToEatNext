# Caramelized Apples

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter or coconut oil
- 4 apples, peeled and sliced
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon dried ginger
- Pinch salt
- 1/2 cup maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Melt butter or oil in large 12-inch pan over medium heat. Add apples to the pan with cinnamon, ginger, and salt.
2. Sauté apples until caramelized, stirring only occasionally, approximately 15 minutes.
3. Add maple syrup to pan. Sauté apples another 5-10 minutes until mixture thickens.
