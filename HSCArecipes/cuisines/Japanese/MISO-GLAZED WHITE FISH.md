# MISO-GLAZED WHITE FISH

**Yield:** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup sake
- 1/3 cup mirin
- 1 cup white miso
- ¾ cup rice syrup
- 1/3 cup shoyu
- 8 white fish filets, skin on, divided into eight 4-ounce portions

## Instructions

1. Combine sake, mirin, miso, rice syrup, and shoyu in small pot. Whisk to emulsify. Transfer to ice bath to quickly cool.
2. Place filets in shallow hotel pan and pour marinade over them. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
3. Heat broiler. Lightly oil half sheet tray. Arrange filets skin side down on tray. Broil 5-7 minutes.
