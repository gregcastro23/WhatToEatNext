# MUSHROOM SAUCE

**Yield:** 1 3/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 4 ounces onion (1/2 medium), small dice
- 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, stemmed and thinly sliced
- Pinch sea salt
- 2 teaspoons ground kuzu
- 1 cup room-temperature vegetable stock
- 1 tablespoon tamari
- 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
- Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
- 2 scallions (1 ounce), trimmed and thinly sliced on bias

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil on medium-high flame. Sauté onion until caramelized. Add mushrooms with salt and continue to sauté a few minutes more, until mushrooms are caramelized.
2. In small bowl, dissolve kuzu in stock and tamari. Whisk kuzu slurry into onion-mushroom mixture.
3. Bring sauce to boil, and reduce flame to medium. Simmer partially covered 1-2 minutes until sauce thickens, or until desired nappé consistency is achieved. Whisk in vinegar.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Garnish with scallions.
