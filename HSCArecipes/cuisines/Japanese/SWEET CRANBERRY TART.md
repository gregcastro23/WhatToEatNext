# SWEET CRANBERRY TART

**Yield:** 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 blind-baked 9-inch tart shell
- 3 cups cranberries, rinsed
- 3/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 cup currants
- 1/2 cup apple juice
- 1 tablespoon agar flakes
- 1 tablespoon kuzu dissolved in 1/3 cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon finely grated orange zest
- 1 cup toasted, chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, mix cranberries, maple syrup, and currants. Bring mixture to boil, reduce to simmer, and cook until all cranberries have popped.
2. In 2 1/2 quart pot, bring apple juice and agar to boil. Reduce heat and cook until agar is dissolved. Stir into cranberry mixture.
3. Mix together kuzu/orange juice slurry and orange zest. Stir kuzu slurry into cranberry mixture and cook until mixture is clear. Remove from heat and add walnuts. Chill to cool.
4. Spoon filling into baked tart shell and chill to set.
