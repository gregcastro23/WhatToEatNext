# Wild Watercress Salad with Citrus

*(Fall/Winter)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 navel oranges, supreme slices (reserve juices)
- 3 blood oranges, supreme slices (reserve juices)
- 2 pink grapefruits, supreme slices (reserve juices)
- 1 pound fennel bulb (1 medium), stalks and core removed, thinly sliced
- 2 ounces red onion (1/2 small), thin rounds
- 1/4 cup oil-cured black olives, pitted, coarsely chopped
- 8 ounces wild watercress, trimmed
- 1/2 cup juice from citrus (see above)
- 1/4 cup rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 1 teaspoon chickpea miso
- 2 tablespoons tahini
- Pinch red pepper flakes
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine citrus supreme in bowl. Set aside.
2. Combine juices from citrus supreme in separate bowl. Set aside 1/2 cup for use in dressing.
3. Cover sliced fennel with cold water in separate bowl. Set aside.
4. Combine dressing ingredients except for oil in blender. Slowly drizzle oil in blender until creamy and emulsified.
5. Drain fennel well. Combine fennel and watercress in medium bowl. Toss with dressing. Garnish with citrus supreme, red onion, olives, and watercress.
