# Vegan Mozzarella

**Yield:** 3 balls

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup raw cashews
- 3/4 cup Cashew Milk (recipe below)
- 1 teaspoon tahini
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/8 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 3/4 cups water, divided
- 2 1/2 teaspoons agar powder

## Instructions

1. Place cashews, cashew milk, tahini, lemon juice, coconut oil, salt, garlic powder, dried basil and 1/4 cup water in a Vitamix and blend until smooth. Blend until very smooth and creamy.
2. Place agar in 1 1/2 cups water and bring to simmer. Simmer until agar dissolves. Add agar mixture to blender and blend until smooth.
3. Pour mixture into silicone molds and refrigerate until set.
