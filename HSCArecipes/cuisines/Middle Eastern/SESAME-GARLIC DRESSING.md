# SESAME-GARLIC DRESSING

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** approx. 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 head garlic, roasted and paste extracted
- 1/2 cup apple juice or cider
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- sea salt

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients (except salt) in blender. Blend to creamy consistency. Season with sea salt.
