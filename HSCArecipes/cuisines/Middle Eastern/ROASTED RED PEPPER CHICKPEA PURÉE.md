# ROASTED RED PEPPER CHICKPEA PURÉE

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup chickpeas, soaked overnight, drained, and rinsed
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tablespoons chopped garlic (2 large cloves)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice (1 1/2 lemons)
- 1 medium red bell pepper, roasted, peeled, and seeded
- 1 tablespoon tahini
- Sea salt and pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika for garnish
- 1 bag pita (6 pieces), cut into triangles and oven toasted

## Instructions

1. Combine chickpeas in pressure cooker with salt, cumin, and bay leaf. Add enough water to cover by 1 inch. Cover and bring to full pressure. Lower heat and cook for 15 minutes. Release pressure by running cold water over lid and drain beans, reserving cooking liquid.
2. Discard bay leaf, and transfer beans to food processor.
3. Add garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, bell pepper, and tahini. Process until smooth, adding bean liquid as necessary to form smooth, thick puree. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Garnish with paprika and serve with pita.
