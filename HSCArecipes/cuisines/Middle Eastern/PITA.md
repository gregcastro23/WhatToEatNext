# PITA

**Yield:** 8 pieces

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups warm water (110°)
- 1 tablespoon warm maple syrup
- 2 teaspoons dry yeast
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/2 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup whole wheat bread flour
- 2 1/2 - 3 cups whole wheat pastry flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat convection oven to 400° F with a pizza stone inside. In bowl, combine water, maple syrup, and yeast. Proof for about 10 minutes or until mixture is foamy.
2. Add oil, salt, all-purpose flour, bread flour, and enough whole wheat pastry flour to create dough that pulls away from sides of bowl.
3. Knead dough about 10-15 minutes, using remaining pastry flour, until smooth dough has been created.
4. Transfer dough to clean, lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Proof in warm place for 25-30 minutes or until dough has doubled in size.
5. While dough is proofing, prepare 3 pizza peels dusted with cornmeal.
6. Fold/turn dough. Rise dough again.
7. Divide dough into 8 equal-sized balls. Using rolling pin, roll each ball of dough into 1/8-inch x 6-inch circle and transfer to pizza peels.
8. Loosely cover dough with damp towels and set aside in warm place. Rise for about 15 minutes.
9. Bake pita for approximately 3-4 minutes or until puffed. Don’t let pita brown, as that makes them too crisp.
