# Cucumber-Citrus Salsa (for Poached Salmon)

*Spring/Summer*

**Yield:** approximately 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 English cucumbers, small dice
- 1 small red onion, small dice
- 1/3 bunch dill, stemmed and minced
- 6 oranges, supreme and each section halved
- juice of 1 lemon
- 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
- salt to taste
- 1/3 jalapeno, minced (optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients and adjust seasonings.
