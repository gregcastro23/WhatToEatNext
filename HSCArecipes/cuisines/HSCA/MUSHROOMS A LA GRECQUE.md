# MUSHROOMS A LA GRECQUE

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds cremini mushrooms, cleaned, stemmed, halved if large
- 1/3 cup dry white wine
- 2 inches lemon peel
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons fennel seeds, coarsely ground
- 3 large cloves garlic, smashed
- 1/2 ounce thyme sprigs
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 teaspoons chopped tarragon (1/4 ounce)
- 4 ounces mesclun greens
- Drizzle of extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, combine mushrooms, wine, lemon peel, lemon juice, olive oil, fennel seeds, garlic, thyme sprigs, salt, pepper, and water.
2. Bring mixture to boil, reduce heat, and simmer, covered, for about 5-7 minutes.
3. Remove lemon peel, thyme sprigs, and smashed garlic. Remove mushrooms from pot with slotted spoon. Serve mushrooms atop mesclun, garnished with tarragon. Drizzle small amount of cooled cooking liquid over salad just before serving.
