# CLASSIC BUTTER AND SAGE SAUCE

## Ingredients

- 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter
- handful fresh sage leaves

## Instructions

1. Heat butter until it browns slightly and add sage leaves.
2. As soon as ravioli are cooked, remove them with slotted spoon, arrange on plates, pour sage butter over.
