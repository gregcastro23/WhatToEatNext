# HORSERADISH-CRUSTED SALMON

**Yield:** Six 4-ounce portions

## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds salmon, skinned and divided into six 4-ounce portions
- Salt and pepper, to season fish
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 ounce parsley, stemmed and finely chopped
- 3 ounces fresh horseradish, peeled and finely grated
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Juice of 2 lemons, approximately ¼ cup
- ¾ cup panko bread crumbs
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Heat convection oven to 375°F.
2. Season filet with salt and pepper.
3. Combine mayonnaise, parsley, horseradish, lemon zest, lemon, bread crumbs, salt, and pepper in large bowl. Stir to combine.
4. Spread mixture in even layer over flesh side of fish.
5. Transfer to half sheet tray lined with lightly oiled parchment paper.
6. Bake for 5-10 minutes, depending on thickness of fish filet.
