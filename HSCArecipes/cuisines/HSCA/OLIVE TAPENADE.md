# OLIVE TAPENADE

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup pitted black olives, preferably Kalamata
- 1 tablespoon capers, drained
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon grated orange zest

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in food processor and blend until smooth. Stop now and again to scrape down sides with spoon or spatula.
