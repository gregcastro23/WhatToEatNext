# Vegan and Gluten-Free Spiced Fruit Muffins

**Yield:** 12 large muffins

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup almond flour
- 1/4 cup flax seed, ground to powder in a coffee grinder
- 1/4 cup red quinoa, ground to powder in a coffee grinder
- 1/2 cup oats
- 2 teaspoons aluminum-free baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon cardamom
- 1/4 teaspoon allspice
- Pinch nutmeg
- Zest of 1 orange
- 1/4 cup applesauce
- 1/2 cup melted coconut oil
- 2/3 cup Grade B maple syrup
- 1 cup diced pear (or may use diced Granny Smith apples)
- 1/2 cup frozen or fresh cranberries (could use blueberries or cherries, instead)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Line muffin tins with muffin papers or use silicon baking molds.
3. Whisk together almond meal, flax meal, ground quinoa, oats, baking powder, salt, pepper, spices, and orange zest in bowl; set aside.
4. Whisk together applesauce, oil, and maple syrup in separate bowl.
5. Make well in dry ingredients and pour in wet mixture; stir to combine. Mix in pear and cranberries (or apples and blueberries).
6. Fill each muffin chamber 2/3 full. Bake 20-25 minutes or until skewer inserted in center of a muffin comes out clean.
7. Leave muffins in chambers for 10 minutes before inverting onto a cooling rack. Allow muffins to cool completely before serving.
