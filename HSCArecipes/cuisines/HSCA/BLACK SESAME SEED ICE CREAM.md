# BLACK SESAME SEED ICE CREAM

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 1 (14 oz.) can coconut milk
- 1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons cup rice syrup
- Pinch sea salt
- 3 tablespoons black sesame seeds, toasted then ground

## Instructions

1. Prepare ice bath. Combine coconut milk, maple syrup, rice syrup, salt, and sesame seeds in pot over high heat. When mixture boils, turn heat down and whisk for 1-2 minutes. Remove mixture from heat, and transfer pot to ice bath to cool mixture to room temperature.
2. Pour mixture into ice cream machine and churn until creamy and somewhat firm.
