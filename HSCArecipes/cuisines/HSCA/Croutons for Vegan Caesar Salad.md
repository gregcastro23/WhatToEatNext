# Croutons for Vegan Caesar Salad

*Year-Round*

## Ingredients

- 1/2 loaf French OR Italian whole wheat bread, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/8 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
2. In deep bowl, toss bread cubes with oil and salt until each cube is well covered.
3. Spread croutons in single layer on parchment-lined sheet tray. Bake until croutons are crisp and evenly brown (20-30 minutes), turning often.
