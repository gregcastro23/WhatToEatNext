# GOAT CHEESE, BEET, AND WALNUT SALAD

**Yield:** 20 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 heads Romaine lettuce
- 4 bunches watercress, stemmed
- 2 heads red oak leaf lettuce
- 2 heads frisee
- 4 endive
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 20 small beets, roasted, peeled and cut into bite size pieces
- 2 cups toasted walnuts
- 2 1/2 pounds soft goat cheese
- 2 baguettes, cut into 1/2" slices and toasted
- 1 recipe Creamy Fresh Horseradish Dressing (recipe below)

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, toss all greens together and put on individual plates.
2. Assemble each plate with endive, onion, beets, walnuts, goat cheese, and baguettes.
3. Dress each salad immediately prior to service.
