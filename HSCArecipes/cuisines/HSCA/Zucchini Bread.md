# Zucchini Bread

*(Vegan)*

**Yield:** One 4 1/2 x 9-inch loaf

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flax seed, ground, mixed with 3 tablespoons water
- 1/2 cup melted oil
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
- 6 ounces zucchini, grated
- 2/3 cup pecans, roughly chopped
- 1 1/4 cups coconut sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup + 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup + 3 tablespoons whole wheat pastry flour

## Instructions

1. Heat convection oven to 350° F. Line a 4 1/2 x 9-inch loaf pan with parchment.
2. Whisk together flax mixture, canola oil, vanilla, and rice vinegar in bowl. Stir in zucchini and pecans. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, sift together coconut sugar, cinnamon, salt, baking soda, baking powder, all-purpose flour and whole wheat pastry flour.
4. Whisk wet ingredients into dry. Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 55 minutes.
5. Let bread cool completely before slicing and serving.
