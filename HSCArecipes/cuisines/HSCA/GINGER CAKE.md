# GINGER CAKE

**Yield:** Two 8-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- Pinch ground cloves
- 4 ounces (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/3 cup maple crystals, sifted
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 3/4 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 1/3 cup orange juice (2 oranges)
- 2/3 cup almond milk, room temperature
- 1/2 teaspoon orange extract

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection). Oil and flour two 8-inch cake pans and line bottoms with parchment.
2. Sift together flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, and cloves in medium bowl and whisk together to combine. Set aside.
3. Place softened butter in bowl of stand mixer. On low speed, mix just until butter is fluffy. Add sea salt and maple crystals; mix until creamy and well combined, about 2 minutes.
4. Add one egg at a time. Slowly add in maple syrup, orange juice, milk, and extracts. Combine on medium speed until well incorporated (mixture may look curdled, but will come back together once remaining ingredients are added).
5. Spoon combined dry ingredients into mixer a little at a time and mix until completely incorporated into butter mixture, scraping down sides of bowl as necessary.
6. Pour batter into 2 prepared pans. Bake about 20 minutes or until tops are golden brown and springy to touch.
7. Cool on wire rack for 10-15 minutes. Remove from pans and completely cool on rack.
