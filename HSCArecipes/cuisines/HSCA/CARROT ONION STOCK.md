# CARROT ONION STOCK

**Yield:** 6-7 quarts (1 3/4 gallons)

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 head garlic, smashed and peeled
- 6 onions (5 pounds), peeled, coarsely chopped (approximately 1/2-inch pieces)
- 6 carrots (2 1/2 pounds), peeled, coarsely chopped
- 1 head celery, coarsely chopped
- 4 shallots (1/2 pound), peeled, coarsely chopped
- Bouquet Garni:
- 1 fresh thyme sprig
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/2 bunch parsley stems

## Instructions

1. In 3-gallon stockpot, heat oil. Add vegetables and sweat.
2. Add water to cover (7-8 quarts) and bouquet garni. Bring to boil and skim off foam. Simmer, partially covered, for 1 - 1 1/2 hours. Strain immediately through chinois.
