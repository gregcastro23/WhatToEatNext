# PINTO BEAN STEW

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 6 ounces onion, medium dice
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 3 tablespoons minced garlic (3 large cloves)
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 cup pinto beans, soaked overnight, drained, and rinsed
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 dried chipotle
- 4 cups water
- 2 medium red peppers, roasted, seeded, and cut into medium dice
- 1 pound plum tomatoes (4), concassé
- Sea salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (1 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil on medium-low flame. Add onion, salt, garlic, and cumin. Sweat until onion becomes translucent. Add beans, salt, bay leaf, chipotle, and water. Cover pot and bring to boil over high heat. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 35-40 minutes, or until beans are tender. Remove chipotle from pot, mince, and set aside.
2. Add peppers and tomatoes; continue simmering for 5 minutes more.
3. Add minced chipotle and season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
