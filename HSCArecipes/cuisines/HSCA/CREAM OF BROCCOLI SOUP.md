# CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP

**Yield:** 7 cups

## Ingredients

- 18 ounces broccoli (1 small bunch)
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 large onion (12 ounces), medium dice
- 1 teaspoon sea salt, divided
- 5-6 cups stock
- 6 tablespoons Arborio rice
- 2 cups soaked cashews
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill (1/2 ounce), for garnish

## Instructions

1. Peel and slice broccoli stems. Separate broccoli florets into bite-size pieces. Set aside.
2. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil over medium flame. Add onion and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Sweat until onions are translucent, about 10 minutes. Stir often to prevent browning.
3. Add stock, rice, and cashews to pot. Raise heat and bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer until rice is tender, about 15 minutes. Set aside.
4. In separate 1-gallon pot, bring 2 quarts water to boil with remaining salt. Prepare ice bath. Blanch broccoli until tender, about 2 minutes. Drain broccoli and transfer immediately to ice bath. Drain broccoli again.
5. Combine onion-rice and broccoli in Vitamix. Blend until creamy.
6. Return soup to pot. Re-heat and season to taste with salt and pepper.
7. Garnish with dill.
