# Orange Blossom Panna Cotta

**Yield:** 10 cups (20 half-cup servings)

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups organic heavy cream
- 1 cup grade A maple syrup
- Pinch sea salt
- 3 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon agar flakes, combined with 1 1/2 cups water (soaked for 10 minutes)
- 3 1/3 cups buttermilk
- 1/3 teaspoon orange blossom water
- 2 teaspoons orange zest
- 4 oranges, supreme
- 1/2 cup honey, whisked with 2 tablespoons water
- 1/2 cup pistachios, toasted

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, combine heavy cream, maple syrup, and salt. Cook uncovered on low simmer, stirring constantly for about 5-7 minutes. Turn off heat and set aside.
2. Pour soaked agar and water mixture into separate 2 1/2 quart pot. Bring to boil, cover pot, lower heat, and simmer until all agar is completely dissolved, about 5 minutes. Add to pot with heavy cream mixture and cook 2-3 minutes longer. Add orange blossom water and orange zest.
3. Add buttermilk to heavy cream/agar mixture.
4. Ladle mixture into 4-ounce ramekins. Refrigerate until set.
5. Combine orange supreme with rice syrup and serve as garnish with nuts.
