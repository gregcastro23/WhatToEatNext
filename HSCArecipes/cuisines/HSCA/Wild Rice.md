# Wild Rice

## Ingredients

- 1 cup wild rice (soaked in 2 cups water for 8 hours and drained)
- 4 cups water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Wash and drain rice.
2. In small saucepot, add rice to water. Add salt. Bring to a boil, covered, lower heat and simmer about 45 to 50 minutes or until rice is tender (rice should butterfly slightly).
3. Place rice in colander and drain excess water.
