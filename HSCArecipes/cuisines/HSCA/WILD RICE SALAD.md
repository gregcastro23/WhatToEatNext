# WILD RICE SALAD

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup wild rice, soaked overnight, drained, and rinsed
- 1 quart water
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries, chopped
- 1/4 cup walnuts, toasted, chopped
- 3 ounces carrots (1/2 large), matchsticks
- 3 ounces radish (3), matchsticks
- 2 scallions, trimmed, minced
- 2 tablespoons minced chives (1 ounce)
- 7 tablespoons chopped parsley (2 ounces)
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 ounces radicchio, chiffonade
- 2 ounces baby arugula, chopped
- Zest of 1 orange
- 2 tablespoons orange juice (1/2 orange)
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 1 tablespoon brown rice vinegar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1/3 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Combine rice in 2 1/2 quart pot with water and salt. Bring rice to boil, reduce heat, and simmer, covered, until grains are tender but still chewy (about 20-25 minutes). Drain rice.
2. Combine all dressing ingredients except for oil in blender. Slowly drizzle oil into blender until emulsified. Pour half of dressing over still warm rice.
3. When rice is completely cooled, combine with cranberries, walnuts, carrots, radishes, scallions, chives, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper, if needed.
4. Toss radicchio and arugula with remaining dressing, arrange on plate, and top with rice salad.
