# Almond Torte

**Yield:** One 8-inch torte

## Ingredients

- 3 cups blanched almonds, dried well
- 6 eggs
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 2 teaspoons almond extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Oil and flour 8-inch spring form pan.
2. Powder almonds in food processor. Add eggs, maple syrup and almond extract. Process until smooth.
3. Pour batter into pan.
4. Bake for 40 minutes. Top should be firm to touch and golden brown.
