# BAKED QUINOA WITH PEAS AND HERBS

**Yield:** approximately 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa, washed and drained
- 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1 1/4 cup boiling water OR stock
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 pound frozen peas (about 1/2 cup), thawed
- 2 tablespoons minced parsley
- 2 tablespoons minced cilantro (about 1/2 bunch)
- 2 tablespoons minced chives (about 1/4 bunch)
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
2. Transfer quinoa to 8 x 8 baking casserole. Roast about 20 minutes or until quinoa is dry. Add 1 tablespoon of oil and stir into quinoa. Bake 5 minutes more, stirring once or twice.
3. Add water and 1/2 teaspoon salt to quinoa. Tightly cover with tin foil and bake 20 minutes or until all water is absorbed. Remove from oven and let sit covered 5 minutes. Remove foil and fluff with fork. Place quinoa into bowl and let cool.
4. Add peas, parsley, cilantro, chives, remaining 1 teaspoon oil, and lemon juice to cooked quinoa. Season with remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt.
