# APPLE CRUMB PIE

**Yield:** 9-inch pie

## Ingredients

- 1 unbaked 9-inch pie dough
- 3/4 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 3/4 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup canola OR melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil
- 2 1/2 pounds apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
- pinch sea salt
- 1/4 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Roll out pie dough and line pie plate. Trim and form edges, cover, and chill for at least 30 minutes.
3. In small bowl, whisk together flours. In separate bowl, whisk together oil and maple syrup. Slowly drizzle oil and maple syrup mixture into flour mixture. Stir lightly to form small, moist crumbs. Cover and chill.
4. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil. Add apple slices and sauté until apples are slightly softened. Set aside to partially cool.
5. In large bowl, combine apples, maple syrup, lemon juice, vanilla, spices, salt, and flour. Toss until apples are well coated.
6. Place filling in pie shell. Top with crumbs. Loosely cover crust with tented foil to keep edges from browning too quickly.
7. Bake pie for 40-45 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is firm. Remove foil 10 minutes before removing pie from oven.
8. Cool pie before serving.
