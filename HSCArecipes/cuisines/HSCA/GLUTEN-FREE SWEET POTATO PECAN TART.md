# GLUTEN-FREE SWEET POTATO PECAN TART

**Yield:** 10-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 cup pecans, lightly toasted and cooled
- 1/4 cup maple crystals
- 6 tablespoons tapioca starch flour
- 6 tablespoons sorghum flour
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons water
- Pinch sea salt
- 3 tablespoons gluten free breadcrumbs mixed with 2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1 1/2 pounds garnet yams (2 large) cooked and mashed (about 2 1/4 cups)
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 3 large eggs, slightly beaten
- 1/3 cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon ginger juice
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
- pinch ground cloves
- pinch sea salt
- 1 3/4 cups whole pecans OR 1 cup thick coconut flakes
- 1-2 tablespoons maple syrup (if using pecans)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F and lightly grease tart pan with small amount of softened butter or oil.
2. Grind pecans and maple crystals to fine meal in food processor. Add flours and pulse to combine about 30 seconds more. Transfer mixture from food processor to bowl. In separate bowl, whisk together melted butter, water and, salt. Pour wet mixture into dry and mix to combine. If mixture is too moist, refrigerate for 5-10 minutes before pressing out crust.
3. Press crust mixture into tart pan. Refrigerate 15-20 minutes or until crust is firm to touch. Pierce crust with fork and partially bake for 15-20 minutes. Cool before filling.
4. Before filling tart shell, sprinkle breadcrumbs and maple crystals over crust (this prevents crust from getting soggy).
5. In food processor, puree yams. Add syrup, eggs, cream, vanilla, ginger juice, ground spices, and salt. Blend until mixture is creamy and smooth. Transfer puree to partially baked pie shell.
6. Decorate top of tart with pecans and brush nuts with maple syrup or top with coconut (omitting maple syrup wash).
7. Bake tart 30-35 minutes until filling is just firm. Allow to cool completely before slicing.
