# BASIC CHICKEN STOCK

**Yield:** 1 gallon

## Ingredients

- 5 pounds chicken bones
- 8 ounces yellow onion (1 medium), large dice
- 4 ounces carrot (1 small), large dice
- 3 ribs celery (6 ounces), large dice
- Sachet of 2 bay leaves, 4 sprigs thyme, 2 teaspoons black peppercorns, 1/2 ounce parsley stems
- 5 quarts cold water

## Instructions

1. Combine bones, onion, carrot, celery, sachet, and water in large stock pot.
2. Cover pot and bring to boil. Remove cover and skim any foam off top.
3. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 3-4 hours.
4. Strain stock immediately, first through china cap, then chinois.
5. Cool stock in ice bath and refrigerate.
