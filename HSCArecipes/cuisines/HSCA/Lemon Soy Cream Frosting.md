# Lemon Soy Cream Frosting

**Yield:** 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup apple juice
- 2 tablespoons agar flakes
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 pounds firm tofu, blanched and lightly pressed
- 2/3 cup maple crystals
- 1/4 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup vanilla extract
- 2 teaspoons lemon extract
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest (1 lemon)

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine juice, agar flakes, and salt. Let sit for 5 minutes. Simmer mixture until agar is fully dissolved, about 10 minutes.
2. In food processor, puree tofu with maple crystals until very creamy. Add maple syrup, oil, and extracts, and process until very smooth.
3. Slowly, with processor running, drizzle hot juice-agar mixture into tofu mixture. Fold in zest.
4. Transfer frosting to medium bowl and refrigerate to set up.
