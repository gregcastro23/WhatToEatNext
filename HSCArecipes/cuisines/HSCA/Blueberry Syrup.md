# Blueberry Syrup

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 3 cups maple syrup
- 10-ounce bag frozen blueberries
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in 2 1/2 quart pot. Bring syrup to boil, reduce heat, and simmer mixture uncovered for 15 minutes.
2. Strain syrup through chinois, pressing any remaining juice from blueberries. Discard blueberries, and transfer syrup to syrup containers.
