# POTATO-CRUSTED BASS FILETS

**Yield:** 8 portions

## Ingredients

- 1 pound russet potatoes, peeled, grated, soaked for 15 minutes in water
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 ounce thyme, picked and finely chopped
- 4 eggs
- 8 bass filets, skin removed, seasoned with salt and pepper
- Canola oil to pan fry

## Instructions

1. Drain potatoes, squeeze as much excess water from potatoes as possible with cheesecloth. Set aside.
2. Combine flour, salt, pepper, and thyme in bowl. Set aside.
3. Combine and whisk eggs in large bowl.
4. Liberally season filets with salt and pepper.
5. Dredge fish filet with flour, shaking off excess.
6. Transfer filets to egg mixture, making sure to coat entire filet.
7. Press potatoes onto filet, completely coating them.
8. Heat non-stick sauté pan over medium heat with 1/4-inch canola oil.
9. Transfer filets to pan and brown on both sides. If filets are thick, transfer browned filets to 375°F oven to finish cooking.
