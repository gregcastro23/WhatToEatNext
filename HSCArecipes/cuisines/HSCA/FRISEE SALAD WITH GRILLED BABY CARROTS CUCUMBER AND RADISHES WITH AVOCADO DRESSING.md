# FRISEE SALAD WITH GRILLED BABY CARROTS, CUCUMBER, AND RADISHES WITH AVOCADO DRESSING

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 pound baby carrots, peeled (retain 1/4-inch of stem)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Pinch sea salt
- 7 ounces radishes (1 bunch), thinly sliced rounds
- 4 ounces English cucumber (1/2 cucumber), peeled, thinly sliced
- 4 ounces frisee, chopped into bite-size pieces
- 2 tablespoons seeded and minced jalapeno (1 jalapeno)
- 1 tablespoon lime juice (1 lime)
- 1/2 avocado, sliced into 8 slices
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic (1 small clove garlic)
- 4 ounces English cucumber (1/2 cucumber) peeled, sliced, seeded
- 1/3 avocado, pitted
- 2 tablespoons lime juice (2 limes)
- 1 tablespoon chopped dill (1/4 ounce)
- 3 tablespoons chopped mint (1/2 ounce)
- 1/4 cup chopped chives (1/2 ounce)
- 3/4-1 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Toss carrots in bowl with olive oil and salt. Heat grill. Grill carrots until grill marks are visible. Set aside.
2. In medium bowl, combine radishes, cucumber, frisee, and jalapeno.
3. Pour lime juice over avocado slices and set aside.
4. Combine dressing ingredients in blender until creamy.
5. Toss salad with dressing. Garnish with carrots and avocado slices.
