# BLUEBERRY CORN MUFFINS

**Yield:** 6 standard size muffins

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons corn flour
- 1/2 cup Gluten-Free Flour Blend (recipe below)
- 4 tablespoon maple crystals
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 stick butter (8 tablespoons), melted
- 1/4 cup milk
- 3 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
- 1/2 cup blueberries, rinsed

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 400°F. Grease muffin pan with butter.
2. In medium bowl, combine dry ingredients.
3. In separate small bowl, combine wet ingredients: butter, milk, maple syrup, and eggs.
4. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Add blueberries. Mix carefully to incorporate.
5. Using standard ice cream scoop, fill muffin tins 3/4 full.
6. Bake 15-18 minutes. Best served warm.
