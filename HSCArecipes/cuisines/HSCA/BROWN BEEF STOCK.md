# BROWN BEEF STOCK

**Yield:** 4 quarts each (1 gallon)

## Ingredients

- 8 pounds beef or veal bones
- 1 pound mirepoix
- 6 quarts water or enough to cover by 2 inches
- 2-3 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup white or red wine or 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Brown bones and vegetables by roasting first in 450° F oven. Simmer 6 to 8 hours.
