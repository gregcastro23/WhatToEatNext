# Gravad Lax

**Yield:** 1 filet

## Ingredients

- 1-1 1/2 pound boneless salmon or Arctic Char filet
- 1 cup sea salt
- 1 cup maple crystals
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 bunch dill, stemmed
- 1 bunch basil, stemmed
- 1 small red onion, sliced thin
- 1/4 cup vodka
- 1 lemon, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Score salmon by making two, 2-3-inch-long and 1/4 inch deep cuts into each side of fish.
2. Transfer salmon to shallow pan skin side down.
3. In bowl, mix all remaining ingredients.
4. Place mixture directly over salmon, packing ingredients down.
5. Wrap salmon tightly in plastic and refrigerate for 3 days to cure. After end of each day, drain any liquid that has been extracted from salmon. If necessary, add another cup of maple crystals and sea salt to salmon.
6. On last day, rinse off salmon. Color of flesh should have darkened, and texture should be firmer.
