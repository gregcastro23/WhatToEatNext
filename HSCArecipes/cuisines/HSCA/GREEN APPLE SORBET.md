# GREEN APPLE SORBET

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup thyme leaves (1/2 ounce)
- 1 cup agave syrup
- 1 cup water
- Pinch sea salt
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 8 medium Granny Smith apples (4 pounds), chopped to fit through feeding tube juicer

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot over medium flame, simmer thyme leaves, agave, water, and salt for 15 minutes.
2. Remove pot from flame. Pour mixture into blender and puree to combine. Strain and set aside in medium bowl.
3. In separate bowl, combine juiced lemons with agave-thyme mixture.
4. Juice apples in Champion juicer and combine with lemon juice and agave-thyme mixture.
5. Transfer mixture to ice cream machines and freeze until scoop-able consistency is achieved. Transfer sorbet to 9-pan and freeze forty minutes to one hour or until sorbet firms up.
