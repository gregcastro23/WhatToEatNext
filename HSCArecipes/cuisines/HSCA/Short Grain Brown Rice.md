# Short Grain Brown Rice

## Ingredients

- 2 cups short grain brown rice, washed
- 3 1/2 cups water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine rice, water and salt in small pressure cooker.
2. Place top on pressure cooker and bring up to pressure.
3. Lower heat and cook 20 minutes. Remove pressure cooker from stove. Let pressure come down completely before attempting to open it.
