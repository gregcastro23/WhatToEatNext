# ASIAN SLAW

*(SPRING SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 12 ounces Napa cabbage, shredded
- 3 ounces snow peas (about 25), julienne
- 5 ounces carrots (2 small), matchstick
- 1 jalapeno, minced (1 teaspoon)
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (1/2 ounce)
- 1 ounce ginger root, juiced (2 tablespoons)
- 6 tablespoons brown rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup rice syrup
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 cup canola oil
- 6-8 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
- 2-3 tablespoons black sesame seeds, to garnish

## Instructions

1. Combine cabbage, snow peas, carrots, jalapeno, and cilantro in a medium/large bowl.
2. Combine dressing ingredients in blender until creamy.
3. Pour dressing over vegetables and mix well.
4. Garnish with black sesame seeds.
