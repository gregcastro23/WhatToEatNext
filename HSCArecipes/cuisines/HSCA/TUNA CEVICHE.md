# TUNA CEVICHE

**Yield:** Eight servings

## Ingredients

- 6 ounces red onion, thin salad slice
- 1 ounce serrano chili, seeded and minced
- 1 1/4 ounces ginger, peeled and minced
- 2 ounces cilantro, stemmed and finely chopped
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 can coconut milk
- Juice of 1 lime
- 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pound tuna, medium dice

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in large bowl.
2. Cover with plastic wrap and transfer to refrigerator for 30-45 minutes before serving.
