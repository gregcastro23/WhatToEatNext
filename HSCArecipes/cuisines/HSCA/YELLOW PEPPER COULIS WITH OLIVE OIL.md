# Yellow Pepper Coulis with Olive Oil

**Yield:** 1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 6 ounces shallots (3 medium), minced
- 10 ounces yellow pepper (2 medium), medium dice
- 1 tablespoon brown rice vinegar
- 1 3/4 cups vegetable stock, warmed
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil over medium flame. Sweat shallots until translucent.
2. Add peppers and sweat until softened.
3. Add vinegar and reduce until almost dry (au sec).
4. Add stock and simmer uncovered for 15 minutes.
5. Puree in blender until creamy and return to pan. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
