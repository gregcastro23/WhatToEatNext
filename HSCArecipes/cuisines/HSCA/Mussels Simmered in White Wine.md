# Mussels Simmered in White Wine

**Yield:** 3 servings (approximately 8 per person)

## Ingredients

- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1/2 small red onion, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup minced parsley (4 ounces)
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter OR 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- black pepper and sea salt to taste
- 20-25 fresh live mussels, cleaned

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot or 12-inch sauté pan, combine wine, onion, garlic, parsley, bay leaf, and butter OR oil. Cover and bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 2-3 minutes. Add mussels, cover and cook for 3-5 minutes over high heat until mussels open. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Using slotted spoon, remove mussels from pot or pan. Allow cooking liquid to settle in case of sand, and then ladle liquid over mussels. Serve immediately.
