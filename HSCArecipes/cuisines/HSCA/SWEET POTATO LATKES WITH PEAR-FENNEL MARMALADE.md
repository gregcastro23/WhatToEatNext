# SWEET POTATO LATKES WITH PEAR-FENNEL MARMALADE

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 25 1-inch latkes

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 pound fennel (1/2 small), cored, thinly sliced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 Bosc pears, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic (1 small clove garlic)
- 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 tablespoon chopped chives (1/4 ounce)
- 1 pound sweet potatoes, peeled, grated (large hole side of grater)
- 2 tablespoons thinly sliced scallions (2 scallions)
- 1/3 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1/4 cup arrowroot
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 3/4 cup canola oil
- Sea salt to taste
- 25 fennel fronds
- 1/2 cup cashews, soaked 4 hours to overnight (discard soaking liquid)
- 2 tablespoon lime juice (1-2 limes)
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt of to taste
- 1 scallion, trimmed, chopped
- 2 tablespoons water

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil over medium flame. Add fennel and salt. Sweat until fennel is translucent. Add pears and sweat for an additional 3-5 minutes or until fennel and pears are barely tender.
2. Add garlic and coriander; continue to sweat for another minute. Add vinegar and water. Simmer for about 5 minutes or until liquid has been absorbed. Season to taste. When cool, add chives and mix to combine. Set aside.
3. In medium bowl, combine grated sweet potatoes, scallions, flour, arrowroot, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Mix to combine. Form latkes using tablespoon measure. Heat canola oil in 12-inch straight-sided sauté pan over medium-high heat.
4. Fry latkes in batches until golden, about 2 minutes on each side. Drain on paper-lined half sheet pan to absorb any excess oil. Season with additional salt to taste. Serve topped with marmalade, cashew cream and fennel fronds.
5. Combine all ingredients in food processor until smooth and creamy. Season to taste.
