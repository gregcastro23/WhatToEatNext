# APRICOT-PAPAYA PUREE

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 5 dried unsulphured apricots, soaked in boiling water until soft (about 5 minutes)
- 1 ripe papaya (1/2 pound), peeled, seeded and chopped

## Instructions

1. In high-speed blender, blend apricots with papaya, adding a bit of soaking water if necessary to form smooth puree.
