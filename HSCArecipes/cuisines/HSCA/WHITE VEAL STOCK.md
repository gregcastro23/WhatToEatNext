# WHITE VEAL STOCK

**Yield:** 4 quarts each (1 gallon)

## Ingredients

- 8 pounds veal bones
- 6 quarts water or enough to cover by 2 inches
- 1 pound mirepoix
- bouquet garni
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice or cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Simmer 4 to 5 hours.
