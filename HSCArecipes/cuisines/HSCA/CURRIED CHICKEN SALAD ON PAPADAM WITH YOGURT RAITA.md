# CURRIED CHICKEN SALAD ON PAPADAM WITH YOGURT RAITA

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** approximately 24 bite size portions

## Ingredients

- 1 pound chicken thighs (4 medium)
- 2 1/2 cups chicken stock
- Sachet (3 sprigs thyme, 1/2 teaspoon coriander seeds, 1/2 teaspoon peppercorns, 1 bay leaf, 1 cinnamon stick)
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne powder
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons minced red onion (1 ounce)
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (1/4 ounce)
- 1/4 cup pistachios, roasted and chopped
- 1/3 cup full fat yogurt
- 1/4 cup reserved braising liquid
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- Sea salt and pepper to taste
- 24 mini papadam, fried in coconut oil
- 1/4 cup full fat yogurt
- 4 ounces English cucumber (1/2 medium), thinly sliced, halved
- 24 sprigs cilantro (3/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, braise chicken thighs with stock, sachet, spices (ginger, curry, cumin, and cayenne, and cinnamon stick), and salt for 45 minutes. Remove chicken from pan. When cooled, remove skin and bones. Chop chicken into small pieces, set aside.
2. Reduce braising liquid until you have about 1/4 cup syrupy liquid. Remove sachet. Reserve syrupy liquid for dressing.
3. In bowl, combine chicken, red onion, cilantro, and pistachios.
4. In small bowl, whisk together yogurt, reduced braising liquid, lime, salt and pepper. Add dressing to chicken salad.
5. Place approximately 2 teaspoons chicken salad on fried papadam. Garnish with dab of yogurt, slice of cucumber, and sprig of cilantro.
