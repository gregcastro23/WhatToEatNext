# Black Quinoa with Capers and Rapini (for Broiled Arctic Char)

**Yield:** Four 1-cup portions

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup black quinoa, rinsed and dry-roasted in pan
- 1 cup vegetable stock
- Pinch of salt
- 1 bunch rapini (1 pound), washed and trimmed (2 inches of stem removed)
- 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1/4 cup basil leaves, stemmed and tightly packed
- 1/4 cup capers, rinsed
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- Zest of 1 lemon

## Instructions

1. Combine quinoa with vegetable stock and salt in small saucepan. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, and cook covered approximately 15 to 20 minutes or until stock is absorbed. Remove from heat, uncover pot and allow quinoa to rest 10 minutes.
2. In separate pot, bring 2 quarts of salted water to boil. Prepare ice bath. Blanch rapini for 3 minutes, shock in ice water, and drain. Squeeze out excess liquid.
3. Roughly chop rapini and set aside.
4. Heat oil in small sauté pan over low flame, add garlic, cooking briefly until just golden. Do not let garlic brown.
5. Transfer garlic and one tablespoon oil from pan to food processor. Add rapini. Pulse mixture until very finely chopped. Add basil and pulse again.
6. Transfer rapini mixture to bowl and thoroughly mix with quinoa, capers, black pepper, and lemon zest.
7. Divide quinoa-rapini mixture among four plates, placing Arctic Char filet atop each portion.
