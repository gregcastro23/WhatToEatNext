# GRAPEFRUIT-CAMPARI GRANITA

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 3 cups grapefruit juice, strained (4 grapefruits)
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup maple crystals
- 1/4 cup Campari

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, over low flame, simmer juice with water and maple crystals until maple crystals dissolve. Add Campari and stir.
2. Pour mixture into half hotel pan (mixture should be 1/4 inch up side of pan) and transfer to freezer for about 1 hour.
3. Remove pan from freezer and use fork to scrape any ice crystals that have formed on side or bottom of pan. Return to freezer and repeat scraping every 30 minutes for 3-4 hours.
4. Once mixture is thoroughly frozen, scrape once more with fork, returning granita to “dry” in freezer for another half hour before serving.
