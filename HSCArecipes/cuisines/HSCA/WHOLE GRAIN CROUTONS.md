# WHOLE GRAIN CROUTONS

**Yield:** 1 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 4 ounces (about 1/4 loaf) good quality whole grain bread, crust removed, medium dice
- 3-4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, minced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F. Line half-sheet with parchment paper.
2. Toss bread in bowl with oil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Transfer croutons to sheet tray.
3. Bake until bread is golden in color and crisp in texture. Serve over soup.
