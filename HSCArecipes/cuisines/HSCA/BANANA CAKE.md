# BANANA CAKE

**Yield:** Two 8-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2/3 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 3/4 cup coconut sugar, ground
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 cup mashed banana (2 ripe bananas)
- 1/2 cup melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon banana extract (optional)
- 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection). Oil and flour two 8-inch cake pans and line bottom of pans with parchment.
2. In medium bowl, sift together dry ingredients. Whisk to combine.
3. In food processor, combine wet ingredient and process until smooth.
4. Using rubber spatula, transfer wet ingredients from food processor to bowl with dry ingredients. Mix to combine.
5. Pour batter into two prepared pans. Bake about 20-25 minutes or until top are golden brown, and cakes are springy to touch.
6. Cool on wire rack for 10-15 minutes. Remove from pans and completely cool on rack.
