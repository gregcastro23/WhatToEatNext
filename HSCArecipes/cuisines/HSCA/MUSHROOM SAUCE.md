# MUSHROOM SAUCE

**Yield:** 4-5 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 2 ounces onion, medium dice
- 1 ounce carrot, medium dice
- 1 ounce celery, medium dice
- 4 ounces tomatoes (1 Roma tomato), concassé
- 7 cups brown stock, warmed
- 6 ounces brown roux (3 ounces flour + 2 1/4 ounces olive oil), cooled
- 1 pound cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
- Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium-high flame. Sauté onions, carrots and celery until golden.
2. Add tomatoes and sauté for few minutes or until slightly caramelized.
3. Add 6 cups stock and bring to boil. Lower heat to simmer.
4. Slowly whisk remaining 1 cup of stock to cooled roux.
5. Slowly add roux-stock mixture back into sauce.
6. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil over medium heat, add mushrooms and sweat until tender.
7. Add mushrooms to pot and simmer uncovered, about 30 minutes, or until reduced by half and nappé consistency is achieved.
8. Strain through chinois and return to pot.
9. Adjust seasoning to taste.
