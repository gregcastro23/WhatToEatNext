# CURRANT SCONES

**Yield:** 10 tiny scones

## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour (rice or Gluten-Free Flour Blend)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoon Maple crystals
- 2 tablespoon cold butter
- 1 egg
- 3-4 tablespoons heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon orange or lemon zest
- 1/4 cup currants

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F.
2. Sift and whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and maple crystals.
3. Cut butter into dry mix to form coarse crumbs.
4. In another bowl, beat egg, heavy cream, zest and currants together.
5. Add wet to dry until just combined.
6. Fold out onto table and form semi-flattened log.
7. Cut log into triangles and bake on parchment for 10 minutes.
8. Serve warm.
