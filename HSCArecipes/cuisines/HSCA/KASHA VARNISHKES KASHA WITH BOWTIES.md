# KASHA VARNISHKES (KASHA WITH BOWTIES)

**Yield:** 4-6 servings

## Ingredients

- 2-3 tablespoons butter OR olive oil
- 2 large onions, 1/2 inch dice
- 2 cups sliced mushrooms (button, or Portobello, or combination)
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- sea salt and black pepper, freshly ground
- 8 ounces bowtie pasta
- 1 large egg
- 1 cup kasha (medium or fine granulation)
- 2 cups mushroom OR chicken stock, heated
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
- 8 ounces smoked salmon, julienne

## Instructions

1. Melt butter or oil in 10-inch sauté pan. Over medium-high heat, cook onions and mushrooms until caramelized, adding garlic toward end of cooking. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer to large bowl.
2. In large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta until al dente. Drain pasta and toss with onion mixture.
3. In small bowl, beat egg and add kasha. Stir until grains are well coated.
4. Wipe out sauté pan and heat again over high heat. Transfer kasha mixture to pan and cook, stirring, until grains are toasted and separate, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Reduce heat to low and add stock. Stir, cover, and simmer until stock is absorbed and kasha is tender but not mushy, 7 to 8 minutes.
6. Stir in pasta mixture. Taste and adjust seasonings. Garnish with parsley and salmon.
