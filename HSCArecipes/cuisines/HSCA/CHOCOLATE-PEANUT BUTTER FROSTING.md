# CHOCOLATE-PEANUT BUTTER FROSTING

**Yield:** 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 12 ounces (1 1/2 cups) coconut milk
- 1 tablespoon + 2 teaspoons agar powder
- 8 ounces coconut cream (1 cup), room temperature
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 1 cup maple crystals
- 3/4 cup cocoa powder, sifted
- Pinch sea salt
- 6 ounces semi-sweet dark chocolate (1 cup), ground, and melted

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk and agar powder. Set aside for at least 5 minutes.
2. Prepare ice bath.
3. In food processor, combine coconut cream, peanut butter, maple crystals, cocoa powder, and salt. Blend until smooth.
4. Bring coconut milk-agar mixture to boil. Simmer until agar powder has dissolved, stirring constantly to avoid scorching.
5. Slowly and carefully add coconut milk-agar mixture and melted chocolate to processor with other ingredients and process to combine.
6. Using spatula, transfer contents from food processor to small bowl over ice bath until chilled.
