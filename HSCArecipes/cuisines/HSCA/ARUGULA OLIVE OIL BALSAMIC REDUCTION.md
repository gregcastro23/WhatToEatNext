# ARUGULA, OLIVE OIL, BALSAMIC REDUCTION

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 2 cups balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 8 ounces arugula
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 pound Reggiano parmesan, grated or shaved (optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine balsamic vinegar and vanilla in medium pan. Bring mixture to simmer, and cook down to approximately 1/2 cup or until mixture thickens enough to coat back of spoon. Set mixture aside to cool.
2. Toss arugula, oil, and salt together in bowl. Drizzle balsamic reduction to taste over greens. Add Reggiano parmesan, if using.
