# CASHEW SOUR CREAM

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cashews, soaked 4 hours to overnight (discard soaking water)
- 1/4 cup lime juice (4 limes)
- 1/4 cup canola oil
- 1/2 teaspoon or more sea salt to taste
- 2 scallions (white part only), chopped
- 2 teaspoons rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup water
- Sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in food processor until smooth and creamy. Season to taste.
