# KIDNEY BEAN SALAD

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** approximately 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup kidney beans, soaked overnight, drained and rinsed
- 3 cups filtered water
- 1 pinch of sea salt
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
- 1/2 to 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 3 red radishes, small dice
- 3 scallions, thin bias cut
- 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Combine beans with water and bring to boil over high heat. Add pinch of sea salt.
2. Lower heat and simmer until beans are tender (about 45 to 60 minutes). Drain beans.
3. Whisk together mustard, lime juice, apple cider vinegar, and salt. Slowly whisk in olive oil. Pour dressing over warm beans.
4. When beans have cooled, toss together with radishes, scallions and parsley.
