# Massaged Kale Salad with Roasted Chickpeas and Pickled Red Onion

*(Fall/Winter)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup chickpeas, soaked overnight, drained, and rinsed
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon ground fennel
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
- Pinch red pepper flakes
- Additional salt to taste
- 4 ounces red onion (1 small), 1/8-inch rounds
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 1/3 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon agave syrup
- 1 pound Lacinato kale (1 bunch), stemmed, chiffonade
- 1/3 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup pitted Kalamata olives, coarsely chopped

## Instructions

1. Combine chickpeas in pressure cooker with salt, bay leaf, and thyme sprigs. Add water to cover by 2 inches. Bring pot to pressure, lower heat, and cook for 15 minutes. Release pressure by running cold water over lid. Drain beans.
2. Heat oven to 350°F. Combine and toss chickpeas in medium bowl with olive oil, fennel, cumin, paprika, pepper flakes, and additional salt to taste.
3. Transfer chickpeas to parchment-lined half sheet pan and roast until crispy, around 15 minutes. Set aside.
4. Combine red onions in medium bowl with boiling water and let sit 5-10 minutes. Drain. Add vinegar, salt, and agave. Mix to combine.
5. Combine kale in medium bowl with salt, lemon zest and lemon juice. Massage with gloved hands until moisture is released and kale begins to soften in texture.
6. Mix kale with chickpeas and olives. Garnish with pickled onions.
