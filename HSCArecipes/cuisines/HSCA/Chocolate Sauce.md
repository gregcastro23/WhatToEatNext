# Chocolate Sauce

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. In double boiler (bowl set over simmering water), melt chocolate.
2. In separate pot, bring almond milk to simmer, and turn off heat.
3. Slowly whisk just enough almond milk into melted chocolate to create a smooth and pourable sauce.
