# BABY BOK CHOY AND RED CABBAGE SLAW (FOR SEAFOOD SAUSAGE)

**Yield:** 8 cups (sixteen 1/2-cup servings)

## Ingredients

- 2 small heads baby bok choy (8 ounces), leaves separated from stalks
- 1/4 head red cabbage (12 ounces) shredded
- 1 carrot (6 ounces), cut into 4-inch long, thin matchsticks
- 6 scallions, trimmed and cut into thin, 1-inch long diagonals
- 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup brown rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1/4 cup agave
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Pinch of ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Blanch bok choy stalks in small pot of lightly salted, boiling water for 30 seconds. Drain and shock in ice water. Cut into 1/2-inch diagonals. Blanch leaves for 10 seconds. Drain and shock in ice water. Wring out excess water from leaves and finely slice.
2. Combine bok choy stems in bowl with cabbage, carrots, and scallions.
3. Whisk dressing ingredients together and combine with slaw.
4. Arrange 1 cup slaw mixture in middle of large plate.
5. Top with 2 pieces of Seafood Sausage and dollop of Honey-Mustard Yogurt.
