# Chocolate Bark

**Yield:** 20 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 cups ground bittersweet chocolate (10 oz chocolate ground in food processor = about 2 cups)
- 1/4 cup sliced almonds, toasted
- 3 tablespoons dried cranberries
- 2 tablespoons cacao nibs
- Sprinkle of coarsely ground sea salt

## Instructions

1. Line half-sheet pan with parchment paper. Prepare ice bath in small bowl. Have offset spatula and candy thermometer at the ready.
2. Bring 4 cups water to simmer in 2-quart pot.
3. Pour 1 1/2 cups ground chocolate in small bowl. Set bowl over simmering water and whisk chocolate until completely melted. Remove bowl from heat and take temperature of melted chocolate. Temperature should not exceed 125°F. Between 110 and 125°F is best.
4. When chocolate is in 110 and 125°F range, whisk in remaining 1/2 cup of ground chocolate. Cool chocolate to 85°F, transferring bowl to ice bath, if necessary.
5. Transfer bowl atop simmering water again, and whisk chocolate until it reaches temperature between 87 and 91°F degrees (be careful as temperature rises quickly). If temperature goes too high, again use ice bath to cool it quickly.
6. Pour chocolate on parchment-lined half-sheet pan. Spread with offset spatula to 1/8-inch thickness.
7. Sprinkle nuts, dried fruit, cacao nibs, and salt over surface.
8. Cool bark in refrigerator and break into pieces.
