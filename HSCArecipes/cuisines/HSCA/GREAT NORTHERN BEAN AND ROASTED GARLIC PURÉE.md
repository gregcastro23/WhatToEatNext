# GREAT NORTHERN BEAN AND ROASTED GARLIC PURÉE

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Great Northern beans, soaked overnight, drained, and rinsed
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 2 heads garlic, roasted and paste extracted
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (1/2 ounce)
- 1 tablespoon chopped dill (1/2 ounce)
- Sea salt and pepper to taste
- 1 whole wheat baguette, sliced and oven toasted

## Instructions

1. Combine beans in pressure cooker with salt, thyme, and bay leaf. Add enough water to cover by 1 inch. Cover and bring to full pressure. Lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Release pressure by running cold water over lid and drain beans, reserving cooking liquid.
2. Discard thyme stems and bay leaf and transfer beans to food processor.
3. Add lemon, garlic, and olive oil. Puree until smooth, adding bean liquid as necessary to form smooth, thick purée.
4. Transfer to bowl season to taste and fold in parsley, dill, salt, and pepper. Serve on toasted baguette.
