# SAUTÉED CHICKEN LIVERS WITH ONION, APPLE, AND FRISÉE

**Yield:** 4-6 servings

## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 pound red onion (3 medium), sauté slice
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- 16 chicken livers, cleaned, rinsed, patted dry
- Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (½ lemon)
- 2 apples (unpeeled), cored, quartered, sliced
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 pound frisée, bite-size pieces
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Heat 3 tablespoons oil in large sauté pan over medium heat. Sweat onions until translucent. Add vinegar and raise heat slightly. Continue to cook until onions begin to brown and caramelize, adding a bit of water if onions begin to stick to pan. When onions are tender and browned, add salt and pepper. Remove onions from pan and set aside.
2. Season livers with salt and pepper. Add remaining 2 tablespoons oil to same pan and heat over medium-high flame. Add livers, making sure not to crowd pan. Sauté livers about 2 minutes on each side. When livers are done, add brandy to pan, and swirl to combine.
3. Transfer cooked livers to paper-lined sheet tray to drain.
4. Add onions back to pan, followed by livers.
5. Toss lemon juice in bowl with apples, salt, and pepper. Add frisée and set aside.
6. In separate small bowl, combine vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper. Slowly whisk in oil until dressing has emulsified.
7. Toss salad with dressing. Serve with caramelized onions and liver.
