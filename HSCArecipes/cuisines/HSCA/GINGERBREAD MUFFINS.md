# GINGERBREAD MUFFINS

**Yield:** 16 mini muffins

## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 2 tablespoons light OR dark corn syrup
- 1 tablespoon ginger juice
- 1 1/4 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground ginger
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- pinch sea salt
- 2 large eggs, lightly beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F (300° F in convection oven). Prepare miniature muffin tins with muffin papers. Place muffin tins on sheet pan and set aside.
2. In small saucepan, on low flame, heat and whisk together butter, molasses, and corn syrup until mixed and butter is melted. Add ginger juice and set aside.
3. In medium bowl, sift together flour, sugars, ground ginger, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt. Use whisk to incorporate ingredients.
4. Pour melted butter, molasses, corn syrup, and ginger juice into bowl with sifted dry ingredients. Stir to combine.
5. Pour batter into electric mixer, add eggs, and beat at medium speed for 1 1/2-2 minutes.
6. Using small ice cream scoop, fill muffin papers half way. Bake 10-12 minutes. Gently remove from pan. Cool completely.
