# GLUTEN-FREE CRISP GINGER COOKIE

**Yield:** approximately 16 2-inch cookies

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup almond meal, sifted
- 3 tablespoons sorghum flour, sifted
- 1 tablespoon arrowroot flour
- 1 teaspoon ginger powder
- Pinch ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- 2 tablespoons rice syrup
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 tablespoons water
- Pinch sea salt
- 16 sliced almonds for garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line 3 half-sheet trays with Silpats.
2. In medium bowl whisk almond meal, sorghum flour, arrowroot flour, ground ginger, and black pepper.
3. In separate small bowl, whisk together oil, rice syrup, maple syrup, vanilla, water, and salt.
4. Pour syrup mixture into nut/flour mixture. Batter should be consistency of heavy cream. If it is too thick, add more water, 1 tablespoon at a time, until you have proper consistency.
5. Using teaspoon measure, drop batter onto sheets (5-6 cookies a sheet). Decorate each cookie with one sliced almond. Bake 15-20 minutes or until cookies are golden. Cool before removing from sheet pan.
