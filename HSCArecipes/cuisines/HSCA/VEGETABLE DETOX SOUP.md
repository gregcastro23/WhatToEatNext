# VEGETABLE DETOX SOUP

**Yield:** 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 8 cups vegetable stock or filtered water
- 1/2 cup tomato paste
- 3 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
- 2 cup green beans, washed, trimmed, cut into 1-inch lengths
- 2 celery stalks, thinly sliced
- 2 medium zucchini, thinly sliced in 1/4 moons
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 bunch parsley, finely chopped
- 1/4 bunch dill, roughly chopped
- 2 scallions, cleaned and sliced on the bias to 1/8 inch

## Instructions

1. Bring stock or water and tomato paste to boil. Blend with wire whisk and skim.
2. Reduce heat, add garlic, beans, celery, and zucchini, and salt. Simmer until they are just tender and still green, about 10 minutes.
3. Stir in parsley, dill and scallions.
4. Serve in individual bowls with 1 teaspoon of Horseradish-Lemon condiment each.
