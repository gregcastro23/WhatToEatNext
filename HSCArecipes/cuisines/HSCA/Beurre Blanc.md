# Beurre Blanc

**Yield:** 1 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
- 3 ounces shallots (1 large), minced
- 4 ounces (1/2 cup) heavy cream
- 1/2 pound unsalted butter (2 sticks), chilled, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
- Sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In 8-inch sauté pan over medium flame, reduce vinegar with shallots until almost dry (au sec).
2. Add heavy cream to pan and reduce until mixture thickens slightly. Reduce heat to low.
3. Add butter, whisking in a few pieces at a time.
4. When all butter is incorporated, season to taste.
5. Strain sauce through chinois. Re-heat carefully over very low flame.
