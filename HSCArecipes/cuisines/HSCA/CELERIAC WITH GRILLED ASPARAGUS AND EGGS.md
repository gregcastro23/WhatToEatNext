# CELERIAC WITH GRILLED ASPARAGUS AND EGGS

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 cups water
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 pounds celeriac (1 large), peeled, thin half-moon slices
- 1 tablespoon brown rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup rice syrup
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 7 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 large egg
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 6 ounces baby spinach
- 3 tablespoons hazelnuts, toasted, chopped

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, bring water to boil with salt. Add celeriac, vinegar, and rice syrup. Lower heat and simmer until celeriac is just tender. Transfer celeriac with slotted spoon to medium bowl.
2. Add lemon juice and 6 tablespoons olive oil to pot with cooking liquids. Increase heat to medium-high and reduce liquid until syrupy in texture. Season with salt and pepper and pour half of reduced cooking liquid over celeriac. Set aside.
3. Combine egg with one-inch water to cover in 2 1/2 quart pot. Cover pot and bring to boil. Remove pot from heat and allow egg to remain in hot water for about 8 minutes. Shock egg in cold water. When cool enough to handle, peel and grate egg through a grater or sieve. Set aside.
4. Combine asparagus in bowl with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix to combine.
5. Heat cast iron griddle grill until hot. Grill asparagus until slightly charred but still firm in texture. Toss with remaining half of reduced cooking liquid and set aside.
6. Arrange spinach on plate. Arrange bundle of celeriac next to bundle of asparagus. Garnish with grated eggs and hazelnuts.
