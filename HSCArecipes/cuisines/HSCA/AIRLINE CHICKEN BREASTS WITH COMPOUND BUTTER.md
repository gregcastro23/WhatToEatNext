# AIRLINE CHICKEN BREASTS WITH COMPOUND BUTTER

**Yield:** 4 breasts

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds airline chicken breasts (4), skin intact
- 1 quart water
- 1 tablespoon sea salt
- 4 tablespoons butter, softened
- 3 tablespoons chopped garlic (3 large cloves garlic)
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice (1 1/2 lemons)
- 1 tablespoon chopped basil (1/4 ounce)
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons ghee

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken breasts in medium bowl with water and salt to brine. Refrigerate one hour. Remove breasts from water and dry well.
2. Combine butter, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, and basil in food processor. Process until ingredients are well incorporated and butter is smooth.
3. Remove compound butter mixture from processor and divide into 4 equal portions. Set aside.
4. Preheat convection oven to 400°F.
5. Gently separate skin from breast, taking care not to tear it. Spread compound butter evenly between skin and breast. Season skin with salt and pepper.
6. Heat ghee in 12-inch sauté pan. Place breasts skin side down in pan. Sear over medium-high heat to caramelize skin.
7. Transfer pan to oven and roast breasts for 15-20 minutes or until juices run clear. Do not turn chicken over while cooking. Serve skin side up.
