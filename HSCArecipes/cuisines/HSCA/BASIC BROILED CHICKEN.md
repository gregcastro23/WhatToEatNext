# BASIC BROILED CHICKEN

**Yield:** 1/2 chicken (2 servings)

## Ingredients

- 1/2 medium chicken (1 1/2 pounds)
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt or more to taste
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat broiler 20 minutes prior to using. Adjust rack to lowest setting.
2. Rub both skin side and inside of chicken with olive oil, salt, and fresh ground black pepper.
3. Heat 10-inch sauté pan over medium flame and place chicken, skin side down, in pan. Do not add any extra oil to pan. Transfer chicken to broiler.
4. Broil chicken skin side down for 15 minutes. Carefully flip chicken skin side up, and continue to broil for another 20 minutes. Depending on size of chicken, full cooking may need to be completed in preheated convection oven at 400°F for another 10-15 minutes.
