# COCONUT-CASHEW FROSTING

**Yield:** 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 37 ounces (4 1/2 cups) coconut milk, room temperature
- 9 tablespoons agar flakes
- Pinch sea salt
- 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 3 tablespoons cashew butter
- 1/2 teaspoon coconut extract

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk and agar flakes. Let sit for 10 minutes. Add salt, vanilla, and maple syrup.
2. Over medium-high heat, bring coconut milk mixture to boil, stirring often, while scraping agar from sides and bottom of pot.
3. Reduce heat to very low and simmer, partially covered, stirring and scraping often, until agar is dissolved, about 15 to 20 minutes.
4. Pour coconut mixture into shallow pan and chill until set (about 15 minutes in freezer or 30-40 minutes in refrigerator).
5. In food processor, combine coconut milk-agar mixture with cashew butter and coconut extract. Process until very smooth.
