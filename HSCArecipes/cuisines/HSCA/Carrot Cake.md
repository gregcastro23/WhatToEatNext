# Carrot Cake

**Yield:** 16 mini muffins

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 3/4 cup grated carrot
- 8 tablespoons melted butter
- 1/3 cup (4 oz.) of crushed pineapple (drained and squeezed to extract all liquid)
- 1 1/2 teaspoon hot water
- 2 tablespoons raisins
- 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F (300° F in convection oven). Prepare miniature muffin tins with muffin papers.
2. Whisk dry ingredients together in large bowl.
3. Whisk together wet ingredients. Add raisins and walnuts.
4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix. Using 1-ounce ice cream scoop, fill muffin papers 2/3 full and bake for 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
