# Orange Cake

**Yield:** Two 8-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
- 1 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 2/3 cup almond milk, room temperature
- 1/4 cup orange juice (1 orange)
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 teaspoons orange extract
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon orange zest (1 orange)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection). Oil and flour two 8-inch cake pans and line bottoms with parchment.
2. Sift together dry ingredients in medium bowl and whisk together to combine. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together wet ingredients and pour into dry. Mix well.
4. Pour batter into prepared pans.
5. Bake for about 15-20 minutes until cakes are springy to touch and pull away from sides of pan.
6. Cool in pans on wire rack for 10-15 minutes. Remove from pans and completely cool on rack.
