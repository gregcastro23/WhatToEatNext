# CHOCOLATE GELATO

**Yield:** 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 4 1/2 cups organic milk
- 2/3 cup organic heavy cream
- 1 1/2 cups maple crystals, divided
- 2 cups cocoa powder
- 4 ounces bittersweet chocolate
- 8 large egg yolks
- 1/4 cup Kahlua
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- Pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. Prepare ice bath.
2. In 1-gallon pot, heat milk, heavy cream, and 3/4 cup of maple crystals until crystals dissolve and milk starts to simmer.
3. Whisk in cocoa powder and bittersweet chocolate. Set aside.
4. Combine egg yolks and remaining 3/4 cup of maple crystals in electric mixer fitted with paddle attachment. Beat on high speed for 3-5 minutes or until mixtures lightens in color and mixture thickens.
5. Lower speed on mixer and slowly pour chocolate-cream mixture into egg mixture.
6. Transfer combined mixtures to 2 1/2 quart saucepan and simmer over low heat stirring constantly until thickened (candy thermometer should read at about 180 degrees). Do not bring mixture to boil.
7. Strain mixture into medium bowl through nut bag or chinois lined with double layer of moistened cheesecloth, and then stir in Kahlua, vanilla, and salt.
8. Transfer mixture to ice bath and cool to room temperature.
9. Pour mixture into ice cream machine and churn until firm and scoop-able.
