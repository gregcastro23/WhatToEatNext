# CRANBERRY WALNUT SCONES

**Yield:** Yield: approximately 12 scones

## Ingredients

- 1 cup unbleached white all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1/3 cup maple crystals
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2/3 cup dried cranberries, chopped
- 1/3 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 teaspoon finely grated orange zest
- 3/4 cup almond milk soured with 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger juice
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon almond milk for brushing
- 1 tablespoon maple crystals

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line half-sheet pan with parchment.
2. In medium bowl, stir together flours, maple crystals, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. With pastry cutter, cut butter into dry ingredients until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add cranberries, nuts, and zest.
4. Make well in center and gradually stir in milk, ginger juice, and maple syrup just to combine. Do not over mix; dough should be sticky and difficult to work with.
5. On lightly floured surface, pat or roll dough to 1/2 inch thickness. Cut into shapes and place on sheet pan. Brush tops with milk and sprinkle with maple crystals.
6. Bake 12-15 minutes or until golden brown and firm to touch. Remove from pan to cool.
