# Spiced Carrot Cake

**Yield:** Two 8-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 3 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- 2 1/2 cups maple crystals
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 cup apple sauce
- 4 large eggs, room temperature
- 1 1/2 cups melted coconut oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 12 ounces carrots, finely shredded (2 cups)
- 1 1/2 cups walnuts, lightly toasted and coarsely chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection). Oil and flour two 8-inch cake pans and line bottoms with parchment.
2. Sift together dry ingredients in large bowl and whisk together to combine. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together wet ingredients and pour into dry. Mix well. Add additional ingredients and mix to combine.
4. Pour batter into prepared pans.
5. Bake for about 45-50 minutes until cakes are springy to touch and pull away from sides of pans.
6. Cool in pans on wire rack for about 10-15 minutes. Remove from pans and allow to completely cool on rack.
