# THREE-CITRUS MARINADE

**Yield:** approximately 2 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
- 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced
- 2 teaspoons coarsely ground black pepper
- 5 bay leaves, crushed
- 5 tablespoons chopped cilantro

## Instructions

1. Combine lime juice, lemon juice, orange juice, olive oil, garlic, black pepper, bay leaves and cilantro in blender.
2. Let stand 30 minutes to allow flavors to develop before using.
