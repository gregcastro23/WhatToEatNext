# Cream of Mushroom Soup

*(Fall/Winter)*

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter OR olive oil
- 1 cup sliced shallots
- 4 tablespoons whole-wheat flour
- 4 cups stock
- 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sherry vinegar, or more to taste
- Black pepper to taste
- Parsley sprigs for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat butter or olive oil. Add shallots and sauté until translucent. Add flour; stir 4-5 minutes, until toasted.
2. Add stock 1 cup at a time, whisking to blend. Bring soup to simmer, add salt.
3. Add mushrooms to soup. Simmer 30 minutes.
4. Puree soup in blender. Add sherry and black pepper. Adjust seasonings. Serve garnished with parsley sprig.
