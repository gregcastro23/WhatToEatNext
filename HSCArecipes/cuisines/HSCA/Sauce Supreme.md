# Sauce Supreme

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Velouté Sauce (reserved from recipe above)
- 4 ounces (1/2 cup) heavy cream

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat Velouté Sauce over low flame and simmer 2-3 minutes or until warmed through.
2. Slowly whisk in heavy cream, and mix until incorporated.
3. Adjust seasoning to taste.
