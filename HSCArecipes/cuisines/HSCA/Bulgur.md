# Bulgur

## Ingredients

- 1 cup bulgur
- 1 1/4 cups water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Dry roast bulgur in small sauté pan.
2. Bring water and salt to boil in small saucepot, add bulgur, cover and bring back to a rolling boil for 1 minute.
3. Remove pot from heat. Let steam, covered, 45 minutes.
