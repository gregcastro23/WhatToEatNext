# Fresh Herb Marinade

**Yield:** approximately 1 1/3 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 9 sprigs fresh rosemary, bruised
- 1 bunch parsley, minced
- black pepper, freshly ground
- 1 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In small bowl, combine all ingredients.
2. Let vegetables soak in marinade for 20 to 30 minutes before grilling.
