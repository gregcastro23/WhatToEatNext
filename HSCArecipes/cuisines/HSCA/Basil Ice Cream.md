# Basil Ice Cream

**Yield:** 2 cups (8 servings)

## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 4 ounces fresh basil, stemmed
- 1 (14 oz.) can coconut milk
- 6 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. Prepare ice bath. Bring water to boil. Add salt. Blanch basil for 2-3 seconds then shock in ice bath. Drain basil and completely wring water from it.
2. Combine coconut milk, maple syrup, rice syrup, vanilla extract, lemon zest, salt and basil in blender.
3. Process mixture until well emulsified.
4. Pour mixture into ice cream machine and churn until creamy and somewhat firm.
