# Long Grain Brown Rice

## Ingredients

- 1 cup long grain brown rice, washed
- 1 3/4 cups boiling water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Dry roast until rice in small pot until dry and slightly aromatic.
2. Add boiling water and salt to rice. Cover. Bring back to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes or until all water is absorbed. Remove pot from heat and let steam 10 to 15 minutes covered.
