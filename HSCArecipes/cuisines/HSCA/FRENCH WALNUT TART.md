# FRENCH WALNUT TART

**Yield:** 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 2 unbaked 9-inch pie doughs
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1/3 cup rice syrup OR 1/2 cup honey
- 2 tablespoons barley malt syrup
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
- pinch sea salt
- 1 cup almond milk
- 3 1/2 cups walnuts, toasted and coarsely chopped
- 2 tablespoons cashew butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon orange extract
- 1 tablespoon brandy (optional)
- 1 tablespoon arrowroot dissolved in 2 tablespoons cold water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Roll out first dough and transfer to tart pan; chill for 15 minutes. Trim edges of rolled dough, allowing 1-inch overhang. Cover and chill for 30 minutes more. Roll out other half of dough and transfer to sheet pan. Cover and chill until ready to use.
3. In one-gallon pot, combine sweeteners. Bring to boil, lower heat and add lemon juice, salt, almond milk, and walnuts.
4. Whisk in nut butter, extracts, and brandy. Add arrowroot slurry. Mix on medium low heat until mixture thickens slightly. Remove from heat and cool.
5. Pour walnut mixture into tart shell. Place top dough over filling, trim and seal edges. Cut a few steam vents.
6. Bake for 30-40 minutes until crust is firm and golden.
7. Cool before serving (about 25 minutes).
