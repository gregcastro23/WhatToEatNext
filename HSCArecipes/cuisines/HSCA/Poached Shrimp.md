# Poached Shrimp

**Yield:** 25-30 pieces

## Ingredients

- 25-30 pieces peeled and deveined shrimp, tails on
- 2 gallons water
- Cocktail Sauce (see recipe below)

## Instructions

1. Bring the water to a boil and add shrimp. Poach for 4-5 minutes and remove with spider.
2. Cool shrimp in ice bath. Remove and place on serving platter with Cocktail Sauce.
