# Flourless Chocolate Cake

**Yield:** 9-inch cake

## Ingredients

- 1 pound bittersweet chocolate, chopped
- 1 1/2 cups date sugar, ground to fine powder
- 1/2 cup boiling water mixed 1 heaping tablespoon instant coffee
- 2 sticks butter (8 ounces), room temperature, cut into pieces
- 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 6 eggs, room temperature
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Heat convection oven to 325°F. Brush 9-inch spring-form pan with oil. Line bottom with parchment paper and outside, bottom of pan with tin foil.
2. Grind chocolate in food processor to fine meal, add date sugar, and continue to grind until well combined.
3. Slowly pour hot coffee into food processor through feeder tube and pulse until chocolate is melted.
4. Add butter pieces and cocoa powder. Pulse to combine. Add eggs, salt, and vanilla. Process until smooth. Batter should be pourable and creamy.
5. Pour batter into spring-form pan. Bake on half-sheet pan as a little batter may seep out.
6. Bake about 45 minutes or until puffed and lightly cracked. Use toothpick to check for doneness.
