# Mocha Frosting

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon instant coffee
- Pinch sea salt
- 1/2 cup boiling water
- 12 ounces (1 1/2 cups) coconut milk, room temperature
- 3 tablespoons agar powder
- 8 ounces coconut cream (1 cup), room temperature
- 1 cup cashew butter
- 1 cup maple crystals
- 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate (1 cup), melted

## Instructions

1. Prepare coffee by combining instant coffee, salt, and boiling water. Set aside.
2. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk, prepared coffee, agar powder, and coconut cream. Set aside for 5 minutes.
3. Bring coconut milk-coffee mixture to boil. Lower heat and simmer until all agar powder has dissolved. Transfer to ice bath.
4. In food processor combine cashew butter and maple crystals. Add coconut-coffee mixture. Blend well until smooth.
5. Add melted chocolate and mix to combine. Transfer mixture to small bowl.
6. Place bowl over ice bath until mixture is firm.
7. Blend frosting in food processor until smooth and pipe-able.
