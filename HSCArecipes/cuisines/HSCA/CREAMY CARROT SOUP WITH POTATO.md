# CREAMY CARROT SOUP WITH POTATO

**Yield:** ___ cups, ___ 1/2 cup servings

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 large yellow onion (___ ounces), medium dice
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 6 large carrots (___ pound), peeled, 1/2-inch rounds
- 8 cups stock
- 1/2 medium potato (___ ounces), peeled, large dice
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or more to taste
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ginger juice, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon dill, chopped for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil over medium-low heat. Add onions and salt. Sweat until onions are softened (about 5-8 minutes). Stir often to prevent browning.
2. Add carrots, cover pot and sweat carrots over low heat for 5-6 minutes. Stir to prevent browning.
3. Add stock and potatoes to pot. Raise heat and bring to boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer 25 minutes, covered, until carrots are very tender.
4. Blend soup until creamy, adding additional stock to achieve desired consistency.
5. Add lemon and ginger juice. Readjust seasonings.
6. Garnish with dill and serve.
