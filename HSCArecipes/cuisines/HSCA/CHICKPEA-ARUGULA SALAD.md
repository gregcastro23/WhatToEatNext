# CHICKPEA-ARUGULA SALAD

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** approximately 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup chickpeas, soaked overnight
- 1 quart water
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 cup Sesame-Garlic Dressing (see recipe below)
- 1/2 bunch arugula, cleaned and chopped into bite sized pieces (5 cups)
- 1 cup olives, pitted and chopped
- 2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
- 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
- sea salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine chickpeas, water, cumin, and salt in stockpot.
2. Bring beans to simmer, lower heat, and cook 45-60 minutes, adding water as needed, until beans are tender. Drain beans.
3. While beans are still warm, toss with dressing. (recipe below).
4. Let beans cool to room temperature and, right before service, add arugula, olives, parsley, and basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
