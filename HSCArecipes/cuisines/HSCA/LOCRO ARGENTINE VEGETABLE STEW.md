# LOCRO (ARGENTINE VEGETABLE STEW)

**Yield:** 3 to 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup dried navy beans, soaked
- 2 tablespoons whole wheat berries, soaked
- 2 tablespoons barley, soaked
- 3 cups water + 1 cup water
- 1/3 pound butternut squash, peeled and large diced
- 1/4 pound potato, peeled and large diced
- 1 small carrot, peeled and large diced
- 1 yam OR sweet potato, peeled and large diced
- bay leaves
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 ear corn, kernels cut from cob
- 2 whole scallions, chopped, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Drain beans, wheat berries, and barley. In 4-quart pot, combine beans, wheat berries, and barley with 3 cups water. Bring to boil, reduce heat, and simmer, covered, for 30 to 40 minutes.
2. Add vegetables to simmering beans and grains. Add remaining 1 cup water, bay leaves and salt. Continue simmering for 1 hour, covered, over low heat. Stir occasionally, and add more water if needed. Vegetables should be soft; squash will disintegrate, making thick stew. Add corn and cook another 10 minutes.
3. Serve hot, topped with Sofrito Topping (recipe below) and chopped scallions.
