# Vegan Cashew Cream

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cashews, soaked overnight
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons brown rice vinegar
- Pinch salt
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/2 cup water (or more if necessary)

## Instructions

1. Combine cashews, vanilla, brown rice vinegar, salt, and maple syrup in Vitamix with wand attachment. Start blending on low speed, and slowly raise speed while stirring with wand.
2. When mixture is starting to smooth, add water, and continue processing. When mixture is almost smooth, turn Vitamix up to highest setting. Finished cream should be thick and pipe-able.
