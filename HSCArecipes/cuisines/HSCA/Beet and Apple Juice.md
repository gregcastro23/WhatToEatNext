# Beet and Apple Juice

**Yield:** 2 quarts

## Ingredients

- 3 large beets, washed and trimmed
- 4 granny smith apples, washed
- 1/2 lemon, peeled

## Instructions

1. Cut produce to fit juicer feed tube.
2. Alternate juicing beets and apples, finishing with beets. Refrigerate juice 5-10 minutes for foam to settle.
3. Scrape off any excess foam and serve immediately.
