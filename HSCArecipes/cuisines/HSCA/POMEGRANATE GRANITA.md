# POMEGRANATE GRANITA

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 1 quart pomegranate juice
- 1 cup orange juice (4 oranges)
- 1 tablespoon maple crystals

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, over low flame, simmer pomegranate and orange juices with maple crystals until crystals dissolve.
2. Pour mixture in half hotel pan (mixture should be 1/4 inch up side of pan) and transfer to freezer for about 1 hour.
3. Remove pan from freezer and use fork to scrape any ice crystals that have formed on side or bottom of pan. Return to freezer and repeat scraping every 30 minutes for 3-4 hours.
4. Once mixture is thoroughly frozen, scrape once more with fork, returning granita to 'dry' in freezer for another half hour before serving.
