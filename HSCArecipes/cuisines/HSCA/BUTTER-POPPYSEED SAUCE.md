# BUTTER-POPPYSEED SAUCE

## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 cup ground poppy seeds

## Instructions

1. Heat butter in 8-inch sauté pan and stir in ground poppy seeds.
2. As soon as ravioli are cooked, remove them with slotted spoon to pre-warmed plates. Pour poppy seed butter over them and sprinkle with grated parmesan.
