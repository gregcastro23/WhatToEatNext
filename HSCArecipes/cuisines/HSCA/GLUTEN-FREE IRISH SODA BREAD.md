# GLUTEN-FREE IRISH SODA BREAD

**Yield:** 8 muffin-size quick breads

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Gluten-Free Flour Blend (see previous recipe)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup maple crystals
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 egg
- 1 1/4 cups buttermilk, chilled
- 1/4 cup raisins

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375° F.
2. In large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and maple crystals.
3. In separate bowl, combine butter, eggs, and buttermilk and beat to combine well. Add raisins.
4. Create well in center of dry ingredients. Add buttermilk and egg mixture and mix gently to combine. Transfer dough to muffin molds.
5. Bake bread about 30-40 minutes, or until golden in color and toothpick inserted in quick bread comes out clean. Remove from oven, and allow bread to cool in muffin forms for 10 minutes before inverting finished bread onto cooling rack.
