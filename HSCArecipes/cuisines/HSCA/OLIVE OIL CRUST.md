# OLIVE OIL CRUST

**Yield:** One 9-inch crust

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 3/4 cup unbleached white flour
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder (optional)
- 1/4 cup ice cold olive oil
- 1/4-1/2 cup ice cold filtered water

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, sift together flour, salt and baking powder.
2. Cut oil into flour mixture all at once but do not mix. Toss flour around lightly coating fat to create little balls of flour coated fat. A light touch is important here. Final blending will take place on pastry board in rolling.
3. Add just enough water (one tablespoon at a time) to form ball, being careful not over-mix and create too much gluten.
4. Flatten dough into disc, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 15 minutes to relax the dough. Roll to desired shape. Transfer rolled dough to pie or tart pan and chill for 30 minutes or more.
5. Heat oven to 375°F, fill crust and bake.
