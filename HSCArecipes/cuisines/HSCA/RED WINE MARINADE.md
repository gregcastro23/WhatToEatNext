# RED WINE MARINADE

**Yield:** 1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup red wine
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine wine, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper and thyme.
