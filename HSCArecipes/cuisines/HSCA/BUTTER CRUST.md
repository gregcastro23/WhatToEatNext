# BUTTER CRUST

**Yield:** One 9-inch crust

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 8 tablespoons cold, sweet butter
- 1/4-1/3 cup ice water

## Instructions

1. Sift then mix flour and salt into bowl. Using two knives or pastry cutter, cut butter into flour to form very coarse meal.
2. With rubber spatula, lightly toss flour while sprinkling water in mixture 1 tablespoon at a time until dough forms. Wrap in plastic and chill 20 to 30 minutes.
3. Roll on lightly floured parchment to desired shape.
