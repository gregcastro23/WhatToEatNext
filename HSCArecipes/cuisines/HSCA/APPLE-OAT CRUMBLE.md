# APPLE-OAT CRUMBLE

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** approximately 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 4 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, cut in into 1/8-inch slices
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons arrowroot powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 2/3 cup apple juice
- 2 cups rolled oats
- 1/2 cup brown rice flour
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 6 tablespoons melted coconut oil
- 6 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp sea salt

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F. While cutting apples, soak slices in acidulated water (juice of 1/2 lemon and 4 cups water).
2. In large bowl, whisk together salt, arrowroot, cinnamon, maple crystals, and apple juice. Drain apples, and combine with cinnamon-maple mixture. Transfer to 9-inch square pan.
3. In medium bowl combine oats, flour, and cinnamon. Whisk to combine.
4. In separate bowl whisk oil, syrup, and salt.
5. Pour wet into over dry and toss until mixture is completely coated.
6. Spread topping over apples. Bake 45 to 55 minutes until topping is golden and apples and filling is bubbly. Serve garnished with drizzle of pomegranate reduction.
