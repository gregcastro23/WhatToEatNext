# CHERMOULA SAUCE

**Yield:** 1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup loosely packed, stemmed cilantro (1 ounce)
- 2 cups loosely packed, stemmed parsley (1 3/4 ounces)
- 2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest (1 lemon)
- 1/2 cup lemon juice (4 lemons)
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic (1 large clove)
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- 2 teaspoons sweet paprika
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. In VitaMix, combine all ingredients until well blended.
