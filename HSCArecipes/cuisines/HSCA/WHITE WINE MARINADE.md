# WHITE WINE MARINADE

**Yield:** approximately 1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup white wine
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1 fresh lemon sliced OR zest of 1 orange
- 1 heaping tablespoon chopped fresh herbs OR 1 teaspoon dried herbs
- several parsley sprigs
- fresh black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients. This marinade can be used for most vegetables (use half amount of oil if using for fruit).
