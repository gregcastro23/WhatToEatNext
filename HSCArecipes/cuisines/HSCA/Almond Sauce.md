# Almond Sauce

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 head garlic
- 5 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 1/4 cup dry white wine
- 4 ounces shallots (2 medium), minced
- 1/3 cup blanched almonds, soaked 10 minutes in warm water and strained
- 3/4 cup vegetable stock
- Sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F.
2. Peel off papery outer layers from garlic head, cut off top third of head, rub cut surface with 1 tablespoon olive oil, and wrap garlic in aluminum foil. Roast garlic for 45-60 minutes or until cloves are soft. When cool enough to handle, extract paste from garlic head and set aside.
3. In 8-inch sauté pan, over medium-high flame, reduce white wine with shallots until almost dry (au sec).
4. In blender combine shallots, roasted garlic paste, almonds and vegetable stock. Through feed tube, slowly add remaining 1/4 cup of olive oil. Blend until creamy. Season to taste.
