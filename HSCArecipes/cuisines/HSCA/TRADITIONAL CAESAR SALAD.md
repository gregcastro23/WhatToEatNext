# TRADITIONAL CAESAR SALAD

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 large cloves garlic, halved lengthwise
- 8 ounces (1/2 loaf) whole grain bread, crust removed, medium dice
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- Poached garlic cloves (2) from crouton recipe
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 2 large anchovy fillets
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 large eggs, boiled for 3 minutes, cooled, peeled
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 1/2 pounds (2 small heads) romaine lettuce, bite-size pieces
- 2-3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (1/2 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F.
2. In 8-inch sauté pan, heat oil and garlic on low heat. Poach until garlic just begins to turn golden. Remove garlic and reserve for dressing.
3. Toss bread cubes in flavored oil and bake on half sheet pan in oven for about 10 minutes or until crisp. Set aside.
4. In blender, combine all dressing ingredients except for oil. Slowly drizzle oil into blender until dressing is creamy.
5. Combine dressing with romaine lettuce. Garnish with Parmesan and croutons.
