# SEAFOOD SAUSAGE

**Yield:** Eight 1/3-cup portions

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup minced shallots (2 1/2 ounces)
- 1/3 large leek (4 ounces), white portion small-diced, green portion discarded
- 12 ounces filet of sole
- 2 egg whites
- 1/4 ounce chives minced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 8 ounces scallops, small-diced
- 4 ounces shrimp, shelled, deveined, and small-diced
- Extra virgin olive oil for brushing
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon honey

## Instructions

1. In small sauté pan, heat oil, and add shallots and leeks. Sauté for 2 minutes or until translucent. Transfer to food processor.
2. Add sole to shallots and leeks in food processor and puree 30 seconds. Add egg whites, and process 15 seconds more. Scrape down bowl and process 15 seconds more.
3. Transfer mixture to bowl. Fold in chives, salt, and pepper scallops and shrimp. Divide mixture into eight 1/3-cup portions.
4. Form sausages 4 inches long and 1 1/2 inch thick. Roll each tightly first in parchment (8-inch x 6-inch sheets) and then in foil (8-inch x 6-inch sheets), twisting ends, and poach in simmering water for 9-10 minutes.
5. Unwrap sausage and cool slightly. Slice each sausage once on bias in middle.
6. Lightly brush non-stick pan with olive oil. Arrange sausages in pan so as not to crowd them. Brown them lightly on all sides. Serve with dollop of Honey-Mustard Yogurt and side of Baby Bok Choy and Red Cabbage Slaw (recipes following).
7. In small bowl, whisk all ingredients together.
