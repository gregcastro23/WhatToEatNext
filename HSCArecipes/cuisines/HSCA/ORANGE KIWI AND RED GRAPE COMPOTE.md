# ORANGE, KIWI, AND RED GRAPE COMPOTE

**Yield:** about 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup water
- 2 tablespoons rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon Grade A maple syrup
- 2 whole cardamom pods, crushed
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon vodka (optional)
- 4 large oranges, supremed (about 2 cups of slices)
- 3 kiwis, peeled and sliced (about 1 cup of slices)
- 1 cup seedless red grapes, sliced in half
- 2 teaspoons lime juice
- 2 tablespoons sliced almonds, toasted

## Instructions

1. In small saucepan, combine water, rice syrup, maple syrup, crushed cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and allspice. Whisk together and bring to boil. Reduce until mixture becomes thick and syrupy, about 3-4 minutes.
2. Add vanilla extract and vodka (if using) and continue to cook another 2 minutes. Remove from heat and remove cardamom pods and cinnamon stick.
3. In medium bowl, combine oranges, kiwis, and grapes. Combine syrup mixture with fruit and stir gently to combine. Taste and adjust seasonings, adding lime juice as needed.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour and up to 36 hours to allow flavors to combine. When ready to serve, sprinkle with toasted almonds.
