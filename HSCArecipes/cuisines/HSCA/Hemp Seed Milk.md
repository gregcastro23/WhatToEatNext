# Hemp Seed Milk

**Yield:** 8 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 cups hemp hearts (shelled hemp seeds)
- 6-8 cups filtered water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 Medjool dates, soaked overnight
- 1/4 teaspoon of sea salt

## Instructions

1. Put all ingredients into high-speed blender and blend until smooth.
2. Serve milk chilled or warmed.
