# RICE PILAF

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 10 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 pound onions, small dice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cups long grain brown rice
- 7 cups boiling vegetable stock
- 2 teaspoons salt
- Ground pepper, to taste
- 2 teaspoons thyme
- 1-2 bay leaves
- Fresh herbs for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil and sweat onions until translucent.
2. Add rice and stir to coat with oil.
3. Add stock, salt, pepper, and thyme. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 45 minutes or until all water is absorbed. Remove from heat and keep covered to steam for at least another 15 minutes. Garnish with fresh herbs.
