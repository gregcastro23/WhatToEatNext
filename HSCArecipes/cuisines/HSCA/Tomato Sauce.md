# Tomato Sauce

**Yield:** 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 2 (28 ounce) cans whole tomatoes (including juice)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 tablespoon agave syrup or more to taste
- Pinch red pepper flakes
- 4 basil leaves, torn (1/8 ounce)
- Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Strain whole tomatoes from juice. Reserve juice and set aside. Blend whole tomatoes in food processor.
2. In 12-inch sauté pan, heat oil over low flame. Add garlic and salt. Sweat until garlic softens (do not burn garlic).
3. Raise heat to medium-high.
4. Add pureed whole tomatoes to pan. Sauté tomatoes for 12-15 minutes.
5. Lower heat, add agave, red pepper flakes, and reserved tomato juice. Simmer until sauce thickens, about 10 minutes.
6. Add basil leaves. Simmer 2-3 minutes more. Adjust seasoning to taste with salt and pepper.
