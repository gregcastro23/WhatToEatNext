# PAN-FRIED MILLET CROQUETTES

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup millet, washed and drained
- 2 cups water
- large pinch salt
- 6 tablespoons breadcrumbs
- 1/2 bunch scallions (5 - 6), minced
- 1/3 bunch parsley, minced
- 1 tablespoon + 1 1/2 teaspoons shoyu
- cornmeal (for dredging)
- No-taste oil for frying

## Instructions

1. In sauce pot, dry roast millet over medium heat for 5 to 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
2. Add water and salt, cover, and bring to boil. Simmer 40 minutes or until water is absorbed.
3. Transfer millet to large bowl with breadcrumbs and let cool. Squeeze grain until sticky.
4. Add scallions, parsley, and shoyu to millet.
5. Using 1/4 cup measure, form croquettes. Mold croquettes with wet hands. Dredge in cornmeal.
6. Heat 1/2 inch of oil in large sauté pan. Prepare plate lined with paper towel for draining.
7. Fry croquettes until golden brown on each side. Flip once during cooking.
