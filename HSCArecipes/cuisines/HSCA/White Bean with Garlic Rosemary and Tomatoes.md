# White Bean with Garlic, Rosemary, and Tomatoes

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
- 5 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 5 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 teaspoon dry rosemary
- 1/4 teaspoon dry oregano
- 1 cup white beans, soaked overnight and drained
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 cups water
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200°F. Toss grape tomatoes in bowl with 2 tablespoons olive oil. Spread tomatoes on parchment-lined sheet tray and roast until well caramelized, an hour or more. Set aside.
2. While tomatoes are roasting, heat remaining 3 tablespoons olive oil over medium flame in pressure cooker. Add garlic. The moment garlic sizzles, add rosemary and oregano and stir. Add white beans, salt, and water.
3. Secure lid to pressure cooker, bring cooker up to pressure, lower heat, and cook beans for 25 minutes.
4. Bring down pressure by running cold water on lid of cooker. Drain beans, transfer to bowl and add roasted tomatoes. Season with additional salt and pepper, as needed.
