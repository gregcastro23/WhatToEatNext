# Chocolate Fondue

**Yield:** 3/4 cup (six 2-tablespoon servings)

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup cocoa
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 1/3 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/3 teaspoon orange extract
- 2-3 tablespoons soy milk

## Instructions

1. In small saucepan, combine cocoa, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and orange extract, stirring until well combined.
2. Cover pot and gently bring to simmer. Lower heat and cook for 1-2 minutes, being careful not to burn chocolate.
3. Remove from heat and stir in soy milk until sauce has nappé.
