# RAW JERUSALEM ARTICHOKE SALAD

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons chopped garlic (1 clove)
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice (1 1/2 lemons)
- 2 tablespoon rice syrup
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1/3 teaspoon sea salt
- 5 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 pound Jerusalem artichokes (6 large), peeled, matchsticks
- 7 ounces radishes (1 bunch), matchsticks
- 6 ounces chicory, bite-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Combine all dressing ingredients in blender until creamy.
2. Combine Jerusalem artichokes and radishes in small bowl with half the dressing. Marinate 30 minutes.
3. Dress chicory with remaining dressing.
4. Toss chicory with Jerusalem artichokes and radishes.
