# Skewered Fruit with Chocolate Fondue

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- Six 8-inch skewers
- 3 kiwi, peeled and cut into 12 (1/3-inch thick) rounds
- 12 (1/2-inch square) pineapple chunks
- 1 1/2 oranges, peeled and cut into 3 (1-inch thick) rounds, then cut in half again
- 12 strawberries, stemmed
- 2 bananas, peeled and cut into 12 (1/2-inch thick) diagonal slices
- Chocolate Fondue (recipe below)

## Instructions

1. Skewer fruit as follows: banana-kiwi-pineapple-strawberry-orange-strawberry-pineapple-kiwi-banana. Serve with Chocolate Fondue (recipe below).
