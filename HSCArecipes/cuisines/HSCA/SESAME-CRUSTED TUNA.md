# SESAME-CRUSTED TUNA

**Yield:** 4-6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 pound tuna, skin removed
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup white sesame seeds
- 1/4 cup black sesame seeds
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 2 tablespoons wasabi powder, mixed with water to form smooth paste

## Instructions

1. Season tuna with salt and pepper.
2. Combine sesame seeds and completely crust tuna.
3. Heat canola oil in sauté pan over medium heat.
4. Sear tuna on all sides, approximately 1-2 minutes each side.
5. Let tuna rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
6. Serve with wasabi and dipping sauce.
