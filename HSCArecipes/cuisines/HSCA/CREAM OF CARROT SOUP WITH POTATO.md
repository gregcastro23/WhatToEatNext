# CREAM OF CARROT SOUP WITH POTATO

**Yield:** 6 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 large onion (12 ounces), medium dice
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 pounds carrots, peeled, 1/2-inch rounds
- 5-6 cups stock
- 4 ounces potato (1/2 medium), peeled, medium dice
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ginger juice (1/2 ounce)
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill (1/2 ounce), for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil over medium flame. Add onions and salt. Sweat until onions are softened (about 5-8 minutes). Stir often to prevent browning.
2. Add carrots, cover pot, and sweat over low heat for 5-6 minutes. Stir to prevent browning.
3. Add stock and potatoes to pot. Raise heat and bring soup to boil. Reduce heat and simmer with lid ajar, 25 minutes, or until carrots and potatoes are very tender.
4. Blend soup until creamy, adding additional stock to achieve desired consistency.
5. Return soup to pot. Re-heat and season to taste with lemon and ginger juices.
6. Garnish with dill.
