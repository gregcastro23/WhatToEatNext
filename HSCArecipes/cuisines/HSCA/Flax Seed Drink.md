# Flax Seed Drink

*This drink contains phytoestrogens, omega-3 fatty acids, and is high in soluble fiber.*

## Ingredients

- 1-2 tablespoons organic flax seeds
- 1 cup filtered water

## Instructions

1. Grind flax seeds in spice grinder and mix with water. Drink immediately.
