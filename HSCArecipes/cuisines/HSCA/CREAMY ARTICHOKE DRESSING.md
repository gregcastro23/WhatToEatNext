# CREAMY ARTICHOKE DRESSING

**Yield:** 1 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 medium artichokes (1 pound)
- 2 tablespoons chopped garlic (2 large cloves)
- 1/4 cup lemon juice (3-4 lemons)
- 3 tablespoons rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon chopped chives (1/2 ounce)
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt or to taste
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 to 1/2 cup artichoke cooking liquid or vegetable stock

## Instructions

1. Arrange artichokes in steamer basket (see technique above) and steam until tender, about 20 minutes. Set artichokes aside to cool. Reserve any extra steaming liquid.
2. Remove leaves and choke from cooled artichokes, leaving just heart and stem. Peel outer layer of stem. Cut each artichoke heart into 4 pieces an remove any fibrous material. Blend artichoke hearts with garlic, lemon juice, rice syrup, chives, salt, pepper, and olive oil, adding artichoke water as necessary for desired creamy consistency.
