# Blackened Shrimp

**Yield:** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- Blackening Spice Mix (see recipe below)
- 12 large OR jumbo shrimp, shelled and deveined
- Skewers
- 1/3 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon ground thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon ground rosemary

## Instructions

1. In shallow pan or plate, distribute Blackening Spice Mix evenly.
2. Skewer shrimp, 3 to skewer.
3. Roll skewered shrimp in spice mix. Heat griddle grill.
4. Grill shrimp about 1 1/2-2 minutes on each side until shrimp is opaque.
5. Combine all ingredients for Blackening Spice Mix.
