# Tapioca Pudding

**Yield:** 6 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1/2 cup large pearl tapioca, soaked in 2 cups water for 30 minutes, drained
- 1 quart Rich Almond Milk
- Pinch sea salt
- 6 tablespoons rice syrup
- 6 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- Pinch freshly ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon lemon zest
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, combine soaked and drained tapioca, almond milk, salt, rice syrup, and maple syrup. Whisk together. Bring mixture to simmer over burner with flame tamer, stirring constantly to keep tapioca from sticking to bottom of pot.
2. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until mixture starts to thicken, and tapioca just becomes translucent, about 10-15 minutes. Do not overcook or mixture will become glue-like.
3. Stir in vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Remove from heat. Let mixture cool down, and chill further in refrigerator.
