# POACHED CHICKEN AND ROASTED ASPARAGUS

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon curry powder
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 6 cups chicken stock
- 2 ounces coconut milk (1/4 cup)
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 sprigs thyme
- 4 sprigs cilantro
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 3 skinless and boneless chicken breasts (1 pound)
- 2 ounces fennel bulbs (1 small), cored, thinly sliced, soaked bowl of cold water
- 6 ounces mesclun
- 1 head red endive, separated
- 2 ounces red onion (1/2 small), thin rounds
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (1/4 ounce)
- 1/4 cup lime juice (4 limes)
- 1/4 cup yogurt
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground fennel seed
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 cup canola oil
- 3 tablespoons flax seed oil

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375°F. Toss asparagus with oil, salt, 1/4 teaspoon curry powder, and pepper. Transfer asparagus to parchment-lined half sheet pan. Roast until just tender and slightly browned. Set aside.
2. In 1-gallon pot, bring poach ingredients – chicken stock, coconut milk, bay leaves, thyme, cilantro, salt, remaining 1/2 teaspoon curry powder, and pepper – to simmer. Add chicken, lower heat, cover pot, and poach 15 minutes or until chicken is tender and flesh is opaque.
3. Remove pot from heat; let chicken remain in stock 20 to 25 minutes. Remove chicken from stock, cool, and cut into 1/3-inch thick diagonal slices. Set aside.
4. In blender, combine dressing ingredients until creamy. Drain fennel and toss with 2-3 tablespoons of dressing.
5. On large plate, arrange mesclun, poached chicken, fennel, endive, onion, and asparagus. Drizzle dressing over salad ingredients and chicken. Garnish with cilantro.
