# Vanilla Cake

**Yield:** One 8-inch cake

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 cup melted coconut oil
- 3/4 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 2 vanilla beans, split and paste extracted
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection oven). Oil and flour one 8-inch cake pan. Line bottom of pan with parchment.
2. Sift together dry ingredients in medium bowl and whisk together to combine. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together wet ingredients and pour into dry. Mix well.
4. Pour batter into prepared pan.
5. Bake 30-40 minutes until cake is springy to touch and pulls away from sides of pan.
6. Cool in pan on wire rack for 10-15 minutes. Remove from pan and completely cool on rack.
