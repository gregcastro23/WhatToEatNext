# CAROB CAKE WITH WALNUTS

**Yield:** One 8-inch cake

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups + 2 tablespoons whole wheat pastry flour
- 3 tablespoons carob powder
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 6 tablespoons canola OR melted coconut oil
- 2/3 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 3/4 cup almond milk OR soymilk, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped (optional for top of cake)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Oil and flour one 8-inch cake pan and line bottom with parchment.
2. Combine dry ingredients in bowl and whisk together to combine. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together wet ingredients and pour into dry. Mix well.
4. Pour batter into prepared baking pan.
5. Sprinkle walnuts on top.
6. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean.
