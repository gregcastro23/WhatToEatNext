# BOILED NOODLES – SHOCK METHOD

**Yield:** 4 to 5 cups per 8 ounces of noodles

## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 cups water
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 8 ounces soba noodles

## Instructions

1. Bring water to boil over medium heat. Add salt if needed.
2. Add noodles and stir to separate them.
3. Cover pot. Bring to rolling boil. Add 1/2 cup cold water and stir noodles.
4. Bring to rolling boil again. Add 1/2 cup cold water and stir noodles. Bring to rolling boil a third time. Add 1/2 cup cold water and stir noodles. Bring to rolling boil again. Remove from heat and let stand 2 to 3 minutes. Noodles are done when they are the same color inside and out.
5. Drain and rinse in cold water until noodles are cooled.
