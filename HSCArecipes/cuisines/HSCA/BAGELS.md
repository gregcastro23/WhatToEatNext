# BAGELS

**Yield:** approximately 16 bagels

## Ingredients

- 2 1/3 cups warm water (110° F)
- 1/4 cup rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon dry yeast
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 3 cups unbleached high-gluten flour
- 3-4 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- Toppings: poppy seeds, garlic, sesame seeds, Kosher salt, minced onion

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, combine water, rice syrup, and yeast. Proof for 10 minutes or until mixture is foamy.
2. Add olive oil and salt to yeast mixture. Stir together bread and pastry flour. Add enough flour to yeast mixture to create dough that pulls away from sides of bowl.
3. Knead dough using remaining flour as necessary, about 10-15 minutes, or until dough is smooth.
4. Transfer dough to clean, lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Proof in warm place for 45-60 minutes or until dough has doubled in size.
5. While dough is proofing, prepare 2 sheet pans lined with oiled parchment. Bring 1 gallon water to boil and preheat oven to 400° F.
6. Fold/turn dough. Rise again.
7. Divide dough into 16 equal-sized balls (3 ounces each by weight).
8. Form dough into bagel shapes by rolling to 8-inch strips and overlapping and rolling ends or poking hole through center and spreading dough apart.
9. Place toppings in bowls. Boil 3-4 bagels at a time for 30 seconds, flipping once or twice. Remove from pot with spider and turn bagel face side down into toppings. Gently lift bagels out of topping and transfer to prepared baking sheets. Repeat process until all bagels have been boiled.
10. Transfer bagels to oven and bake approximately 20-25 minutes or until dough is golden and firm to touch.
