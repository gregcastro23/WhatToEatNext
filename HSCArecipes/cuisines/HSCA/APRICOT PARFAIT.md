# APRICOT PARFAIT

**Yield:** 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 6 cups apricot juice
- 1/2 cup agar flakes
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 tablespoons cashew butter
- 1/2 cup almonds, toasted and chopped
- Mint leaves for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, combine juice, agar flakes and vanilla extract. Let sit for 10 minutes to help agar dissolve.
2. Bring mixture to boil, and reduce to simmer, covered, until agar is completely dissolved, stirring and scraping bottom and sides of pot with wooden spoon to avoid sticking.
3. Pour juice mixture into hotel pan to cool quickly. Once steam has stopped rising, place in refrigerator to set up and harden into kanten.
4. Blend 3/4 kanten briefly in processor. Pour in serving bowls.
5. Blend remaining 1/4 kanten briefly with cashew butter. Serve as topping. Garnish with almonds and mint leaf.
