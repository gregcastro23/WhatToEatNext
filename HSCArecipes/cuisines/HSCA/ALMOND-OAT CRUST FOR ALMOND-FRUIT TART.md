# ALMOND-OAT CRUST (FOR ALMOND-FRUIT TART)

**Yield:** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup blanched almonds
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 3/4 cup oat flour
- 1/4 cup + 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 tablespoon melted coconut oil or melted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Oil 9-inch tart pan and line with parchment paper or use 5-6 four-inch, non-stick paper tart pans.
2. In food processor, grind almonds, oats, and oat flour together until pulverized to fine meal.
3. In small bowl, combine maple syrup, salt, and oil or butter. Whisk until emulsified.
4. Pour maple-oil mixture into food processor, pulsing to form cohesive dough. If dough is too moist to work with, transfer to fridge for 5-10 minutes.
5. Using fingers, press dough evenly into prepared pan. Blind bake with pie weights to prevent crust from puffing up. Bake 15-20 minutes or until edges of crust are browned. Set aside to cool.
