# BALINESE CHILE MARINADE

**Yield:** approximately 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 medium cloves garlic
- 1 small red onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons tomato puree
- 1 teaspoon ginger juice
- 1/4 cup macadamia nuts
- 1 small jalapeno pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 cup lime juice

## Instructions

1. Combine olive oil, water, garlic, red onion, tomato puree, ginger juice, macadamia nuts, jalapeno pepper, turmeric, red pepper flakes, salt and lime juice in blender. Process to make thin paste. Add water to thin if necessary.
