# HOT WINGS WITH STILTON-YOGURT DRESSING

**Yield:** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour, sifted
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 4 pounds chicken wings (about 16)
- 3 tablespoons canola oil
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1/4 cup Tabasco sauce
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1 cup strained Greek yogurt
- 6 ounces crumbled Stilton cheese
- Freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat convection oven to 400°F.
2. In medium bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, thyme, pepper, cayenne, and salt.
3. Toss wings in canola oil, then flour-spice mixture, and shake off excess.
4. Transfer wings to baker’s rack atop half sheet tray and roast for 15 minutes. Flip wings and roast an additional 15 minutes.
5. Melt butter and combine with Tabasco and maple syrup in medium bowl.
6. Add cooked wings to hot sauce-butter mixture and toss to coat.
7. Combine strained yogurt and cheese. Mix thoroughly. Serve with wings.
