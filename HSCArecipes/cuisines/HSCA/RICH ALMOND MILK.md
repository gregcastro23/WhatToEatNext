# RICH ALMOND MILK

**Yield:** 2 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 3 cups water
- 1 1/2 cups blanched almonds
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, bring water to boil. Add almonds and remove pot from flame. Let sit covered for 20-30 minutes.
2. Transfer almonds and water to Vitamix. Process for few minutes until well homogenized.
3. Strain milk through nut bag or chinois lined with 2 layers of moistened cheesecloth. Reserve almond pulp and set aside.
4. Add vanilla and salt. Whisk to combine.
