# RAW "CHEESECAKE"

**Yield:** 16 slices

## Ingredients

- 5 dates, pitted
- 1 1/2 cups pecans
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- Pinch sea salt
- 2 1/2 cups cashews, soaked overnight
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 3/4 cup agave nectar
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 vanilla bean, seeds extracted
- Pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. Line 9-inch spring-form pan with parchment paper.
2. In food processor combine dates, pecans, coconut oil, and sea salt. Process until dough binds when pressed with fingers. Do not over-blend.
3. Press dough evenly into bottom of spring-form pan.
4. Freeze until crust is set, about 15 minutes.
5. Drain and rinse cashews, discarding soaking water.
6. In high-powered blender, puree cashews, coconut milk, agave nectar, coconut oil, lemon juice, vanilla bean, and salt. Blend until very smooth.
7. Decorate surface of cheesecake with droplets or lines of puree.
8. Using skewer or dinner knife, swirl mixture to create marbled effect.
9. Freeze cheesecake until solid.
