# NIÇOISE SALAD

*(YEAR-ROUND)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup lemon juice (2 lemons)
- 2 ounces shallot, sliced
- 1/2 teaspoon minced thyme leaves
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 anchovies
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 pound small redskin potatoes, scrubbed, halved
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 pound haricots verts, stems trimmed
- 2 large eggs
- 3/4 pound tuna
- 2-3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 12 ounces romaine lettuce (2 heads), bite-size pieces
- 4 ounces grape tomatoes, halved lengthwise
- 1/4 cup Niçoise olives
- 1 tablespoon capers, rinsed

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine all dressing ingredients until creamy. Season to taste. Set aside.
2. Heat oven to 350°F. Combine potatoes in medium bowl and toss with 1 teaspoon of salt and olive oil. Transfer to parchment-lined half sheet pan and roast until golden and tender. Toss with 2-3 tablespoons of the dressing. Set aside.
3. In 2 1/2 quart pot, bring 6 cups of water to boil. Add haricots verts and remaining 1/2 teaspoon of salt and blanch until tender crisp, about 3 minutes. Shock in ice cold water. Drain for a few minutes in colander. Cut haricots verts in half lengthwise and toss with 2-3 tablespoons of the dressing. Set aside.
4. Combine eggs in 2 1/2 quart pot with water to cover by one inch. Cover pot, bring to boil, and remove pot from heat. Allow eggs to remain in hot water for 10 minutes. Shock eggs in cold water. When cool enough to handle, peel and slice each egg into 4 wedges. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of the dressing. Set aside.
5. Heat cast iron grill pan. Drizzle fish with olive oil and season with salt. Grill fish on each side for about 1-2 minutes, depending on thickness of fish. Remove fish from grill. Let fish rest about 7-10 minutes before slicing into 1/4-inch slices.
6. Pour remaining 1/4 cup of dressing over lettuce and mix to combine. Arrange potatoes, haricots verts, eggs, sliced fish, tomatoes, and olives on mound of dressed greens. Garnish with capers.
