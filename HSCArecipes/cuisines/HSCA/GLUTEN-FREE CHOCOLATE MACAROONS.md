# GLUTEN-FREE CHOCOLATE MACAROONS

**Yield:** approximately 14-16 cookies

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate pieces
- 2 large egg whites
- Pinch sea salt
- 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons maple crystals, ground to powder
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
- 3 tablespoons cocoa powder, sifted

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line half sheet tray with parchment paper.
2. Melt chocolate in double boiler, and set aside to cool.
3. In medium bowl, add salt to egg whites and beat with hand mixer or whisk to soft peaks. Slowly add maple crystals, spoonful by spoonful, to form until incorporated.
4. Using spatula, fold in vanilla and coconut.
5. Fold in cocoa powder and cooled melted chocolate. Gently fold to incorporate all ingredients.
6. Using 1-ounce ice cream scoop, transfer cookie dough to sheet tray, leaving space between for slight spread.
7. Bake 15-20 minutes. Cool and serve.
