# WILTED GREENS WITH SPRING ONIONS

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound spring onions (2 bunches), trimmed and halved lengthwise (leave about 1-inch stem intact)
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 pound baby spinach
- 1/2 pound chicory, chopped
- 1/2 pound radicchio, chopped
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 3 tablespoons rice syrup, warmed
- 3 tablespoons sun-dried tomatoes, rehydrated, drained, chopped
- 3 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted
- Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine onions in medium bowl with oil, vinegar, salt and pepper. Toss to combine. Marinate 15-20 minutes.
2. Heat grill. Grill onions on both sides until grill marks form and onions are softened. Set aside.
3. Combine spinach, chicory, and radicchio in medium bowl.
4. In 12-inch sauté pan, heat oil over low heat, sweat garlic and salt until garlic begins to turn golden.
5. In small bowl, whisk together vinegar and syrup until well combined.
6. Add spinach, chicory, and radicchio to pan and sauté until just barely wilted. Add balsamic-rice syrup mixture and toss to combine.
7. Transfer greens to plate and garnish with grilled spring onions, sun-dried tomatoes, and pine nuts.
