# CARROT-CELERY-GINGER JUICE

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 bunches parsley (4 1/2 ounces), washed and spun dry
- 1 bunch celery (15 stalks or 2 pounds), cleaned
- 1 1/2 lemons, peeled
- 1 ounce ginger, washed, unpeeled
- 2 1/2 pounds carrots, washed and trimmed

## Instructions

1. Cut produce to fit juicer feed tube. Juice produce alternating ingredients (parsley, celery, lemon, ginger, carrots, parsley, etc.).
2. Skim foam and serve immediately.
