# SOFRITO TOPPING

**Yield:** About 1/2 cup

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 red pepper, seeded and finely chopped
- 2 ripe tomatoes, seeded and finely chopped
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil. Add red pepper, tomatoes, salt, and paprika. Stir well and cook, uncovered, over low heat for 20 minutes, stirring often. Serve hot.
