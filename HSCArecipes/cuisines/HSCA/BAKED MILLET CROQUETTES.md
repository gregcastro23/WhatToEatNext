# BAKED MILLET CROQUETTES

## Ingredients

- 1 cup millet, washed and drained
- 2 cups water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup sunflower seeds, toasted and ground in food processor
- 1/3 bunch scallions, minced
- 1/3 bunch parsley, minced
- 1 small carrot, finely grated
- 2-4 tablespoons shoyu

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
2. In sauce pot, dry roast millet over medium heat, stirring constantly for about 10 minutes or until it smells like popcorn.
3. Add water and salt to millet and bring to boil. Simmer covered 30 minutes or until water is absorbed. Transfer millet to large bowl and cool.
4. Add sunflower seeds, scallions, parsley and carrots to millet. Add shoyu to taste, and squeeze mixture together with hands until soft and sticky.
5. Using 1/4 cup measure, form croquettes and bake on parchment-lined sheet tray until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
