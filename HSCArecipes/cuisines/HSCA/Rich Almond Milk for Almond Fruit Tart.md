# Rich Almond Milk (for Almond Fruit Tart)

**Yield:** Approximately 2 3/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups blanched almonds
- 3 cups boiling water
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine almonds and water in blender or VitaMix. Process for few minutes until well-homogenized.
2. Strain almond milk through fine sieve or nut milk bag. Add vanilla. Set aside 2 cups for almond cream recipe (recipe following).
