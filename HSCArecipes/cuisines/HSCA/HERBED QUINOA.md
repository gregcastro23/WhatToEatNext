# HERBED QUINOA

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 4 to 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
- 1 1/2 cups boiling water
- 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 1 tablespoon fresh tarragon, chopped

## Instructions

1. Toast quinoa in 2 1/2 quart saucepan over high heat, stirring constantly until it smells nutty and browns lightly (5-7 minutes).
2. Remove quinoa from heat. Add boiling water and salt. Lower heat and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes or until quinoa is cooked and all liquid is absorbed.
3. Fluff with fork and add tarragon before serving.
