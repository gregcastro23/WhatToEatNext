# COCONUT-LIME CREAM TART

**Yield:** 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
- 2/3 cup ground macadamia nuts
- 1/3 cup shredded coconut, lightly toasted
- 1/4 cup maple crystals
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
- 28 ounces coconut milk (2 cans)
- 3 tablespoons agar flakes
- 2 teaspoons rum
- 3/4 cup grade A maple syrup
- 1/4 cup brown rice syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup semolina flour
- 1 1/2 cups cold water
- 1/2 cup lime juice (4 limes)
- 1 tablespoon lime zest
- 1 tablespoon toasted shredded coconut
- 1 tablespoon lime zest

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Oil 9-inch tart pan with removable bottom.
2. Using your fingertips, blend together flour, macadamias, shredded coconut, maple crystals, salt and coconut oil until mixture resembles coarse meal. Press mixture into tart pan. Refrigerate for 15-20 minutes. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown. Set aside to cool.
3. Combine coconut milk into 2 1/2 quart saucepan with agar flakes, rum, maple syrup, brown rice syrup and salt. Bring to boil over medium heat, lower heat and simmer (whisking occasionally) about 30 minutes or until agar is completely dissolved.
4. In second 2 1/2 quart saucepan, combine semolina flour and cold water. Over high flame, whisk constantly until mixture thickens (consistency should resemble thick cream of wheat). Turn off heat and whisk into coconut milk mixture. Add lime juice and zest. Whisk to combine.
5. Pour filling into large bowl to cool, stirring constantly, to prevent mixture from setting up. When filling is warm (not hot), pour into cooled tart shell. Garnish with toasted coconut and lime zest.
