# Fried Calamari

## Ingredients

- 1 to 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
- Canola oil OR coconut oil for deep-frying
- 1 1/2 pounds clean squid, cut into 1/4 inch ringlets
- 1 tablespoon chopped mint (1/4 ounce)
- 2 lemons, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. In small mixing bowl, combine salt, pepper and flour.
2. In wok, heat oil to 350°F-375°F.
3. Dredge squid in flour mixture, coating well. Shake off excess flour.
4. Deep-fry squid in small batches until golden (this helps to maintain constant oil temperature to produce crispy squid).
5. Drain fried calamari on paper towels.
6. Sprinkle with salt; garnish with mint and lemon wedges.
