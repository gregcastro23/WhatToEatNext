# HORSERADISH AND LEMON CONDIMENT

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound horseradish, peeled
- 3 lemons, juiced (about 1 1/2 cups)

## Instructions

1. Grate horseradish on box grater using fine holes.
2. Combine horseradish with lemon juice and set aside for ten minutes at room temperature.
3. Serve cold or room temperature mixed into water, stock or soup.
