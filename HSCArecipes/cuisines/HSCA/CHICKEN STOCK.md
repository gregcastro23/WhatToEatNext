# CHICKEN STOCK

**Yield:** 4 quarts each (1 gallon)

## Ingredients

- 8 pounds bones (necks, backs, wing tips)
- 6 quarts water
- 1 pound mirepoix
- bouquet garni
- 2 tablespoons brown rice vinegar or cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Simmer 1 1/2 to 4 hours.
