# CREAM OF CURRIED SWEET POTATO SOUP

**Yield:** 7 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon coconut oil
- 8 ounces onion (1 medium), medium dice
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder
- 2 pounds sweet potatoes (2 large), medium dice
- 6 cups Vegetable Stock
- 1 cup coconut milk
- Salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon lime juice (1 lime)
- 2 limes, sliced into 7 thin rounds, for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat coconut oil over medium flame. Add onion, salt, and curry powder. Sweat until onions are translucent.
2. Add sweet potatoes and stock to pot. Raise heat bring and bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer with lid ajar until potatoes are tender.
3. Puree soup in blender until creamy.
4. Return soup to pot. Re-heat and whisk in coconut milk. Season to taste with salt and lime juice.
5. Garnish with lime rounds.
