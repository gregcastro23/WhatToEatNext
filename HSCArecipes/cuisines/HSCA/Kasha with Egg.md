# Kasha with Egg

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups mushroom or onion stock
- 1 cup kasha, medium grain (Wolf's brand is best)
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In saucepot, bring stock to boil.
2. In medium bowl combine kasha with egg, stirring well to combine.
3. Heat 10-inch sauté pan over medium-high flame and add kasha.
4. Using wooden spoon, stir constantly, toasting until buckwheat is dry.
5. Turn off heat under pan and add stock. Quickly cover to avoid sputtering.
6. Bring back to boil and reduce to simmer for 10 to 15 minutes until all water is absorbed.
