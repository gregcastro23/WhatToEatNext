# Watermelon Juice

**Yield:** 2 quarts

## Ingredients

- 1 (2 pound) organic, seedless watermelon, scrubbed and peeled up to white pith
- 1/4 cup of lemon juice (about 1 lemon)

## Instructions

1. Cut watermelon into approximately 2 inch x 4 inch pieces.
2. In a high-speed blender puree watermelon pieces in batches.
3. Using a mesh strainer set over a large bowl strain watermelon juice. Discard solids.
4. Stir in lemon juice and refrigerate until ready to serve.
