# BRAISED CHICKEN WITH VEGETABLES AND TARRAGON

**Yield:** 4 portions

## Ingredients

- 1 pound chicken legs (4)
- 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour, sifted
- 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons whole wheat pastry flour
- 8 ounces carrot (1 large), 1-inch roll cut
- 3 ribs celery (6 ounces), large dice
- 1/2 pound onion (1 medium), large dice
- 1/2 pound fingerling potatoes, 1-inch size pieces
- 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- Sachet of 2 bay leaves, 10 black peppercorns, 6 sprigs thyme, 6 parsley sprigs, 1/2 ounce tarragon
- 1/3 cup white wine
- 1 1/2 cups chicken stock
- 2 tablespoons chopped tarragon (1/8 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 325°F.
2. Toss chicken legs in bowl with flour, salt, and pepper to thoroughly coat.
3. Heat oil in 12-inch sauté pan on medium-high flame.
4. When oil is hot, lower heat and sear chicken on both sides, being careful not to burn it.
5. Add carrots, celery, onion, and flour; sauté for 1 minute. Add potatoes, salt, pepper, and sachet. Stir in white wine to deglaze. Add stock, cover pot, and bring braise to boil.
6. Transfer pan to oven and cook for 40-45 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked and vegetables are tender but not mushy.
7. Garnish with tarragon. Season to taste and serve.
