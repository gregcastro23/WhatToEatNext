# SPICE LOAF RING

**Yield:** 8-10 Servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups (3 sticks) softened butter, + 1-2 teaspoons butter for greasing pan
- 1 cup maple crystals
- 4 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 3/4 cup sorghum flour
- 3/4 cup Gluten-Free Flour Blend (recipe below)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/8 teaspoon allspice
- 1/8 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour 9 or 10-inch non-stick Bundt pan.
2. In food processor, combine butter and maple crystals. Process 2-3 minutes. Add eggs one at a time. Add almond extract. Continue to blend 2-3 minutes longer. Transfer to medium bowl.
3. In separate medium bowl, sift together flours, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, and salt.
4. Fold flour mixture into butter egg mixture (batter will be slightly thick).
5. Pour batter into Bundt pan (should be about 2/3 full).
6. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Cool in pan about 10 minutes. Run knife around edge to loosen cake from pan. Cool completely on rack.
