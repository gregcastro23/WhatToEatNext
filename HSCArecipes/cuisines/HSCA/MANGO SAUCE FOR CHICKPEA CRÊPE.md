# MANGO SAUCE (FOR CHICKPEA CRÊPE)

**Yield:** approximately 1 1/4 cups (ten 1-ounce servings)

## Ingredients

- 1 ripe mango, peeled, pitted and coarsely chopped
- 2 ripe plum tomatoes, concassé (peeled, seeded, and chopped), reserve juices
- 1 shallot, coarsely chopped
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 2-3 teaspoons lime juice (about 1/2 small lime)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1-2 jalapeño peppers, minced
- 2-3 tablespoons cilantro, chopped

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine mango, tomatoes, tomato juices, shallot, canola oil, lime juice, and salt. Blend until creamy.
2. Adjust seasonings. Garnish sauce with jalapeño and cilantro.
