# Broiled Arctic Char Over Black Quinoa, Rapini, and Capers

**Yield:** Four 3-ounce portions

## Ingredients

- Four 3-ounce, skinless Arctic char filets (12 ounces), butterflied and pounded to 1/4 inch between parchment
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 cup lemon juice (2 lemons)
- Black Quinoa with Capers and Rapini (see following recipe)

## Instructions

1. Heat salamander and set rack on highest setting.
2. Combine salt and pepper, and sprinkle over char.
3. Lightly coat sizzle platter with oil and arrange fish on top.
4. Sprinkle remaining oil and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice atop each portion of fish.
5. Broil char under salamander for approximately 3 minutes.
6. Serve over Black Quinoa (recipe below).
