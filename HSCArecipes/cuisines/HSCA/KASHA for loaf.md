# KASHA (for loaf)

## Ingredients

- 1 cup kasha
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/4 cups boiling water or stock

## Instructions

1. In small pot, combine kasha, salt and water.
2. Bring to boil, immediately turn off heat, and let sit (with lid on) until all water is absorbed (approximately 10 minutes).
