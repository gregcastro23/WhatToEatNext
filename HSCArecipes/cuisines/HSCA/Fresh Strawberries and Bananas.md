# Fresh Strawberries and Bananas

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 2 pints strawberries, trimmed and sliced
- 2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 3 bananas, sliced
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Instructions

1. In bowl, toss strawberries with maple crystals. Set aside to macerate for 30 minutes.
2. In separate bowl, combine bananas with lemon juice. When strawberries are done macerating, add bananas to strawberries.
