# FRESH ALMOND MILK

**Yield:** 12 cups

## Ingredients

- 14 cups water
- 6 cups blanched almonds

## Instructions

1. In 2-gallon pot, bring water to boil, pour over blanched almonds and let sit covered for 20-30 minutes.
2. Transfer almonds and water to Vitamix. Blend until well homogenized.
3. Pour almond mixture in batches through sieve or chinois lined with double layer of rinsed cheesecloth, wringing and squeezing cheesecloth to extract as much liquid from pulp as possible.
