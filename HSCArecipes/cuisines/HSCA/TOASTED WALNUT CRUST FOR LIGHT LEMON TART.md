# TOASTED WALNUT CRUST (FOR LIGHT LEMON TART)

**Yield:** One 10-inch tart (8-10 servings)

## Ingredients

- 1 cup walnuts, toasted and cooled
- 1 cup Gluten-Free Flour Blend (recipe below)
- 1 tablespoon maple crystals
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon melted coconut oil (or butter)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Oil and parchment 8 or 9-inch tart pan. Alternatively, use 5-6 four-inch, non-stick paper tart pans.
2. Combine nuts, flour, maple crystals, and salt in food processor. Grind to fine meal.
3. Add syrup and oil to processor. Pulse to form dough.
4. Place dough in middle of tart pan. Using your fingertips, press dough out so that bottom and sides are evenly covered. Trim crust and discard any excess. Blot excess oil from crust with paper towel.
5. Transfer tart pan(s) to refrigerator for 10-15 minutes to chill.
6. Transfer tart pan(s) to sheet tray and bake about 20 minutes. Cool completely before filling.
