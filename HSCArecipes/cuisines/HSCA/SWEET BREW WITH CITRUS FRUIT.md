# SWEET BREW WITH CITRUS FRUIT

**Yield:** 10 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 quarts filtered water
- 6 kukicha tea bags (1/2 cup loose kukicha)
- 4 mint tea bags
- 1/2 lemon, thinly sliced
- 1/2 orange, thinly sliced
- 2 quarts organic apple juice

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, bring water to boil.
2. Turn off flame. Steep tea bags and fruit in water, covered, for 5 minutes.
3. Add apple juice to pot, stir and serve.
