# TERIYAKI SAUCE

**Yield:** 1 3/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup filtered water
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed
- 2 tablespoons ginger juice (1-inch knob ginger)
- 3-4 tablespoons shoyu
- 6 tablespoons barley malt
- pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine all ingredients and process until smooth.
