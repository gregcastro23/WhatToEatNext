# GLUTEN-FREE CRÊPES

**Yield:** approximately 16 crepes

## Ingredients

- 3 large eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted (plus more for pan)
- 3 tablespoons maple crystals
- 2 cups Gluten-Free Flour Blend
- 2 cups whole milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Emulsify all ingredients in blender until smooth.
