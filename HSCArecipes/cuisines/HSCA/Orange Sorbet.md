# Orange Sorbet

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 cups orange juice, strained (8 oranges)
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1/3 cup rice syrup
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 2 tablespoons vodka
- Pinch sea salt
- 2 teaspoons finely grated orange zest

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, whisk together orange juice, maple syrup, rice syrup, lemon juice, vodka, and sea salt.
2. Pour mixture into ice cream machine and freeze until scoop-able consistency is achieved. When mixture starts to firm up, add zest and continue to churn until incorporated. Transfer sorbet to freezer to firm consistency.
