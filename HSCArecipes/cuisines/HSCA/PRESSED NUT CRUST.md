# PRESSED NUT CRUST

**Yield:** One 9-inch crust

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup almonds
- 1/2 cup oats
- 3/4 cup oat flour
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- pinch of salt
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup canola oil OR 2 tablespoons melted coconut oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Oil 9-inch pie or tart pan.
2. Grind almonds in food processor to form fine almond meal.
3. Grind oats coarsely in food processor.
4. In medium bowl, combine almonds, oats, oat flour, cinnamon and salt; mix well.
5. In separate bowl, whisk together maple syrup, and canola or melted coconut oil.
6. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients.
7. With wet fingertips, press crust into oiled pie or tart pan.
8. Bake crust 20 to 25 minutes or until golden and completely baked.
