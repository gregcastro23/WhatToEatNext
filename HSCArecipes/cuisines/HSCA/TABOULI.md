# TABOULI

**Yield:** approximately 2 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup bulgur, cooked, still warm
- 1/2 cup lemon juice
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 plum tomatoes, seeded and cut into small dice
- 1 bunch parsley, chopped
- 4 scallions, thinly sliced
- 2 ribs celery, cut into small dice
- 1/2 bunch mint, chopped
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, toss warm bulgur with lemon juice, olive oil, and salt. Let sit until it reaches room temperature.
2. Combine bulgur with tomatoes, parsley, scallions, celery, and mint, mixing thoroughly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
