# SESAME PRESSED CRUST

**Yield:** One 9-inch crust

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 cup food-processed rolled oats
- 1/3 cup toasted sesame seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 3 tablespoons warm maple syrup
- Chilled juice OR water to bind as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Oil 9-inch pie or tart pan.
2. Process together whole wheat pastry flour, oatmeal, sesame seeds and salt until mixture is uniformly flour-like.
3. Whisk together oil and syrup.
4. Add oil-syrup mixture to processor and mix until thoroughly combined. Add juice or water if more liquid is needed.
5. With wet fingertips, press mixture into oiled pie plate.
6. Bake 20 to 25 minutes, until golden.
