# Chimichurri Sauce

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 3 ounces parsley, stemmed, washed, and spun dry
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic (2 large cloves)
- 1/2 ounce red onion, minced
- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 1 teaspoon dry oregano
- 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Roughly chop parsley and transfer to food processor.
2. Add garlic, onion, vinegar, lemon juice, and oregano to food processor with parsley and pulse to combine.
3. Add olive oil through feed tube and continue to process for 30 seconds or until sauce is blended, but still retains some texture. Season to taste.
