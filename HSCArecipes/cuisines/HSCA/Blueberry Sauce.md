# Blueberry Sauce

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 2 pints blueberries (4 cups)
- 2 cups apple juice
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine blueberries, apple juice, and maple syrup in pot. Bring mixture to simmer.
2. Simmer sauce uncovered until blueberries begin to pop open and sauce begins to thicken, approximately 15 minutes.
3. Stir in lemon zest and vanilla. Simmer another minute.
