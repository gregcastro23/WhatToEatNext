# HERBED DINNER ROLLS

**Yield:** 24 small rolls

## Ingredients

- 2 cups warm water
- 1 tablespoon dry yeast
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1 cup unbleached white high-gluten flour
- 2 cups whole wheat pastry flour, divided
- 2-3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon +1 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme OR other herbs
- 1/4 cup sesame OR poppy seeds for top
- olive oil OR rice syrup diluted with water for brushing

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. In large bowl, combine water, yeast, maple syrup, white high-gluten flour, and 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour. Stir until thick batter forms. Cover sponge with plastic wrap and set aside in warm place for 30-45 minutes or until foamy and doubled in size.
2. Add oil, salt, and thyme to sponge and stir to combine. Add remaining whole wheat pastry flour and just enough all-purpose flour to create dough that pulls away from sides of bowl.
3. Knead dough with remaining flour for about 10-15 minutes or until smooth dough has been created. Transfer dough to clean, lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Proof in warm place for 45-60 minutes or until dough has doubled in size.
4. Fold and turn dough. Rise again. Divide dough into 1-ounce balls.
5. Form dough into dinner roll shapes (see diagrams following) and transfer rolls to parchment-lined sheet trays. Cover with damp towel and set aside to rise for 10-15 minutes more.
6. Lightly brush tops of rolls with olive oil or egg wash. Sprinkle sesame seeds or poppy seeds on top.
7. Bake 15-20 minutes or until lightly golden and firm to touch.
8. Remove from sheet trays and cool on racks before serving.
