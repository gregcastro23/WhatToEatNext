# GLUTEN-FREE BANANA BREAD

**Yield:** 9 muffin-size quick breads

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Gluten-Free Flour Blend (see previous recipe)
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
- 1/4 cup butter (4 tablespoons), melted
- 1/2 cup maple crystals
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 cup mashed banana (from about 2 medium bananas)
- 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons buttermilk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons maple crystals
- 3 tablespoons gluten-free whole rolled oats

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
2. In large bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
3. In separate medium bowl, whisk together butter, maple crystals, egg, mashed bananas, vanilla extract, and buttermilk.
4. Whisk wet ingredients into dry. Fold together, making sure to scrape bottom of bowl to mix well. Pour dough into prepared muffin molds.
5. In small saucepan, melt butter for crumble. Combine butter, maple crystals, and rolled oats in bowl. Transfer crumble to top of bread.
6. Bake for 30-45 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center of loaf comes out clean. Remove from oven and allow to rest in pan for 15 minutes, before inverting onto wire rack to cool completely.
