# WATERCRESS AND DANDELION GREENS WITH BABY ARTICHOKES

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds baby artichokes (6), trimmed, cleaned, tossed in 2 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 pound watercress (2 bunches), stemmed
- 2 ounces dandelion greens, stemmed, chopped
- 7 ounces radishes (1 bunch), stemmed, sliced into thin rounds
- 1 recipe Creamy Artichoke Dressing (see recipe below)

## Instructions

1. Arrange baby artichokes in steamer basket set in 1-gallon pot filled with 2 cups water. Steam artichokes until just tender when pierced with a toothpick. Cool and cut in half lengthwise. Toss with olive oil and salt. Set aside.
2. Heat grill. Grill artichoke hearts until grill marks are visible. Remove from grill and set aside.
3. Combine watercress and dandelion greens in large bowl.
4. Toss watercress and dandelion greens with dressing (see below). Garnish with radishes and artichokes.
