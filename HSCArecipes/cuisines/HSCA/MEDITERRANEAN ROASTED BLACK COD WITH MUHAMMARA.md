# MEDITERRANEAN ROASTED BLACK COD WITH MUHAMMARA

**Yield:** Six 3-ounce portions

## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon fennel seed
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin seed
- 1/4 teaspoon allspice
- 1/4 teaspoon sumac
- 1 1/4 pounds black cod, skinned
- 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
- Scant 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- Extra virgin olive oil for brushing
- 2 large red peppers (1 pound), roasted, skinned and seeded
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 tablespoon pomegranate molasses
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 cup walnuts, toasted
- 1/4 cup plain breadcrumbs
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons vegetable stock or water
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sherry vinegar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
2. In small sauté pan over low heat lightly toast fennel and cumin. Remove from heat.
3. When spices are cool, powder them in coffee grinder and add allspice and sumac.
4. Divide black cod into six portions and sprinkle spice mixture on both sides of fish. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Transfer fish to parchment-lined half sheet pan. Lightly brush fish with oil.
6. Roast fish for about 7-8 minutes or until inside of fish is opaque. Remove from oven and set aside. Serve with Muhammara (recipe below).
7. Combine peppers, garlic, pomegranate molasses, lemon juice, and walnuts in bowl of food processor and puree.
8. Add breadcrumbs, black pepper, olive oil, vegetable stock, and sherry vinegar. Puree until smooth. Serve with Mediterranean Roasted Black Cod.
