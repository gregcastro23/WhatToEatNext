# RICE FLOUR GRIDDLE CAKES

*(Gluten-Free, Vegan)*

**Yield:** 18 four-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown rice flour
- 1 tablespoon + 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 cup maple crystals
- 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon flax seed, ground to fine meal
- 2 cups almond milk
- 2 teaspoons coconut oil, melted

## Instructions

1. Whisk together flour, baking powder, maple crystals, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt and ground flax seed.
2. In blender combine almond milk, and butter or oil.
3. Combine liquid ingredients and dry ingredients.
4. Using 1-ounce ladle, pour batter onto griddle. Cook cakes until bubbles in batter break on surface; flip and cook until browned.
