# Hemp Coconut 'Milk' (for Golden Turmeric 'Panna Cotta')

**Yield:** Serves 4

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup hemp seeds
- 1 (14 ounce) can full-fat coconut milk
- 2 cups filtered water
- pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. Add hemp seeds, coconut milk, water, and salt to a high-speed blender.
2. Blend mixture until smooth, and strain through fine mesh strainer (or nut milk bag), if finer texture is desired.
3. Serve warm or cold, or use in your favorite recipes. Store in refrigerator in an airtight container for 3 to 5 days.
