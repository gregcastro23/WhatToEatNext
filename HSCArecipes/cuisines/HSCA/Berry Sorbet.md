# Berry Sorbet

**Yield:** 5 cups (eight 4-ounce servings)

## Ingredients

- 4 cups strawberries (12 ounces)
- 2 cups frozen raspberries (6 ounces)
- 2 cups frozen blackberries (6 ounces)
- 2 cups white grape juice
- 3 tablespoons vodka
- 4 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 cup grade A maple syrup

## Instructions

1. In blender, puree berries with grape juice, vodka, and lime juice.
2. Strain puree through fine sieve to remove seeds. Add maple syrup to taste.
3. Transfer berry mixture to ice cream machine and process until frozen.
