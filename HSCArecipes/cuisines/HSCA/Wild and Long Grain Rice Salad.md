# Wild and Long Grain Rice Salad

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups cooked long grain rice, cooled
- 1 1/2 cups cooked wild rice, cooled
- 2 ribs celery, strings removed, brunoise
- 6 radishes, brunoise
- 3/4 cup currants
- 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted and chopped
- 1/3 bunch parsley, chopped
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- 5 tablespoons brown rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1 tablespoon stone-ground mustard
- 1/2 - 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon fresh herbs, optional
- 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, combine rice, celery, radishes, currants and walnuts.
2. In blender, combine dressing ingredients.
3. Add dressing to rice-vegetable mixture.
4. Add parsley and season to taste with salt and pepper.
