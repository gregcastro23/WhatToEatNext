# GLUTEN-FREE PEAR-CARDAMOM QUICK BREAD

**Yield:** 6-8 muffin-size quick breads

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Gluten-Free Flour Blend (see previous recipe)
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 cup maple crystals
- 1/2 cup canola oil
- 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- 1 ripe pear, grated
- 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
2. In large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and cardamom.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together egg, maple crystals, canola oil, and vanilla extract. Add wet ingredients to dry and stir to combine. Add pears and walnuts and divide batter between muffin molds.
4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
5. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to wire rack to finish cooling.
