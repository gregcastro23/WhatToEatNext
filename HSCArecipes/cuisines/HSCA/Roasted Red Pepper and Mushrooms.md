# Roasted Red Pepper and Mushrooms

## Ingredients

- 1 pound red pepper, cut into large dice
- 1/2 pound mushrooms, washed, stemmed, cut into quarters
- 1 zucchini, cut into large dice
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled and cut in half
- 2-3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F-400°F.
2. Combine vegetables, garlic, and olive oil in large roasting pan.
3. Roast for 30 minutes. Drain and cool.
