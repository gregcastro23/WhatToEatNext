# SWEET CORN SAUCE WITH BUTTER

**Yield:** 1/2 cup

## Ingredients

- 4 ears corn
- 2 teaspoons butter, chilled
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- Sea salt to taste
- Pinch white pepper

## Instructions

1. Remove corn kernels from cob.
2. Puree corn kernels in VitaMix until smooth.
3. Transfer puree to strainer lined with moistened double layer of cheesecloth. Squeeze 'corn milk' through cheesecloth.
4. Transfer 'corn milk' to 2 1/2 quart pot, and simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring constantly, until mixture begins to thicken. Whisk in butter. Sauce should have nappé consistency.
5. Whisk in lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
