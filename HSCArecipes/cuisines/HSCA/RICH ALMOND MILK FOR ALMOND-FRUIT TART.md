# RICH ALMOND MILK (FOR ALMOND-FRUIT TART)

**Yield:** Approximately 2 3/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups blanched almonds
- 3 cups boiling water
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine almonds and water in blender or Vitamix. Process for a few minutes until well-homogenized.
2. Rinse piece of cheesecloth (double thickness) with cold water and wring thoroughly.
3. Pour small amount of almond mixture through sieve or chinois lined with prepared cheesecloth, wringing and squeezing cheesecloth to extract as much liquid from pulp as possible. Discard each batch of almond pulp when no more liquid can be extracted. Rinse cheesecloth well in cold water and proceed with next batch, repeating this process until all of almond mixture has been 'milked.'
4. Strain almond milk through sieve. Add vanilla.
