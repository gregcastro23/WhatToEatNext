# Fig-Thyme Preserves (for Portobello Mousse)

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried figs, stemmed and quartered
- 1/4 ounce thyme, stemmed (approximately 2 tablespoons)
- 2 cups apple juice
- 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/3 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine figs, thyme, apple juice, salt, pepper, and apple cider vinegar in 2-quart saucepan.
2. Bring mixture to simmer and cook for 20 minutes with lid slightly ajar.
3. Use immersion blender to roughly puree mixture slightly (mixture can be chunky).
4. Chill preserves before serving.
5. Serve fig-thyme preserves with Portobello mousse and sliced baguette (see previous recipe).
