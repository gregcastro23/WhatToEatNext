# CHICKPEA CRÊPES WITH CURRIED CHICKPEAS, VEGETABLES & MANGO SAUCE

**Yield:** approximately 10 crêpes

## Ingredients

- Chickpea Crêpe (recipe below)
- Curried Chickpea Filling (recipe follows)
- Mango Sauce, for garnish (recipe follows)

## Instructions

1. Place a warm Chickpea Crêpe flat on a clean surface or plate.
2. Spoon a generous portion of the Curried Chickpea and vegetable filling down the center of the crêpe.
3. Fold or roll the crêpe to neatly enclose the filling.
4. Garnish with Mango Sauce drizzle and fresh herbs. Serve warm.
