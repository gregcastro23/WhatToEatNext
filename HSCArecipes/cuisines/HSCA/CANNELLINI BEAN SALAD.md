# CANNELLINI BEAN SALAD

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cannellini beans, soaked overnight, drained and rinsed
- 3 cups filtered water
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice (1 1/2 lemons)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 roasted red pepper, peeled, seeded, and cut into small dice (1/4 inch)
- 4 ounces red onion, minced
- 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
- 2 tablespoons basil chiffonade (1 ounce)
- Sea salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine beans, water, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and bay leaf in 1-gallon pot and bring to boil over high heat.
2. Lower heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 25-30 minutes or until beans are tender. Remove bay leaf and drain beans in colander.
3. Whisk together remaining 1/2 teaspoon of salt, black pepper, mustard, lemon juice, and olive oil. Pour over warm beans.
4. When beans have cooled, toss together with red peppers, red onion, olives, and basil.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
