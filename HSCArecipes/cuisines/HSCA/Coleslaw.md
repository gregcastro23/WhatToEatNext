# Coleslaw

*(Fall/Winter)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup blanched almonds
- 1/2 cup water
- Pinch sea salt
- 1 head garlic, roasted, paste extracted
- 1 scant tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon agave syrup
- 2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 1/4 cup canola oil
- 1/3 teaspoon sea salt or to taste
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 1/4 cup water (or as needed)
- 12 ounces green cabbage, cored, chiffonade
- 6 ounces red cabbage, cored, chiffonade
- 5 ounces yellow pepper (1 medium), brunoise
- 8 ounces carrot (1 large), brunoise
- 7 tablespoons chopped parsley (1 ounce)
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill (1 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Combine almonds in 2 1/2 quart pot with water and salt. Bring to boil, cover, and remove pot from heat. Let almonds soak for 20 minutes.
2. In blender, combine almonds, soaking water, garlic paste, mustard, agave syrup, vinegar, lemon juice, canola oil, salt, and pepper. Blend until creamy, adding water a little at a time until consistency is similar to mayonnaise. Set aside.
3. In large bowl, combine cabbage (green and red) with yellow pepper and carrots.
4. Toss dressing with vegetables. Fold in parsley and dill.
