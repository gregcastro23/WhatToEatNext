# CORNMEAL CRUST

**Yield:** 9-inch tart

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1/2 cup pecans
- 1 cup oats
- 1/4 cup canola OR melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine cornmeal, pecans, and oats in food processor. Grind to fine meal.
2. Whisk together oil, maple syrup, and sea salt. Add wet ingredients to dry in processor until cookie-like dough is formed. Press dough into lightly oiled tart pan. Bake tart 12-15 minutes or until firm and lightly browned. Cool.
