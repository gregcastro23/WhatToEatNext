# SWEET CORN SAUCE

**Yield:** 1/2 cup

## Ingredients

- 4 ears corn
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- Sea salt to taste
- Pinch white pepper

## Instructions

1. Remove corn kernels from cob.
2. Puree corn kernels in VitaMix until smooth.
3. Transfer puree to strainer lined with moistened double layer of cheesecloth. Squeeze 'corn milk' through cheesecloth.
4. Transfer 'corn milk' to 2 1/2 quart pot, and simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring constantly, until desired nappé consistency is achieved.
5. Whisk in lemon juice, and season to taste with salt and pepper.
