# Master Cleanse

*Popularized by Stanley Burroughs (From Elson M. Haas, MD, The Detox Diet)*

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1-2 tablespoons organic maple syrup (preferably Grade B)
- Pinch cayenne pepper
- 8 ounces filtered water

## Instructions

1. Mix and drink 8-12 glasses throughout day.
2. Eat or drink nothing else except water, laxative herb tea, and peppermint or chamomile teas.
3. Keep master cleanse in glass jar, not in plastic.
4. Rinse your mouth with water after each glass to prevent lemon juice from damaging enamel of your teeth.
