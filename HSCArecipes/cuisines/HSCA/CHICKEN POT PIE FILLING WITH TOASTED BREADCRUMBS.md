# CHICKEN POT PIE FILLING WITH TOASTED BREADCRUMBS

**Yield:** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 16 bone-in chicken thighs with skin (5 pounds)
- 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 10 tablespoons butter
- 2 pounds onion (3 large), small dice
- 3/4 cup whole wheat pastry flour, sifted
- 6 cups chicken stock
- 1 pound carrots (2 large), medium dice
- 6 ribs celery (12 ounces), medium dice
- 4 ounces frozen peas (1 1/2 cups), thawed
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme (1/4 ounce)
- 1 1/2 cups whole wheat Panko breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (2 ounces)
- Salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat convection oven to 400°F.
2. Combine thighs and oil in bowl. Coat thighs with 1/4 cup olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer chicken to parchment-lined half sheet pan, skin side up, and roast for 20 to 25 minutes, until cooked through but still moist. After chicken has cooled enough to handle, remove and discard skin and bones. Cut chicken into large dice and set aside.
3. In 1-gallon pot, melt butter over medium flame. Add onion and sweat until translucent (about 10 minutes). Stir in flour and continue to cook over low heat about 7-8 minutes, stirring constantly. Add chicken stock and simmer over low heat for another 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Bring 1 gallon water to boil and add 1 tablespoon salt. Prepare ice bath. Blanch carrots and celery for 5 minutes in boiling, salted water. Shock in ice water, drain, and set aside.
5. Add chicken, carrots, celery, peas, and fresh thyme to pot with chicken stock and continue to cook over low heat for an additional 5 minutes. Check seasoning and adjust if necessary.
6. Heat 10-inch sauté pan over low heat. Add Panko and remaining 2 tablespoons of oil. Carefully sauté Panko until golden brown. Add parsley and season lightly with salt and pepper, continuing to toast 2 minutes more.
7. Divide pot pie mixture equally between 8 bowls and top with breadcrumb mixture.
