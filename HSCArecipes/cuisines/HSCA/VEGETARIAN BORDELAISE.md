# VEGETARIAN BORDELAISE

**Yield:** 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 1/2 ounces shallots (1 medium), minced
- 1 1/2 ounces carrots, minced
- 1 rib celery, minced
- 2 ounces onion, minced
- 3 peppercorns
- 1 thyme sprig
- 1/4 cup red wine
- 2 parsley sprigs
- 1 quart Sauce Espagnole (Brown Sauce)
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, heat oil over medium flame. Sweat shallots, carrots, celery, and onions until vegetables are softened.
2. Add peppercorns, thyme sprig, and red wine. Increase heat to medium-high and reduce wine until almost dry (au sec).
3. Add parsley sprigs and Sauce Espagnole.
4. Bring sauce to boil and skim. Reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, for 20 to 25 minutes, or until flavor has rounded and nappé consistency is achieved.
5. Strain sauce through chinois. Adjust seasoning to taste.
