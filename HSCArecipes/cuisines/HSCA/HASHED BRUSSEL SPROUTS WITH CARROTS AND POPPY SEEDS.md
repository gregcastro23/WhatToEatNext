# HASHED BRUSSEL SPROUTS WITH CARROTS AND POPPY SEEDS

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** approximately 6 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 pound Brussels sprouts, ends trimmed and finely sliced
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (about 1/2 small lemon)
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 pound carrot (1 large), small dice
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon lightly toasted poppy seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 small bunch parsley, chopped (about 1/3 cup)

## Instructions

1. In large bowl toss Brussels sprouts with lemon juice.
2. In 12-inch sauté pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat. Add carrots and sauté for about 2 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon of wine, lower heat and continue to cook until carrots are bright orange but still crunchy.
3. Add Brussels sprouts, garlic, poppy seeds and remaining 2 tablespoons of oil. Add remaining 3 tablespoons of wine. Cook until Brussels sprouts are tender (about 3-4 minutes).
4. Warm right before service and add salt and pepper. Add parsley just before service.
