# Flounder Roulades

**Yield:** 8-10 portions

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 2 ounces yellow onion, small dice
- 8 ounces zucchini, seeded and grated
- 2 tablespoons gluten-free bread crumbs
- 2 teaspoons capers
- 3 Roma tomatoes, concassé
- 8-10 flounder filets, seasoned with salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 4 sprigs thyme

## Instructions

1. Preheat conventional oven to 375°F.
2. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in 12-inch sauté pan. Add onions and sweat until tender. Add zucchini and cook for 1-2 more minutes.
3. Transfer onion/zucchini mixture to bowl and stir in breadcrumbs, capers, and tomatoes.
4. Place fish filets on cutting board presentation side down. Divide filling mixture into even portions among filets.
5. Roll filets and transfer to baking dish drizzled with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil (seam side down).
6. Sprinkle butter and wine in pan along with sprigs of thyme.
7. Bake for 20 minutes.
