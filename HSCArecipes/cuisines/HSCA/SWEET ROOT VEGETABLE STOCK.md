# SWEET ROOT VEGETABLE STOCK

**Yield:** approximately 2 quarts

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds onions, peeled, large dice
- 1 pound carrots, 3/4-inch dice
- 4 ribs celery, 3/4-inch chunks
- 3/4 pound parsnips, 3/4-inch chunks
- 1 pound turnips, 3/4-inch chunks
- Bouquet garni: 2 bay leaves, 8 parsley stems, 2 sprigs thyme, 8 black peppercorns
- 3 quarts water

## Instructions

1. In medium stockpot, simmer ingredients for 45 minutes. Strain.
