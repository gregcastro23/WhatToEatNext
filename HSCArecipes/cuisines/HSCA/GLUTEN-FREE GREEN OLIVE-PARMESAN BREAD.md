# GLUTEN-FREE GREEN OLIVE-PARMESAN BREAD

**Yield:** 6 muffin-size quick breads

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Gluten-Free Flour Blend (see previous recipe)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1/4 cup green olives, halved
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325° F.
2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda in one bowl.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together egg, buttermilk and melted butter.
4. Mix wet into dry until just combined. Add olives and parmesan.
5. Pour batter into muffin molds.
6. Bake for 30-45 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in bread comes out clean.
