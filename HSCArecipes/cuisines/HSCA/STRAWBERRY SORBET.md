# STRAWBERRY SORBET

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 pints strawberries, stemmed and rinsed
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons agave syrup
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- Pinch sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped basil leaves (1/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In blender, puree strawberries, maple syrup, agave syrup, and balsamic vinegar until smooth. Transfer mixture to medium bowl and whisk in salt, pepper, and basil.
2. Transfer mixture to ice cream machine and freeze according to directions. Remove sorbet from canister and transfer to 9-pan. Freeze forty minutes to one hour or until sorbet firms to scoop-able consistency.
