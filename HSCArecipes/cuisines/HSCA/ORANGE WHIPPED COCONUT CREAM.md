# ORANGE WHIPPED COCONUT CREAM

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 12 ounces (1 1/2 cups) coconut milk
- 1 tablespoon agar powder
- 12 ounces (1 1/4 cups) coconut cream, melted
- 1 cup agave syrup
- 2 teaspoons orange zest (1 orange)
- 1/4 cup orange juice (1 orange)
- Pinch sea salt
- 1/4 cup coconut butter, melted

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk and agar. Set aside for at least 5 minutes.
2. Prepare ice bath. In Vitamix, combine melted coconut cream, agave syrup, orange zest, orange juice, and salt. Blend until smooth. Slowly add coconut butter and blend to combine.
3. Bring coconut-agar mixture to simmer. Simmer until all agar powder has dissolved, stirring constantly to avoid scorching.
4. Slowly add boiling coconut-agar mixture to Vitamix with other ingredients and blend to combine.
5. Using spatula, transfer mixture to medium bowl and set over ice bath until chilled.
6. Process frosting in food processor until smooth.
