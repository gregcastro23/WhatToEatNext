# Kabocha Salad

*(Fall/Winter)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds kabocha squash (1 medium), peeled, seeded, and bite-size chunks
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 6 ounces baby arugula
- 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds
- 1/4 cup crumbled firm feta cheese (2 ounces)
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- Pinch dried ginger
- 2 teaspoons canola oil
- 1/4 cup pomegranate juice (use juice from fresh pomegranate)
- 2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
- 2 tablespoons chopped shallot (1 1/2 ounces)
- 3 tablespoons ginger juice (5 ounces ginger)
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 3/4 cup canola oil

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375°F. Toss squash with oil, salt, and pepper in medium-size bowl. Arrange squash on parchment-lined half sheet pan and roast about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned and tender. Remove from oven and set aside.
2. Toss pumpkin seeds in small bowl with cumin, salt, ginger, and oil. Bake in oven until seeds are lightly toasted, 5-7 minutes.
3. Combine all dressing ingredients in blender until creamy. Toss arugula with dressing.
4. Place about 3/4 cup of squash on plate.
5. Arrange arugula on atop or alongside squash. Garnish arugula with nuts, pomegranate seeds and feta.
