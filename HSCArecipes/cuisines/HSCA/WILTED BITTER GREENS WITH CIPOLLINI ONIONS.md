# WILTED BITTER GREENS WITH CIPOLLINI ONIONS

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces Cipollini onions, root end trimmed and outer skins removed
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 pound radicchio, bite-size pieces
- 1/2 pound romaine lettuce, bite-size pieces
- 1/2 pound chicory, bite-size pieces
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 3 tablespoons rice syrup, warmed
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 3 tablespoons currants
- 3 tablespoons pistachios, lightly roasted, coarsely chopped
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375°F.
2. Toss onions with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper in medium-size bowl.
3. Transfer onions to parchment-lined half sheet pan and roast until tender. Set aside.
4. Combine radicchio, romaine, and chicory in medium bowl.
5. In separate small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar and rice syrup until well combined.
6. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil over low flame and sweat garlic with salt until garlic begins to turn golden.
7. Add salad greens to pan; sauté until just wilted. Remove pan from heat. Plate greens and lightly drizzle balsamic vinegar-rice syrup mixture over them.
8. Garnish salad with onions, currants, and pistachios. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
