# HAZELNUT-CRUSTED FLOUNDER

**Yield:** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 cups hazelnuts, toasted, skins removed
- 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 8 flounder filets, seasoned with salt and pepper
- 1/2 cup canola oil
- Mango Salsa (recipe below)

## Instructions

1. Preheat conventional oven to 375°F.
2. Combine hazelnuts and breadcrumbs in food processor. Pulse mixture until uniformly crumb-sized. Transfer mixture to half-hotel pan. Transfer flour and eggs, each to separate half hotel pans.
3. Dredge fish filet in flour, shaking off excess. Transfer filets to egg mixture, making sure to coat entire filet. Press breading onto filet, completely to coat.
4. Heat canola oil in large sauté pan over medium-high heat. Add filets to pan, careful not to crowd them. Cook each side until golden brown, approximately 1-2 minutes per side. If fish is not cooked through, can pace onto parchment-lined sheet tray and place into oven for 5 minutes.
