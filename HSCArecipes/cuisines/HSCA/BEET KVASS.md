# BEET KVASS

**Yield:** 8 cups

## Ingredients

- 2-3 medium organic beets (¾ pound), scrubbed (not peeled), chopped in 1-inch pieces
- ¼ cup whey or sauerkraut juice (optional, as starter)
- 1 tablespoon sea salt (or 1 ½ tablespoons if not using whey/juice)
- 2 quarts filtered water
- ½ gallon glass jar
- cheesecloth (enough to cover jar mouth)

## Instructions

1. Place beets in bottom of jar, add whey/juice (if using), and salt as needed.
2. Cover beets with water, place cheesecloth over mouth of jar and secure with string.
3. Set in cool dark spot for 2 days to ferment if using whey/juice or 3 days without.
4. Strain beets from liquid, and discard beets. Return liquid to jar and refrigerate.
