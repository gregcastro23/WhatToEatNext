# FRESH TOMATO SAUCE

**Yield:** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 28-ounce can whole tomatoes, drained OR 2 pounds fresh tomatoes, concassé, 1/2-inch dice
- Sea salt
- Freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan combine oil, garlic, basil and tomatoes.
2. Cook over medium heat 25 to 35 minutes until tomatoes have released their juices and water has cooked out.
3. Add salt and black pepper to taste. You can puree sauce in blender, or serve it as is.
