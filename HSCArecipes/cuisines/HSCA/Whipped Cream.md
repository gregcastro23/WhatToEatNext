# Whipped Cream

**Yield:** Serves 8

## Ingredients

- 1 pint heavy cream
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon maple crystals

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in stand mixer fitted with chilled bowl and whip until stiff peaks form.
