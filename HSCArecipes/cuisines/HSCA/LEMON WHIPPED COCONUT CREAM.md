# LEMON WHIPPED COCONUT CREAM

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 can (13.66 ounces) coconut milk
- 1 tablespoon agar powder
- 2 cans (5.4 ounces each) coconut cream, melted
- 1 cup agave syrup
- 2 teaspoons lemon zest (2 lemons)
- 1/4 cup lemon juice (2-3 lemons)
- Pinch sea salt
- 1/4 cups coconut butter, melted

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk, agar, and coconut cream. Set aside for at least 5 minutes.
2. Prepare ice bath. Bring coconut mixture to simmer constantly stirring until all the agar is dissolved.
3. In Vitamix, combine coconut mixture, agave syrup, lemon zest, lemon juice, and salt. Blend until smooth. Slowly add coconut butter and blend to combine.
4. Transfer to bowl and set over ice bath until chilled.
5. Process frosting in food processor until smooth.
