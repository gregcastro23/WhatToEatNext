# Fudgy Chocolate Cake

**Yield:** One 8-inch cake

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup maple crystals
- 6 tablespoons cocoa powder plus 2 teaspoons for dusting pan
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 cup almond milk, room temperature
- 3/4 cup maple syrup, room temperature
- 1/4 cup canola oil
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 1/4 ounces bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F (300°F in convection). Oil one 8-inch cake pan. Dust pan with 2 teaspoons of cocoa powder. Line bottom of pan with parchment paper.
2. Sift together dry ingredients in medium bowl and whisk to combine. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together wet ingredients and pour into dry. Mix well.
4. Add finely chopped chocolate and mix to combine. Pour batter into prepared pan.
5. Bake 35-40 minutes until cake is springy to touch and pulls away from sides.
6. Cool in pan on wire rack 10-15 minutes. Remove from pan and completely cool on rack.
