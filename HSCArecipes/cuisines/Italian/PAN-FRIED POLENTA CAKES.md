# PAN-FRIED POLENTA CAKES

**Yield:** approximately 8-10 portions

## Ingredients

- 1 recipe Polenta
- cornmeal for dredging
- no taste oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Pour hot polenta onto parchment-lined half-sheet tray, and spread with lightly oiled spatula to 1/2 inch thickness. Let set until firm, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
2. Cut into desired shapes using cookie cutters.
3. Spread cornmeal on plate and dredge polenta. Prepare another plate with paper towel for draining.
4. Heat 1/4 inch oil in sauté pan, and fry polenta cakes over medium high heat until golden and crisp. Flip once and fry other side.
5. Drain on paper and serve hot.
