# SAUTÉED CHICKEN BREAST MARSALA WITH CRIMINI MUSHROOMS

**Yield:** 4 portions

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts (4)
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 cup whole wheat pastry flour, sifted
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons minced shallot (2 ounces shallots)
- 1/2 pound cremini mushrooms, stemmed, thinly sliced
- 3/4 cup Marsala wine
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 2 tablespoons chilled butter, cubed
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley (1/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then lightly dust with flour and shake off excess.
2. Heat olive oil over medium-high flame in 12-inch sauté pan. Cook breasts on each side for approximately 3-4 minutes. Remove chicken from pan and set aside. Add shallots to pan and sauté for 1 minute.
3. Add mushrooms to pan and sear until caramelized. Sear in batches, if necessary. Do not crowd pan.
4. Remove pan from heat, add chicken back to pan, and pour Marsala wine over chicken. Place pan over flame again and tilt pan carefully toward flame to flambé.
5. When flames subside, add stock and simmer over medium to high heat to reduce stock au sec (almost dry), about 2 to 4 minutes.
6. Add cold butter cubes to pan one at a time and swirl until dissolved.
7. Garnish with chopped parsley.
