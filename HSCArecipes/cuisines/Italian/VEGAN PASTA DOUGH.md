# VEGAN PASTA DOUGH

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound silken tofu
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup water
- pinch sea salt
- 2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour OR 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour and 1 cup whole wheat bread flour

## Instructions

1. Blend tofu, olive oil, water, and salt in blender under smooth.
2. In food processor, pulse flour for few seconds to aerate it. Add tofu mixture and process 30 seconds. Squeeze dough into ball. Remove dough to lightly floured work surface and knead for 1 minute, or until smooth. Wrap in plastic wrap and let sit at least 15 minutes.
