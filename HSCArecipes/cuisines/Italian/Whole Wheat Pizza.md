# Whole Wheat Pizza

**Yield:** 1 thick or 2 thin pizzas

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon dry yeast
- 2/3 cup warm water
- 1 tablespoon rice syrup or honey or maple syrup
- 1/4 cup whole wheat bread flour
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 1/2 - 3 cups whole wheat pastry flour

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, mix sponge ingredients with whisk.
2. Cover with plastic wrap and rise for 20-30 minutes in warm place.
3. Add water, salt and oil to sponge. Mix together with wooden spoon. Add flour in 1/2 cup increments.
4. Remove dough from bowl, form into ball and knead on floured work surface. Dough should be soft and pliable. Knead for about 8-10 minutes.
5. Place dough in lightly-oiled bowl, cover with plastic and place in warm spot until doubled in size about 40 minutes. Punch down, shape dough and bake.
6. Preheat oven to 475°F. Place pizza stone in oven to heat up at least 30-40 minutes before baking.
7. Transfer pizza to peel before adding sauce and toppings.
8. Bake on pizza stone from 8-10 minutes, turning once at half-way point.
