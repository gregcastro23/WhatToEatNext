# MEDITERRANEAN-INSPIRED PASTA SALAD

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 head garlic roasted, garlic paste extracted
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest (same lemon for juice)
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice (1 lemon)
- 2 tablespoons pine nuts
- 2 tablespoons agave syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon fennel seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 1 pound eggplant (1 small), peeled, 1/2-inch dice
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 12 ounces broccoli rabe (1/2 bunch), stemmed and chopped
- 6 cups salted water (1 tablespoon sea salt)
- 8 ounces gluten-free or whole wheat fusilli
- 1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes, rehydrated in hot water until soft, drained, chopped
- 1/4 cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
- 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
- 1/4 cup currants
- 1/2 cup loosely packed fresh basil, chiffonade (1 ounce)
- Sea salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In blender, combine all dressing ingredients until creamy. Set aside.
2. Heat oven to 350°F.
3. Toss eggplant in bowl with olive oil and salt. Transfer to parchment-lined half sheet pan and roast in oven until eggplant is tender (about 20 minutes). Set aside.
4. Bring 1-gallon pot of salted water to boil. Add broccoli rabe and blanch about 2-3 minutes. Shock in ice water. Wring excess moisture from broccoli rabe and add to bowl with dressing.
5. In same pot, boil pasta for 8-10 minutes or until al dente. Drain and transfer to bowl with dressing and broccoli rabe; toss to combine.
6. Add eggplant, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, pine nuts, currants, and basil to pasta, broccoli rabe, and dressing. Toss to combine. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve at room temperature.
