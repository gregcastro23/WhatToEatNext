# Spinach Pasta

**Yield:** 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch spinach (3/4 pound), washed, stemmed, cooked until tender, drained, and finely chopped
- 3 large eggs, beaten
- 2 1/2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour OR half all-purpose flour/half whole wheat bread flour

## Instructions

1. Press spinach with your hand to squeeze out any excess liquid. Mix spinach with eggs and set aside.
2. Pulse flour in food processor to evenly distribute and aerate. Add spinach/egg mixture; process until dough forms rough ball, about 30 seconds. If dough resembles small pebbles, add water, 1/2 teaspoon at a time; if dough sticks to side of bowl, add flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, and process until dough forms rough ball.
3. Turn dough ball onto work surface; knead until dough is smooth, 1 to 2 minutes. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside for at least 15 minutes and up to 2 hours to relax.
