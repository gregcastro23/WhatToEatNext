# Bolognese Sauce with Chicken

**Yield:** 7 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 8 ounces ground chicken (dark meat preferred)
- 12 ounces onion (1 large), minced
- 4 ounces carrot (1/2 large), minced
- 3 tablespoons minced garlic (3 large cloves)
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1 teaspoon fennel seeds, ground
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 7 ounces tomato paste (3/4 cup)
- 2 (28 ounce) cans whole tomatoes (including juice)
- 2 tablespoons rice syrup
- 4 leaves fresh basil, torn
- Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat 3 tablespoons of oil over medium flame. Add ground chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring often to prevent any scorching. Remove chicken from pan.
2. Add remaining tablespoon of oil over medium-high flame.
3. Add onion and carrots. Sauté until golden.
4. Lower flame to medium, add garlic, salt, fennel, oregano, and tomato paste. Sweat until all vegetables are tender.
5. Add ground chicken back to pot and mix to incorporate. Continue to sweat few minutes longer.
6. In Vitamix, blend whole tomatoes and juices.
7. Add puree to pot and simmer until thickened to desired consistency.
8. Add rice syrup and basil. Simmer few minutes more. Adjust seasoning to taste.
