# BROILED AND/OR BAKED POLENTA

**Yield:** approximately 8-10 portions

## Ingredients

- 1 recipe Polenta
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon + 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
- pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat broiler or preheat oven to 350° F.
2. Pour hot polenta onto parchment-lined half-sheet tray, and spread with lightly oiled spatula to 1/2 inch thickness. Let set until firm, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
3. Combine garlic, olive oil, and salt. Set aside.
4. Cut polenta into desired shapes using cookie cutters.
5. Transfer cut polenta to parchment-lined sheet tray for baking or lightly oiled sheet tray (no parchment) for broiling. Brush top side with garlic oil.
6. Broil or bake polenta until golden and crisp (approximately 2 minutes in broiler or 10-15 minutes in the oven). Flip over with spatula, brush other side with oil, and broil.
7. Serve hot.
