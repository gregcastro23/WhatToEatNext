# Tomato Sauce

**Yield:** 4 cups sauce

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 large onion, small dice
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon dry rosemary
- 1/4 teaspoon dry thyme
- 1/4 teaspoon dry basil
- 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
- 1 28-ounce can peeled tomatoes, chopped (pulsed in food processor)
- 2 cups red wine
- sea salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in 1-gallon pot over low heat. Add onion, garlic and salt. Sweat onion and garlic for 5-10 minutes, covered, stirring occasionally.
2. Add rosemary, thyme, basil, tomatoes, and wine. Bring sauce to simmer.
3. Simmer sauce until flavors are well developed, approximately 40 minutes to 1 hour.
4. Add salt and pepper.
