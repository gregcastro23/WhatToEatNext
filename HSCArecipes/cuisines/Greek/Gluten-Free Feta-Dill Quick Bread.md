# Gluten-Free Feta-Dill Quick Bread

**Yield:** 6 muffin-size quick breads

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Gluten-Free Flour Blend (see previous recipe)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1 cup organic whole milk
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 6 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup chopped dill

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Mix together flour, baking powder, and salt in large bowl.
3. In separate bowl, combine olive oil, milk, egg, and feta cheese. Add wet mixture to dry, and mix dough to combine. Add dill.
4. Pour batter into muffin molds and immediately transfer back to oven to bake.
5. Bake for at least 30-40 minutes. Bread is ready when toothpick inserted in bread comes out clean.
