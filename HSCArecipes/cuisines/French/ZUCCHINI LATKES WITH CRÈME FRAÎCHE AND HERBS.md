# ZUCCHINI LATKES WITH CRÈME FRAÎCHE AND HERBS

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 20 1-inch latkes

## Ingredients

- 1 pound zucchini, shredded (large side of grater)
- 1/4 cup minced red onion (2 ounces)
- 1 tablespoon chopped dill (1/4 ounce)
- 1 large egg
- 6 tablespoons rice flour
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- Canola oil for frying (2/3 cup)
- 3-4 tablespoons (1 ounce) crème fraîche
- 20 dill fronds

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, combine zucchini, onions, dill, egg, flour, salt, and pepper. Drain in colander for 10 minutes and squeeze out excess liquid.
2. Form latkes using tablespoon measure.
3. In 8-inch, non-stick sauté pan, heat oil over medium-high flame. Fry latkes in batches until golden on each side, about 2 minutes. Drain on paper towels and serve with dollop of crème fraîche and sprig of dill.
