# Meringue Frosting

**Yield:** 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites (about 1/2 cup), room temperature
- 1 cup agave, room temperature
- 1/4 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- Pinch teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, whisk egg whites, agave, vinegar, salt, and cream of tartar. Place bowl over 2 1/2 quart pot of boiling water. Make sure water underneath bowl is rapidly boiling.
2. Whisk mixture for about 10 minutes (mixture will become white and frothy).
3. Remove from heat and add vanilla extract.
4. Using hand mixer, whip at medium-high speed until light and fluffy, about 4-5 minutes.
