# ROMAINE SALAD WITH SHERRY VINAIGRETTE

**Yield:** ___ cups, ___ 1-cup servings

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup sherry vinegar
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon minced shallot
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1 head Romaine lettuce, chopped
- 2 ribs celery, diagonal slice
- 1 apple, small dice
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup pecans, toasted and chopped

## Instructions

1. Whisk together vinegar, mustard, shallot, and salt in bowl. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking until dressing is properly emulsified.
2. Toss lettuce, celery, and apple in bowl with enough dressing to coat.
3. Divide salad into portions, ladling on extra dressing as needed. Garnish each salad with raisins and pecans.
