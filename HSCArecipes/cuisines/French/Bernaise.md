# Bernaise

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon shallots
- 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon
- 1/2 teaspoon white peppercorn crushed in saucepan

## Instructions

1. Reduce all ingredients at simmer to 1-2 tablespoons.
2. Cool reduction and strain before adding to yolks in Hollandaise recipe (above).
