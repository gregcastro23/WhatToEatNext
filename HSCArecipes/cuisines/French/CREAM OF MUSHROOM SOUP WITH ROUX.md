# CREAM OF MUSHROOM SOUP WITH ROUX

**Yield:** 4 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 6 ounces shallots (4), fine dice
- 1 pound cremini mushrooms, stemmed, sliced
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 cup sherry
- 1/4 cup oat flour
- 4 cups stock
- Salt and pinch freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (1/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil on medium flame. Sweat shallots and mushrooms with salt until tender, about 10-12 minutes. Add sherry, reducing until almost dry. Add flour, and stir 5 to 7 minutes.
2. Add stock, 1 cup at a time, whisking to blend. Raise heat and bring soup to boil. Reduce heat, and simmer, with lid ajar, for 30 minutes.
3. Puree soup in blender until creamy.
4. Return soup to pot. Re-heat and season to taste with salt and pepper.
5. Garnish with parsley.
