# GREENS WITH CUCUMBER ICE AND LIME VINAIGRETTE

*(SPRING/SUMMER)*

**Yield:** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon lime juice (1 lime)
- 3 tablespoons rice syrup
- 1 English cucumber (7 ounces), seeded and cut into chunks
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 teaspoons chopped mint (1/4 ounce)
- 1/4 cup lime juice (4 limes)
- 2 tablespoons rice syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 head hydroponic lettuce, leaves separated
- 1/2 pint grape tomatoes, halved lengthwise
- 1 avocado (8 slices), drizzled with 1 tablespoon lime juice (1 lime)
- 15 whole cilantro leaves (1/4 ounce)
- 25 whole mint leaves (3/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, heat lime juice and rice syrup over medium heat until syrup has dissolved.
2. Puree cucumber in Vitamix with rice syrup-lime juice mixture, salt, and mint. Blend until smooth.
3. Transfer mixture to ice cream maker and process until frozen. Set aside in freezer.
4. In small bowl, combine dressing ingredients – lime juice, rice syrup, salt, and pepper. Slowly whisk in oil until dressing emulsifies.
5. Arrange salad greens on plate. Scoop cucumber ice into center. Drizzle with dressing. Garnish with tomatoes, avocado, cilantro, and mint leaves.
