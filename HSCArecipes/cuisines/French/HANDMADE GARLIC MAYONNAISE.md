# HANDMADE GARLIC MAYONNAISE

**Yield:** approximately 2/3-1 1/4 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon minced garlic (2 cloves)
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 egg yolks, room temperature
- optional: 1/2 teaspoon Dijon-type mustard
- 1/3-1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil (Troy brand Turkish olive oil OR Spectrum brand Californian olive oil)
- 1 1/2-2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Smash garlic with salt to paste on cutting board.
2. Put garlic paste in bowl together with egg yolks and mustard; whisk until smooth.
3. Slowly add oil (no more than one teaspoon at a time) to egg yolk mixture, whisking until well incorporated before adding more oil.
4. As mixture begins to thicken, oil can be added somewhat more rapidly (no more than one tablespoon at a time).
5. When all oil has been incorporated, whisk in lemon juice.
