# Velouté

**Yield:** 5 cups

## Ingredients

- 5 ounces (10 tablespoons) butter
- 4 ounces (3/4 cup) all-purpose flour
- 8 cups chicken stock, cooled
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 12-inch sauté pan, melt butter over medium-low flame. Sprinkle in flour and stir for about 3-4 minutes.
2. Slowly whisk in stock 1/2 cup at a time, waiting for sauce to thicken between each addition, until sauce is smooth.
3. Lower heat and simmer about 35-40 minutes or until sauce achieves nappé consistency.
4. Adjust seasonings to taste.
