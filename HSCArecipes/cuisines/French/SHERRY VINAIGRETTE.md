# SHERRY VINAIGRETTE

**Yield:** 1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon

## Ingredients

- 3/4 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon style mustard
- 3 tablespoons sherry vinegar
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon + 1 1/2 teaspoons Grade A maple syrup
- 4 tablespoons + 1 1/2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. In medium mixing bowl, combine garlic, mustard, vinegar, salt, and maple syrup. Gradually whisk in olive oil.
