# BLENDER MAYONNAISE

**Yield:** approximately 1 1/3 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk, room temperature
- 1 whole egg, room temperature
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- optional: 1 teaspoon Dijon-type mustard
- 1 cup refined, expeller-pressed oil

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients except oil in blender and process until completely smooth.
2. With motor running, add oil very slowly, in steady stream. As mixture starts to thicken, oil can be added a bit more quickly.
