# Vegan Béchamel

**Yield:** 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons coconut oil
- 1 ounce shallot (1 small), minced
- 1 1/2 ounces (1/4 cup) oat flour
- 6 cups Fresh Almond Milk, cooled
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- Pinch nutmeg
- 2-3 tablespoons lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart sauce pan, heat oil over medium flame. Sweat shallots until translucent.
2. Add flour and cook over low heat, stirring constantly for about 5 minutes.
3. Slowly whisk in almond milk with thyme, bay leaf, and nutmeg.
4. Increase heat to simmer and continue cooking for about 20-25 minutes, stirring often until sauce thickens.
5. Strain sauce through chinois. Add lemon juice. Season to taste.
