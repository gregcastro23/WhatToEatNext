# Avocado and Coconut Terrine

**Yield:** 1 terrine (1 quart per layer)

## Ingredients

- Avocado Layer
- 4 avocados
- 3/4 cup lime juice (8 limes)
- Pinch sea salt
- 3/4 cup coconut milk (6 ounces)
- 3/4 cup coconut cream (4.75 ounces)
- 1 cup agave syrup
- Coconut Layer
- 3 cups coconut milk, room temperature (two 13.66-ounce cans)
- Pinch sea salt
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon coconut extract
- 1/3 cup maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Avocado layer: Blend avocados, lime juice, salt, coconut milk, coconut cream, and agave syrup in Vitamix until creamy.
2. Transfer mixture to ice cream machine and freeze until scoop-able consistency is achieved. Transfer mixture to half hotel pan and freeze for 20 minutes or until mixture firms to scoop-able consistency.
3. Coconut layer: In medium bowl, whisk together coconut milk, salt, vanilla extract, coconut extract, and maple syrup.
4. Transfer to ice cream machine and freeze until scoop-able consistency is achieved. Transfer to half hotel pan and freeze until mixture firms to scoop-able consistency.
5. Brush terrine mold lightly with oil and line with parchment paper. Spread avocado sorbet evenly in bottom of terrine mold. Swirl in coconut sorbet. Smooth surface of terrine and transfer to freezer to set until firm.
