# SMOKED TROUT TERRINE

**Yield:** 1 terrine (3 x 11 inches) 8-10 servings

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup heavy cream (5 fluid ounces)
- Pinch saffron
- 14 ounces smoked trout
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 2 egg whites
- 8 ounces shrimp, peeled, deveined, roughly chopped
- 8 ounces scallops, cleaned, roughly chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (1/4 ounce)
- 2 tablespoons chopped basil (1/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 300°F. Line terrine mold with plastic. Prepare an ice bath.
2. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, heat cream to 160°F and add pinch of saffron. Transfer pot to ice bath and cool cream to room temperature.
3. Combine trout, lemon juice, salt, pepper, egg whites, and infused cream in food processor.
4. Puree until smooth and fully combined. Transfer puree to bowl. Fold in shrimp, scallops, parsley, and basil. Transfer mixture to terrine mold. Fold ends of plastic wrap or parchment over top of terrine.
5. Poach terrine in 170°F water bath for about 35 minutes.
6. Remove terrine from water bath and cool before refrigerating at least 3-4 hours.
