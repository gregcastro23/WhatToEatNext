# Baked Meringue Shells

## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound (4 ounces) egg whites
- 1/2 pound (8 ounces) maple crystals

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 225°F and line sheet tray with parchment paper.
2. Beat egg whites to soft peaks, then add sweetener one tablespoon at a time until incorporated and meringue is stiff.
3. Using pastry bag or spoon, pipe or spoon meringue on sheet tray. Bake until crisp (minimum 2 hours).
4. Cool shells completely and remove from parchment.
