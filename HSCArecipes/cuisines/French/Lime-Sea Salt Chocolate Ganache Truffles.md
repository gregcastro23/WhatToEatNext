# Lime-Sea Salt Chocolate Ganache Truffles

**Yield:** 40 truffles

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup coconut milk
- 1/4 teaspoon lime zest, tightly packed
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 cups finely chopped semisweet chocolate
- Chocolate Coating (recipe following)
- Zest of 2 limes
- About 1 tablespoon Maldon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Bring coconut milk, 1/4 tsp lime zest, and 1/4 tsp salt to simmer in 2-quart pot.
2. Remove coconut milk from heat. Add chocolate. Stir with spatula until smooth.
3. Pour chocolate mixture into shallow baking pan and chill in freezer for 30 minutes, or until firm enough to scoop.
4. Form 1-inch diameter truffles using small half-ounce scoop or teaspoon.
5. Return truffles to freezer to firm.
6. Dip truffles in Chocolate Coating (recipe following) and garnish with lime zest and sea salt. Serve truffles at room temperature.
