# Mint-Chocolate Ganache Truffles

**Yield:** 40 truffles

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup coconut milk
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 cups finely chopped semisweet chocolate
- 1/3 teaspoon peppermint extract
- Chocolate Coating (recipe following)

## Instructions

1. Bring coconut milk and salt to simmer in 2-quart pot.
2. Remove coconut milk from heat. Add chocolate and mint extract. Stir with spatula until smooth.
3. Pour chocolate mixture into shallow baking pan and chill in freezer for 30 minutes, or until firm enough to scoop.
4. Form 1-inch diameter truffles using small half-ounce scoop or teaspoon.
5. Return truffles to freezer to firm.
6. Dip truffles in Chocolate Coating (recipe following). Serve truffles at room temperature.
