# Polenta, Goat Cheese, and Black Bean Terrine

**Yield:** 1 loaf pan (9 x 5 inches) (10-12 servings)

## Ingredients

- 1 cup black beans, soaked, rinsed, drained
- 3 cups water
- 3 bay leaves
- 3 tablespoons minced garlic (3 large cloves)
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne
- 1/4 cup whole wheat breadcrumbs (optional)
- 1 quart water
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1 cup polenta
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground white pepper
- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne
- 1 cup cilantro leaves, roughly chopped
- 10.5 ounces (1 log) goat cheese, crumbled
- 2 red peppers (10 ounces), roasted, peeled, seeded, and pureed in blender
- 16 sprigs fresh cilantro (1/4 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Combine beans in small pressure cooker with water, bay leaves, garlic, and salt. Bring to pressure, lower heat, and cook 15 minutes. Bring down pressure, remove bay leaf, and drain beans in colander. Transfer beans to food processor.
2. Melt butter in 8-inch sauté pan over low flame. Add cumin, salt, pepper, oregano, thyme, basil, and cayenne. When spices are fragrant (30 seconds), add them to beans. Puree beans in food processor. Stiffen mixture with breadcrumbs if needed to create texture in which spoon will stand.
3. In 2-quart saucepan, bring water and salt to boil. Slowly whisk in polenta. Exchange whisk for spoon and cook another 20 minutes, stirring frequently. Add in butter, coriander, white pepper, and cayenne and cook 5 minutes more. Adjust seasonings.
4. Line loaf pan with parchment paper.
5. Pour two-thirds polenta into terrine mold. Fold goat cheese and cilantro into remaining one-third polenta. Spread in mold as second layer. Spread bean puree in mold as third layer. Refrigerate until thoroughly chilled, about 1 hour.
6. Garnish with cilantro sprigs. Serve with crackers and roasted red pepper puree.
