# SAUCE ESPAGNOLE (BROWN SAUCE)

**Yield:** 1 quart

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 ounces onion, medium dice
- 1 ounce carrot, medium dice
- 1 ounce celery, medium dice
- 4 ounces tomatoes (1 Roma tomato), concassé
- 8 cups brown stock, warmed
- 4 ounces brown roux (2 ounces flour + 1.5 ounces olive oil), cooled
- Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil over medium-high flame. Sauté onions, carrots, and celery until golden.
2. Add tomatoes and sauté for few minutes longer or until slightly caramelized.
3. Add 7 cups stock and bring to boil. Lower heat to simmer.
4. Slowly whisk remaining cup of warmed stock with cooled roux.
5. Slowly add roux-stock mixture back into sauce.
6. Simmer uncovered about 30 minutes or until reduced by half and nappé is achieved.
7. Strain through chinois. Adjust seasoning to taste.
