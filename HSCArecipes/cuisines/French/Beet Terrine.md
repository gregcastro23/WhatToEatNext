# Beet Terrine

**Yield:** 9 4-ounce ramekins

## Ingredients

- 1 cup grapefruit juice, strained (1 1/2 - 2 grapefruits)
- 2 cups orange juice, strained (approximately 8 oranges)
- 1 cup beet juice (without foam), strained (approximately 1 pound beets)
- 2 tablespoons agave syrup
- 1 1/4 teaspoons agar powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons chives, chopped (1/4 ounce)
- 1/4 ounce micro-greens for garnish

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart saucepan, combine grapefruit, orange, and beet juices with agave. Cover pan and bring to boil.
2. Reduce heat to simmer and add agar powder, stirring continuously for 5-10 minutes or until agar is dissolved. Add salt and stir to combine.
3. Pour 1/2 cup of mixture into each ramekin. Refrigerate until mixture sets, about 20 minutes. Using small offset spatula, carefully remove terrine from ramekin mold.
4. Top each terrine with dollop of sour cream, chives and micro-greens.
