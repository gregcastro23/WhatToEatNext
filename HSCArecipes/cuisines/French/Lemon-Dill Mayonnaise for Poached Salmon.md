# Lemon-Dill Mayonnaise (for Poached Salmon)

*Fall/Winter*

**Yield:** approximately 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoon warm water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup canola oil
- 2-3 tablespoon fresh dill, minced
- zest from 1 lemon

## Instructions

1. Whisk together egg yolk, mustard, lemon juice, water and salt in bowl.
2. Drop by drop add oil, whisking constantly. When half the oil is used up, mayonnaise will be thick enough to add remainder about one tablespoon at a time, until it’s all used up. Add lemon zest. Refrigerate.
