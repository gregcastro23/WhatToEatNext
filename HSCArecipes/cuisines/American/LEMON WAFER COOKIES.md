# LEMON WAFER COOKIES

*(Gluten-Free)*

**Yield:** approximately 15 cookies

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup blanched almonds, toasted and cooled
- 3/4 cup sorghum flour
- 1/4 cup arrowroot
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- pinch sea salt
- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- zest of 2 lemons
- juice of 2 lemons (approximately 1/3 cup)
- 3-4 tablespoons almond slices, toasted

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F. Line 2 sheet trays with parchment paper.
2. In food processor, grind almonds to fine meal with flours, baking soda, and salt.
3. In separate smaller bowl, whisk together wet ingredients.
4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients in processor (mixture will be loose). Chill dough in refrigerator for 10-15 minutes.
5. Using 1-ounce ice cream scoop, scoop batter onto sheet tray, leaving room between each cookie as batter spreads.
6. Sprinkle each cookie with few almond slices.
7. Cook 15 minutes or until cookies are slightly golden. Cool before removing from parchment paper.
