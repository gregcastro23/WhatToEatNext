# AMARETTI COOKIES

**Yield:** approximately 16 two-inch cookies

## Ingredients

- 3 large egg whites
- 10 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- pinch salt
- 1 cup almond flour
- 3/4 cup pignoli nuts

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 325°F. Line 2 half-sheet pans with Silpats.
2. Beat egg whites to soft peaks; add maple crystals one tablespoon at a time and continue whipping until incorporated.
3. Add extracts and salt; whip 10 seconds more.
4. Using rubber spatula, fold almond flour into egg white mixture. Mixture will be soft and 'meringue-like' in consistency.
5. Scoop dough onto sheet tray with 1-ounce ice cream scoop. Top each cookie with 8-10 pignoli nuts.
6. Bake 18-20 minutes or until cookies are golden. Cool cookies before carefully removing from pan with metal offset spatula.
