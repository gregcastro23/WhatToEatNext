# BUCKWHEAT PANCAKES

*Vegan*

**Yield:** 40 three-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 cups sifted unbleached white flour
- 1 cup buckwheat flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 teaspoons maple crystals
- 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 3 cups almond milk
- 1/2 very ripe banana, puréed

## Instructions

1. In medium bowl, combine flours, baking powder, salt, baking soda, and maple crystals. Set aside.
2. In separate bowl, combine apple cider vinegar, and almond milk. Let sit for 5-10 minutes. Add banana.
3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Beat batter only until blended.
4. Using 1-ounce ladle, pour batter onto griddle. Cook cakes until bubbles in batter break on t surface; flip and cook until browned.
