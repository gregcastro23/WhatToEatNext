# WALNUT TEA COOKIES

**Yield:** approximately 40-60 small cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold butter (2 sticks)
- 1/2 cup powdered maple crystals
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1/4 cup brown rice flour
- 3/4 cup walnuts, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Line 2 half-sheet trays with parchment paper.
2. Cream butter and maple crystals in stand mixer until fluffy and well combined, about 5 minutes. Add vanilla and salt until mixed.
3. In separate smaller bowl, whisk together flours. Slowly sift flours into butter mixture in stand mixer, until all flour is incorporated. Add in walnuts. Divide dough in 2 and wrap each in plastic. Refrigerate 10-15 minutes.
4. Remove 1 dough from refrigerator. Roll between parchment paper to 1/4" thickness. Freeze dough until hard. Cut shapes from dough using cookie cutters. Repeat process with second dough.
5. Bake about 6 minutes. Rotate baking pans and continue to bake about 5-6 more minutes or until lightly golden. Remove cookies from pans to racks to cool.
