# JAM DOT COOKIES

**Yield:** approximately 20 2-inch cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 cup almonds
- 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 cup rolled oats
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ cup extra virgin canola oil, olive oil, melted butter OR 6 tablespoons melted coconut oil
- ½ cup maple syrup
- Pinch sea salt
- Apricot jam (about ½ cup)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line 2 half-sheet trays with parchment paper.
2. Grind almonds, flour, oats, and cinnamon in food processor to fine meal.
3. In separate small bowl, whisk together oil, maple syrup, and sea salt. Add liquid to dry mixture in food processor and mix until well combined.
4. Form cookies using level 1-ounce scoop. Round them into walnut sized balls. Place on baking sheet and form indentation in middle of cookie using floured ¼ teaspoon measure. Fill indentation with about ½ teaspoon of jam and bake 15-20 minutes or until golden.
