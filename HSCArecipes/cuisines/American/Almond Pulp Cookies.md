# Almond Pulp Cookies

## Ingredients

- 2 cups almond pulp (left over from making Rich Almond Milk)
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds
- Pinch salt
- 2 tablespoons melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Combine all ingredients in bowl with spatula.
2. Form cookies on parchment-lined 1/2 sheet pan with 1-ounce scoop.
3. Bake cookies 10-12 minutes, or until lightly golden.
