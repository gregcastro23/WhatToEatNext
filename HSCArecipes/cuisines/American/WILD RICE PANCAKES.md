# WILD RICE PANCAKES

**Yield:** approximately 24 pancakes for 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 cup unbleached white flour
- 2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup cooked wild rice (from 1/3 cup raw rice)
- 1 cup organic whole milk
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons canola oil OR melted butter + more for frying
- 2 large egg whites

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, whisk together flour, maple crystals, baking powder, soda and salt.
2. Roughly chop cooked rice (needs to be dry, fluffy before adding to batter) and mix with dry ingredients.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together milk and lemon juice. Let stand 15 minutes. Add 2 tablespoons of oil or butter.
4. Gradually add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir to combine. Whisk egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into combined ingredients.
5. Heat griddle or large non-stick pan over medium heat. Brush little of remaining tablespoon of oil over surface of pan.
6. Using 1-ounce ladle, pour batter onto griddle. Cook cakes until bubbles in batter break on surface; flip and cook until browned.
