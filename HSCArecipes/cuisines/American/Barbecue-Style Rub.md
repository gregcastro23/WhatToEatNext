# Barbecue-Style Rub

**Yield:** 3/4 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon black pepper, ground
- Pinch cayenne
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 tablespoons cumin, ground
- 3 tablespoons maple sugar
- 1 tablespoon oregano, ground
- 4 tablespoons paprika (not hot)
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper, ground
- 1/3 cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Whisk together all ingredients.
2. Toss food being grilled in rub (tempeh, tofu, chicken, or fish) in spice mixture. Let food marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour before grilling.
