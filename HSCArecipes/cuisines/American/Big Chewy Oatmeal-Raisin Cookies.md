# Big Chewy Oatmeal-Raisin Cookies

**Yield:** 24 small cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 stick unsalted butter, softened but still firm
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
- 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
- 3/4 cup raisins (optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350° F (300° F in convection oven). Prepare 2 half sheet trays with parchment paper.
2. In stand mixer, beat butter with sugars until creamy, approximately 3 minutes. Beat in egg.
3. Separately whisk flour, salt, baking powder, and nutmeg together. Add dry ingredients into butter-sugar mixture. Stir in oats and optional raisins.
4. Form dough into 24 (1-inch) balls using 1-ounce scoop, placing each cookie every 2 inches apart on sheet trays.
5. Bake until cookie edges turn golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes. Halfway during baking, rotate trays front to back and switch them from top to bottom.
6. Slide cookies cooling rack and let cool at least 15 minutes before serving.
