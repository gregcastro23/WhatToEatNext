# Fresh Ketchup (for Red Lentil and Toasted Sunflower Burger)

**Yield:** 2 Cups (sixteen 1-ounce servings)

## Ingredients

- 1 red onion (4 ounces), finely chopped
- 1 1/4 cup vegetable stock
- 4 ounces tomato paste
- 2 tablespoons agave syrup
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- Pinch of sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine onion and vegetable stock in 2-quart sauce pot. Bring mixture to boil, reduce to simmer, and cook for 15 minutes or until onion mixture is soft and reduced to 1/3 cup.
2. Add remaining ingredients to pot and simmer for 10 minutes more. Refrigerate.
