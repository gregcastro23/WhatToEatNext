# CORNMEAL PANCAKES (JOHNNY CAKES)

**Yield:** Twelve 4-inch cakes

## Ingredients

- 1 cup yellow cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1-2 tablespoons maple crystals
- 1 1/4 cups boiling water
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup organic milk
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1/2 cup unbleached white flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- Zest of 1 lemon, optional
- 1 cup blueberries, optional

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, whisk cornmeal, salt and maple crystals. Slowly add boiling water. Cover and let stand for 10 minutes.
2. In separate bowl, beat together egg, milk and melted butter. Add to cornmeal mixture.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking powder and stir into batter with few swift strokes. Add lemon zest and blueberries, if using.
4. Using 1-ounce ladle, pour batter onto griddle. Cook cakes until bubbles in batter break on surface; flip and cook until browned.
