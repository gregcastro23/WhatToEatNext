# GHIRARDELLI AWARD WINNING CHOCOLATE BROWNIES

**Yield:** 10-12 brownies

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- 3/4 cup sweet ground cocoa
- 2/3 cup un-sifted flour
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375° F (325° F in convection oven). Grease and flour 8 x 8-inch square baking pan and line with parchment paper.
2. Whisk eggs with sugar and vanilla; add melted butter.
3. Whisk together dry ingredients (cocoa, flour, baking powder, and salt) and fold into egg mixture. Add nuts.
4. Bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in middle of brownie comes out clean.
