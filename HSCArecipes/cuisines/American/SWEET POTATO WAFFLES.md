# SWEET POTATO WAFFLES

**Yield:** 6 waffles

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup boiled, food-milled sweet potato
- 3 eggs, separated
- 1 cup organic milk
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons cornmeal
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons maple crystals

## Instructions

1. Heat waffle iron.
2. In large bowl, whisk together sweet potatoes, egg yolks, milk and butter.
3. In separate bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, cornmeal, salt, baking powder, and maple crystals.
4. Mix wet ingredients into dry until batter is smooth. Set aside.
5. Whisk egg whites until soft peaks form. Fold egg whites into mixed batter.
6. Using 2 or 3-ounce ladle, pour batter onto waffle iron and cook 5-6 minutes, or until crispy.
