# CHICKEN KOTLETKY (CHICKEN BURGERS)

**Yield:** 15 (1/4 cup sized) burgers

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup whole wheat breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 1/2 pounds ground chicken or turkey
- 8 ounces onion (1 medium), grated
- 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 pint sour cream (optional)
- 15 sprigs fresh dill (1/2 ounce)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350°F.
2. Toss bread crumbs and milk in medium-size bowl until bread has soaked up all milk. Combine bread crumb mixture with ground chicken, onions, dill weed, salt, and pepper.
3. Using 1/4 cup measure, portion chicken burgers.
4. Heat oil in 12-inch sauté pan over medium-high flame, and lightly brown patties on both sides.
5. Transfer burgers from sauté pan to half sheet tray lined with parchment.
6. Transfer burgers to oven and bake for 15 minutes.
7. Garnish with sour cream and fresh dill sprigs.
