# REAL BUTTERMILK WAFFLES

**Yield:** 6 waffles

## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon maple crystals
- 3 eggs, separated
- 12 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 cups buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Heat waffle iron.
2. In large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt, and maple crystals.
3. In separate bowl whisk egg yolks, butter, and buttermilk.
4. Mix wet ingredients with dry until batter is smooth. Set aside.
5. Beat eggs whites until soft peaks form. Fold egg whites into mixed batter.
6. Using 2 or 3-ounce ladle, pour batter onto waffle iron and cook 5-6 minutes, or until crispy.
