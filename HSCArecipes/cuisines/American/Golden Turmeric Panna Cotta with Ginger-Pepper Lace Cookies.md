# Golden Turmeric 'Panna Cotta' with Ginger-Pepper Lace Cookies

**Yield:** Serves 6 to 8

## Ingredients

- 1 tsp canola oil
- 3 Tbsp agar flakes
- 2 1/2 cups Hemp Coconut 'Milk' (see recipe)
- 12 cardamom pods, crushed
- 1 Tbsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 tsp ginger powder
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 1 vanilla bean, scraped
- pinch of sea salt
- 1/2 cup pecans, toasted and coarsely chopped
- 5 Tbsp whole wheat pastry flour
- 2 Tbsp arrowroot powder
- 2 Tbsp melted coconut oil
- 1/4 cup brown rice syrup
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp ginger powder
- 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
- pinch sea salt
- 2 pints blackberries
- 1/2 cup maple syrup
- 1 container blackberries, halved lengthwise
- 1 container gold dust and brush
- 1 bunch mint

## Instructions

1. Grease inside of small silicone molds with oil.
2. Combine agar flakes and milk in medium saucepan and simmer over medium heat until agar flakes have dissolved. Add cardamom, turmeric, ginger, maple syrup, vanilla bean, and salt. Simmer an additional 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
3. Strain mixture through chinois and pour into silicone molds. Cool until firm.
4. In blender, puree blackberries and maple syrup. Strain coulis and transfer to squeeze bottle.
5. Preheat convection oven to 300°F. Line 5 half-sheet trays with Silpats.
6. Transfer pecans to medium bowl. Add flour and arrowroot. Whisk to combine.
7. In separate bowl, whisk oil, rice syrup, maple syrup, vanilla, ginger, black pepper, and salt. Add to pecan/flour mixture, mixing until combined. Let batter sit for 15 minutes.
8. Use teaspoon to drop batter onto Silpat-lined baking sheets. Place only 5 cookies per sheet – one in each corner and one in middle. Bake one sheet at a time for 10 minutes until cookies are bubbly.
9. Cool cookies for approximately 30 seconds before removing from Silpats. Working quickly (because cookies cool and set hard very fast), remove tuiles from sheet tray using small offset spatula and wrap around cannoli molds. Repeat procedure with remaining sheet trays until all batter is used.
10. Invert panna cotta onto plate. Garnish with tuile, blackberries, gold dust, blackberry coulis and mint sprigs.
