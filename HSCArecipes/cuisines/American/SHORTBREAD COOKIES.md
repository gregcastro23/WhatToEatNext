# SHORTBREAD COOKIES

**Yield:** 24 medium cookies

## Ingredients

- 10 tablespoons (1 1/4 sticks) butter, softened
- 1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons powdered maple crystals
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300° F and line half sheet tray with parchment paper.
2. Cream butter, sugar, vanilla and salt in stand mixer.
3. Gradually sift in flour until well mixed.
4. Firmly press dough into flattened 6-inch oval in center of sheet tray. Cut dough into 16 equal-sized slices and pierce each slice with fork in decorative design (be sure to pierce through to pan to allow excess steam to escape).
5. Bake about 45-50 minutes or until entire dough is golden brown and firm to touch.
6. Remove from oven and cool on wire rack. Retrace cuts in dough to loosen cookies but allow to remain on sheet tray to fully cool (do not move cookies as they are very fragile at this stage).
