# SWEET AND SOUR MANGO BARBEQUE SAUCE

**Yield:** 3 1/2 cups

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon canola oil
- 4 ounces onion (1/2 small), minced
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic (1 large clove)
- 1 small jalapeno, seeded, ribs removed, minced
- 1 tablespoon ground instant coffee
- 3 tablespoons coconut sugar
- 1/4 cup (2 ounces), Worcestershire sauce
- 1 mango, skinned and pitted
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup orange juice (2 oranges)
- 1 cup organic ketchup
- 2 tablespoon maple syrup
- Sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon pot, heat oil on medium flame. Add onions, salt, chili powder, paprika, and cumin. Sweat until onions become translucent.
2. Add garlic and jalapeno and continue sweating until all vegetables are tender.
3. Reduce flame to low and add coffee and coconut sugar to pot and simmer for 5 minutes.
4. In a blender, combine the Worcestershire sauce, mangos, water and orange juice. Blend until creamy.
5. Add blended mixture to pot along with ketchup and maple syrup.
6. Simmer sauce 10-15 minutes or to desired consistency. Season to taste.
