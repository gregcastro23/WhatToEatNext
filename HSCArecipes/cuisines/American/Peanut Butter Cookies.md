# PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES

**Yield:** approximately 20 cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 3/4 cup well-emulsified natural peanut butter
- 1/3 cup canola oil
- 3/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Line 2 half-sheet trays with parchment.
2. In medium bowl, sift flour, baking powder, and salt together.
3. In stand mixer, blend peanut butter, oil, maple syrup, and vanilla.
4. Add flour mixture to peanut butter mixture in stand mixer.
5. Using 1-ounce scoop, form cookies onto parchment-lined baking sheets and press cookies with lightly floured fork.
6. Bake 10-15 minutes or until bottoms are golden brown. Transfer cookies from pans to racks to cool.
