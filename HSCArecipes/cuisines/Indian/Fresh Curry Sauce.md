# Fresh Curry Sauce

**Yield:** 2 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 (13.66 ounce) cans coconut milk (about 3 1/2 cups)
- 2 teaspoons whole cumin seed, lightly toasted
- 1 teaspoon whole coriander seeds, lightly toasted
- 2 cloves
- 2 cardamom pods
- 4 ounces shallots (2 medium), small dice
- 1 small jalapeno, seeded, roughly chopped
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 2 tablespoons ginger juice (1-ounce piece)
- 2 tablespoons lime juice (1 lime)
- Additional salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In 2 1/2 quart pot, combine coconut milk with cumin seeds, coriander seeds, cloves, cardamom pods, shallots, jalapeno, turmeric, and salt. Simmer on low flame 15-20 minutes or until sauce is thickened to nappé consistency. Do not allow sauce to boil at any time.
2. Strain vegetables and spices from sauce, and return sauce to pot.
3. Add ginger and lime juices. Season to taste.
