# CURRY STOCK

**Yield:** approximately 3 quarts

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons coconut oil OR ghee
- 2 pounds carrots, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 pound zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1/2 pound celery, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 1/2 pounds onions, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1/2 ounce parsley, chopped
- 1 head garlic, smashed and peeled
- 5 cinnamon sticks, smashed
- 10 whole cloves
- 4 tablespoons coriander seeds, toasted
- 2 tablespoons cumin seeds, toasted
- 2 tablespoons cardamom pods, toasted
- 1 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. In 1-gallon stockpot, heat oil OR ghee. Add vegetables, herbs, spices, and salt.
2. Sweat for 15-20 minutes. Add 4 quarts filtered water.
3. Bring to boil, covered, lower heat and simmer 30 minutes, uncovered. Strain stock through chinois.
