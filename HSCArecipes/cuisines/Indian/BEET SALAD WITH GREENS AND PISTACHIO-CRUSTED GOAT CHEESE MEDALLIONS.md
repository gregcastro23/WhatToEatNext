# BEET SALAD WITH GREENS AND PISTACHIO-CRUSTED GOAT CHEESE MEDALLIONS

*(FALL/WINTER)*

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds beets, trimmed
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 cup orange juice (1 orange)
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- Pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 4 ounces semi-soft goat cheese, cut into approximately 1/4-inch wide rounds
- 1/2 cup pistachios, lightly toasted, ground
- 2 bunches watercress, stemmed
- 1 head red Belgian endive, leaves separated
- 1 orange, suprême slices

## Instructions

1. In medium-size pressure cooker, combine beets with salt and water to cover by 2 inches. Bring to full pressure, lower heat, and cook beets until just tender when pierced with a sharp knife (large beets – 30 minutes; medium beets – 20 minutes; small beets – 15 minutes).
2. Drain, cool, and peel beets. Cut into thin 1/8-inch rounds with a mandolin. Set aside.
3. Puree all dressing ingredients in blender until creamy. Pour half of the dressing over the cooked beets and toss.
4. Roll edges of cheese rounds in pistachios. Transfer rounds to refrigerator until service.
5. Toss watercress with remaining dressing.
6. Arrange plate with endive, watercress, beets, orange supreme, and cheese rounds.
