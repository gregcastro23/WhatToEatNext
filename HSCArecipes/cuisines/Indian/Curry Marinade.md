# Curry Marinade

**Yield:** approximately 3/4 cup

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon plus 1 1/2 teaspoons coriander seeds
- 3/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 3/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon sweet paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/8 teaspoon anise OR fennel seeds
- 1/8 teaspoon peppercorns
- 1/8 teaspoons cardamom seeds
- 1/8 teaspoon celery seeds
- Pinch nutmeg
- Pinch cayenne pepper
- 2 cloves
- 3 fenugreek seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In 8-inch sauté pan, heat oil and fry garlic and spices until fragrant (30 to 60 seconds), taking care not to burn them.
2. Cool for approximately 10 minutes. Puree mixture in blender.
