# GRILLED PINEAPPLE CHUTNEY

**Yield:** 4 Servings (2 - 2 1/2 tablespoons per serving)

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon canola OR coconut oil
- 1/2 red onion, sauté sliced
- 1/2 red pepper
- 1/4 jalapeño, seeded and minced
- 1/2 pineapple
- 1/4 teaspoon salt or to taste
- 1 teaspoon lime juice
- 1 tablespoon cilantro, minced

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch sauté pan, heat oil and sweat onion until translucent.
2. While onion is cooking, roast red pepper over open flame. When skin is blackened, place in paper bag for 10 minutes. Remove from bag, let cool and remove blackened skin. Cut pepper into small dice and add to pan with red onion.
3. Add jalapeño to onion and pepper mixture. Cook 5 minutes more and set pan aside.
4. Peel and cut pineapple into 4 wedges of equal size. Grill slices and set aside. When slices are cool, cut into medium-small dice and add to onion and pepper mixture.
5. Season with salt and lime juice and garnish with minced cilantro.
