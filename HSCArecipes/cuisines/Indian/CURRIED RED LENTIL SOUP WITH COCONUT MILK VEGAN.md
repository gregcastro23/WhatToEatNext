# CURRIED RED LENTIL SOUP WITH COCONUT MILK (VEGAN)

**Yield:** 7 cups

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 12 ounces onion, small dice
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 3 tablespoons minced garlic (3 large cloves)
- 8 ounces carrots, quarter moons (1/2-inch thick)
- 1 tablespoon curry powder
- 1 cup red lentils
- 5 cups vegetable stock
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 ounces (1/4 small head) cauliflower, cut into bit-sized florets
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 3 tablespoons ginger juice (5 ounces fresh ginger)
- 2 tablespoons lime juice (1 lime)
- Sea salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (1 ounce)
- 2 tablespoons shredded coconut, toasted

## Instructions

1. Heat coconut oil in 3-quart pot over medium-low flame. Add onion, salt, garlic, carrot, and curry powder. Sweat vegetables covered, stirring occasionally, until they begin to soften, about 10 minutes.
2. Add lentils, stock, and bay leaf. Cover and bring to boil over high heat. Reduce heat to simmer, and cook partially covered until lentils are broken down, approximately 30 minutes. Remove bay leaf.
3. Add cauliflower florets, and simmer until tender. Remove 2 cups of soup and purée in blender until creamy. Add blended ingredients back to pot.
4. Add coconut milk, ginger juice, and lime juice.
5. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Garnish with cilantro and shredded coconut.
